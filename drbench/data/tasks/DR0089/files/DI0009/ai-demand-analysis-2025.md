## Market Trends in AI Automotive Technologies

In 2024, the global market for AI in automotive industry was estimated to be $2.5 billion, showing a 13% growth from 2023. The AI applications were primarily focused on autonomous driving and predictive maintenance. Companies like Elexion Automotive also started investing significantly in AI-driven vehicle connectivity solutions.

## Analysis of AI Driver Assistance Demand in 2025

While the overall AI driver assistance market grew by 15% in 2025, the demand for AI-enhanced safety features like collision prevention systems and lane departure warnings saw significant growth. However, the adoption rate of AI-powered predictive maintenance systems remained relatively stagnant throughout the year.
