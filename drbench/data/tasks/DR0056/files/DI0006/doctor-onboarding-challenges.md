# Onboarding Challenges with New Medical Professionals

## Introduction

As we continue to grow and innovate, we've onboarded a substantial number of new medical professionals to MediConn Solutions. This report aims to elucidate the major challenges faced during this process from January 2016 to June 2023. Our focus is to understand these hurdles better to refine our onboarding process and improve the experience for both our medical professionals and our patients.

## Review of Onboarding Process and Compliance

Our onboarding process at MediConn Solutions has been refined over the years to ensure compliance with all necessary regulations. Starting with a comprehensive interview process, we further conduct a thorough background check to ensure our prospective doctors meet the ethical standards necessary in our field. Our compliance team, effective since 2014, has maintained a record of zero breaches, upholding our commitment to secure and compliant healthcare.

## Evaluation of Technological Infrastructure

MediConn Solutions has a robust technological infrastructure that supports our digital healthcare services. Built on a secure, cloud-based platform, our system facilitates seamless patient-doctor interactions. As of December 2020, our platform had handled over 500,000 virtual consultations with a 99.8% uptime, demonstrating our commitment to reliable digital healthcare.

## Insights into Medical Professional Onboarding Challenges

The onboarding of medical professionals at MediConn Solutions is a meticulous process, involving orientation, system training, and an introduction to our patient-first approach. By 2018, we had successfully onboarded over 200 healthcare professionals who are instrumental in our mission to provide accessible digital healthcare across Canada.

## Conclusion

By understanding the challenges encountered during onboarding, we can refine our process and ensure smoother transitions. This will allow us to increase our medical professional base and improve our service quality. Based on our findings, we will implement necessary adjustments and continuously monitor their effect. The ultimate goal is to ensure efficient onboarding and high satisfaction among our medical professionals, leading to better healthcare delivery.
