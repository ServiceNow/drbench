# ITSM Security and Compliance Strategy 2025

## Introduction

As MediConn Solutions continues to evolve as a leading provider of virtual healthcare services, our commitment to security and regulatory compliance remains paramount. This document outlines our strategic roadmap for managing security protocols, ensuring regulatory compliance, and implementing preventive measures against network and information system security risks by Q3 2025. It is designed to guide our IT Service Management (ITSM) team in safeguarding our electronic medical data, which is both sensitive and critical to our operations.

## Regulatory Compliance Assurance Measures

In 2023, our ITSM team streamlined the incident and request workflows to comply with new IT operations regulations. We introduced a new ticketing system that improved workflow efficiency by 15%. These changes helped us to better manage the workload and improve our response times.

## ITSM Security Protocol Enhancement

Through a comprehensive review of our IT Service Management (ITSM) system post the network update in Q1 2024, we discovered a significant reduction in manual hours expended by our team. The adoption of ITSM automation led to an efficient reallocation of approximately 1,400 hours of our staff's time. This considerable saving enabled our personnel to concentrate on critical areas of patient care and follow-up services, thus enhancing our overall service delivery. The automation of routine tasks has strengthened our security protocols, supporting MediConn Solutions' commitment to ensuring the integrity and security of electronic medical data.

## Proactive Network and Information System Security

In the second quarter of 2024, we expanded our IT team by hiring five additional network engineers. They were tasked with maintaining the integrity of our information systems and ensuring smooth operation. As a result, we reduced our system downtime by 10%.

## Conclusion

With the outlined strategy, MediConn Solutions will continue to prioritize data security and regulatory compliance. The proposed measures will minimize security risks, ensuring the integrity of our digital healthcare services and maintaining trust with our customers. As the IT Service Desk Manager, I will coordinate with the ITSM team to implement these actions. We will review our progress regularly and adjust our strategy as necessary to meet our Q3 2025 goal.
