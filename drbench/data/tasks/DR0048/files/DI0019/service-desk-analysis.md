# IT Service Desk Performance Analysis

## Introduction

This document presents an analysis of our IT service desk's performance for November 2024. It aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of the issues encountered, focusing on the number of customer complaints. This data is crucial for our continuous efforts to improve our virtual healthcare services and ensure the smooth running of our operations.

## Analysis of Complaint Nature and Frequency

In a review of the Q3 2024 data, we found that a majority of complaints were related to integration issues with third-party devices. Our software showed compatibility issues with certain brands, which led to an increased frequency of calls to our IT service desk. Interestingly, these complaints were more frequent during weekends and late evening hours.

## Impact of Complaints on Service Delivery

The surge in complaints in Q3 2024 had a tangible impact on our service delivery timelines. Our average response time increased by 15% during this period. Despite this, our team managed to resolve 85% of the complaints within the first 24 hours.

## Proposed Measures for Complaint Resolution

In response to these issues, we've initiated a comprehensive software update scheduled for release in late December 2024. We're also planning to expand our IT service desk team by 20% in Q1 2025 to improve our response and resolution times.

## Conclusion

The analysis reveals a number of complaint trends that require immediate attention. The frequency and nature of these complaints impact our service delivery and customer satisfaction. Moving forward, we propose specific measures for effective complaint resolution. Addressing these issues will significantly enhance our service quality and customer experience.
