# Patient Demographic Analysis Report

## Introduction

As MediConn Solutions continues to expand its patient base, understanding the demographic profiles of our patients, including the age range, is vital. This report provides detailed insights into our patient demographic profiles, offering a comprehensive understanding of our patient population's age distribution, geographical location, and the utilization of our services. This will allow us to tailor our services more effectively and identify growth opportunities.

## Geographical Distribution of Patient Base

MediConn Solutions has successfully extended its services to all provinces in Canada, with a user base percentage of 20% in Ontario, 15% in Quebec, 10% in British Columbia, and the remaining spread across other provinces. We've seen a steady increase in our user base in remote northern areas, thanks to our robust digital health infrastructure. As of the end of the fiscal year 2025, we have seen a 15% increase in new users from these regions.

## Analysis of Patient Age Profiles

A detailed analysis of patient age profiles was conducted by our Business Intelligence department. It was observed that the average time spent on our platform differs significantly across age groups, suggesting different usage patterns. For instance, patients above the age of 60 tend to utilize the platform more during early morning hours, while younger patients use it more in the evening.

## Service Utilization by Patient Age Groups

Our data shows a notable trend in the types of services utilized by different age groups. Younger patients often seek mental health services, while older patients typically require more chronic disease management. This trend has been consistent over the last two fiscal years, influencing our service development and marketing strategies.

## Conclusion

Understanding the age profile of our patient base is crucial for strategic decision-making. This report has highlighted the predominant age groups, their geographical location, and service utilization patterns. This information can guide our future marketing strategies and assist in the development of customized services. Continued monitoring and analysis of our patient demographics will ensure we remain responsive and adaptive to our patients' needs.
