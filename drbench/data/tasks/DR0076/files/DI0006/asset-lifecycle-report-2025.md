# Asset Lifecycle Management Report Q2-Q3 2025

## Introduction

This report provides an overview of Elexion Automotive's asset lifecycle management for the period between April 1 and September 30, 2025. It aims to provide insights into the number and nature of assets logged into our lifecycle management platform. These insights will help in understanding the company's growth and investments in physical and digital assets during the given period.

## Overview of New Digital Asset Deployments

In the third quarter of 2025, Elexion Automotive rolled out a significant update to its in-vehicle infotainment software across its entire EV range. This digital asset enhancement touched roughly 1,500 vehicles in circulation, marking a 25% increase from the previous quarter's deployments. The digital asset team, in collaboration with the product development department, worked tirelessly to ensure a seamless rollout.

## Insight into Physical Assets Addition

Elexion Automotive added a new production line at its main manufacturing facility in July 2025. This addition increased the company's manufacturing capacity by 15%, enhancing its ability to meet the growing demand for electric vehicles. Additionally, the company undertook a significant inventory reorganization, optimizing the storage and management of key physical assets.

## Lifecycle Management Platform Utilization Metrics

User engagement with Elexion Automotive's lifecycle management platform saw a 10% increase year-on-year as of September 2025. The platform's latest features, rolled out in June, have been well-received, particularly the predictive maintenance module. Additionally, the platform's user interface was simplified, leading to a reduction in training time for new users.

## Conclusion

The report summarizes our asset logging activities in the Asset Lifecycle Management Platform during Q2-Q3 2025. It highlights our company's growth in terms of asset accumulation and usage of our lifecycle management platform. The data discussed provides an understanding of the balance between our physical and digital assets. Future asset management strategies should consider these insights for efficient resource allocation and planning.
