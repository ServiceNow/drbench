In fact, in 2024, we implemented a new strategy that increased the efficiency of our maintenance procedures by 25%. This enhancement involved the integration of AI systems into our procedures, which enabled predictive analysis and facilitated timely repairs, thereby reducing the potential for vehicle breakdowns.

Our analysis from Q1-Q3 2025 maintenance schedule updates revealed a 15% decrease in major component failures across our fleet. This improvement is largely attributed to our enhanced maintenance procedures and the efficiency of our lifecycle team in managing maintenance schedules. However, we anticipate slight variations in these trends as we continue to expand our fleet.
