# Seasonal Merchandising Review: Q1 2025

## Introduction

This document provides a comprehensive overview of our Q1 2025 merchandising strategy, with a specific focus on seasonal items. It aims to assess the effectiveness of our seasonal merchandising strategies and to provide insight into the availability of seasonal decorations at Lee's Market during this period. The report's purpose is to analyze the impact of these strategies on sales, customer satisfaction, and store operations.

## Q1 2025 Merchandising Strategy Overview

Our Q1 2025 merchandising strategy entailed a comprehensive product line expansion, focusing on exotic fruit imports. Our collaboration with global suppliers allowed us to increase our fruit selection by 25% compared to Q4 2024, solidifying our commitment to providing diverse food options. Our supermarket layout was strategically redesigned to accommodate these new products, promoting an engaging shopping experience.

## Seasonal Decorations Availability Analysis

Our analysis revealed a remarkable increase in the availability of Halloween-themed decorations across the retail industry in 2024. We also noticed an emerging trend of retailers focusing on eco-friendly decorations, reflecting consumer demand for sustainable products. The analysis, however, did not include an examination of our own store's decoration offerings.

## Impacts on Sales and Customer Satisfaction

Our customer satisfaction survey in Q1 2025 showed a 15% increase compared to the same period in 2024. In particular, customers appreciated our diverse product selection and convenient delivery options. Meanwhile, sales of organic products saw a significant 30% rise, contributing to our overall revenue growth.

## Conclusion

Our Q1 2025 merchandising strategy, particularly regarding seasonal decorations, played a key role in driving sales and enhancing customer satisfaction. Continued analysis and refinement of these strategies will ensure we remain responsive to our customers' evolving needs. The insights gained from this review will inform our future merchandising strategies to further our commitment to delivering high-quality Asian groceries and household items. Recommendations for adjustments to our seasonal merchandising strategies are forthcoming.
