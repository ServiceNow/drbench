# Q3 2024 Dealership Cost Analysis Report

## Introduction

This report provides a comprehensive review of Elexion Automotive's dealership operations for Q3 2024. The goal is to analyze the various cost factors influencing our dealership operations, with a specific focus on the average cost per customer. We believe this data will provide valuable insights for strategic planning and resource allocation to optimize our operations.

## Dealership Operational Cost Breakdown

In 2023, the operational expenses for our dealerships increased by 4.5%. This rise was primarily due to higher costs of utilities and maintenance, along with a 3% increase in labor wages. Additionally, our investments into dealership infrastructure and amenities introduced an extra 2% expenditure.

## Customer Acquisition and Retention Strategies

In Q2 2024, Elexion Automotive launched an aggressive marketing campaign, resulting in a 12% increase in new customers. Our customer retention programs, such as the Elexion Loyalty Program, have also proven effective, with a 7% rise in repeat purchases during the same period.

## Average Cost Per Customer Analysis

In Q1 2024, Elexion Automotive saw a notable shift in the vehicle preferences of our customers. Our premium vehicles accounted for 60% of total sales compared to 40% for our mid-range offerings. These shifts, while significant, were consistent with industry trends.

The data presented in this report provides a detailed look at our dealership's operational costs and the efficacy of our customer acquisition strategies. The average cost per customer, in particular, provides critical insight into our financial health and operational efficiency.
