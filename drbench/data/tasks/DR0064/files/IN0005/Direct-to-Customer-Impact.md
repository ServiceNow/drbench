# Direct-to-Customer Model Impact Analysis

## Introduction

Given Elexion Automotive's strategic direction towards a complete direct-to-customer sales model by 2026, it is pivotal to understand the potential effects on key sales performance indicators. This report aims to analyze the potential impact of this approach on our sales cycle duration, conversion rates, and average revenue per customer, particularly considering diverse franchise laws across the United States.

## Effect of Franchise Laws on Direct Sales Across States

Franchise laws vary greatly across different states, often posing a significant challenge for EV manufacturers. For instance, in 2020, 30 states had laws that prohibited or limited direct sales by manufacturers. This diversity in legislation necessitates a customized approach to market entry in each state.

## Potential Impact on Key Sales Performance Metrics

Shifting to a DTC sales model can significantly influence various sales performance metrics. For instance, our CRM data shows that our telemarketing team achieved a 20% increase in lead generation in Q4 2020. This demonstrates the potential of DTC strategies to enhance sales performance.

## Evaluation of Direct-to-Customer Sales Models in EV Industry

Our analysis of the 2024 sales cycle showed that Elexion Automotive dealerships' average response time to customer inquiries online was approximately three hours. However, it was also observed that a significant 21% of leads were left unanswered, presenting a potential area for improvement. The transition to a direct-to-customer model could potentially address this gap, ensuring that customer inquiries are promptly addressed, thereby reducing the sales cycle duration and possibly improving conversion rates.

## Conclusion

The transition to a complete direct-to-customer sales model may significantly reshape Elexion Automotive's sales landscape. Considering the potential benefits and challenges, it is essential to tailor our strategies to each state's franchise laws. This transition could influence our sales cycle, conversion rates, and average revenue per customer. Therefore, we need to develop a comprehensive plan to manage this transition effectively.
