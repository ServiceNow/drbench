# Remote Workforce Analysis: Sales Department

## Introduction

In the rapidly evolving automotive industry, Elexion Automotive continually reassesses its operational strategies to remain competitive and efficient. One area of increased focus is the rise of remote work within our teams. This report specifically delves into the sales team's distributed workforce statistics, exploring the percentage of team members working remotely as of September 2025, and the impact on our operations.

## Sales Team Structure: Geographic Distribution

Elexion Automotive's sales team is geographically dispersed, with a significant presence in key markets across North America. As of December 2025, 40% of our sales team is located in the Northeast, 30% in the Midwest, and the remaining 30% across the South and West. This distribution strategy allows us to effectively cater to regional market needs and trends.

## Remote Working Trends within the Sales Department

The shift towards remote work within Elexion's sales department started in early 2020 during the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic. By the end of 2020, 15% of our sales team transitioned to remote work. This trend continued over the next few years, fostering a culture of flexibility and resilience.

## Impact of Remote Work on Sales Performance and Efficiency

While adapting to remote work, Elexion Automotive has observed significant improvements in the sales department's performance and efficiency. In 2021, our sales team managed to increase the number of customer touchpoints by 25%, and the sales cycle time reduced by 15%. This indicates that remote work does not necessarily compromise efficiency and productivity.

## Conclusion

Understanding the dynamics of our remote workforce is crucial to aligning our operations and strategic planning. This report provides insights into the current state of remote work within our sales team. Moving forward, we need to consider these trends when formulating our resourcing strategies, ensuring that we are equipped to manage a hybrid or remote workforce effectively.
