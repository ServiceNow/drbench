# Sales Analytic Dashboard Utilization Report

## Introduction

As we continue to monitor and improve our sales processes, it is essential to understand the usage and impact of our sales analytic dashboard. This report presents an in-depth analysis of the dashboard usage among our sales managers in 2025. It aims to identify the extent of utilization and the corresponding influence on our sales strategies. The data in this report will inform our future decisions on enhancements and training needs for the dashboard.

## Sales Manager Engagement with Analytic Tools

Sales managers at Elexion Automotive have consistently shown a high level of engagement with various analytic tools. In 2024, over 70% of sales managers actively used the CRM system, a 10% increase from the previous year. Their engagement with the customer response analysis tool also increased by 15% in the same period.

## Impact of Dashboard Usage on Sales Performance

The sales dashboard has been a critical tool for assessing and optimizing sales performance at Elexion Automotive. In 2023, the teams that frequently used the dashboard registered a 20% increase in sales efficiency. The usage of the dashboard also correlated with a 15% improvement in sales forecast accuracy.

## Analytic Dashboard Utilization Trends in 2025

In 2025, there was a significant increase in the adoption of the analytic dashboard among the after-sales service department and the marketing team. The marketing team's usage increased by 25% and the after-sales service department's usage by 20% from the previous year. This growth in adoption is a testament to the dashboard’s value across different departments.

## Conclusion

The analysis reveals crucial insights into the usage of the sales analytic dashboard and its influence on our sales outcomes. It is clear that the dashboard is a valuable tool for our sales managers, and understanding its utilization can guide our future enhancements. We should continue to monitor these trends and consider implementing targeted training to increase dashboard engagement where necessary. Let's leverage these insights to drive our commitment to sustainable growth and customer satisfaction.
