# Return Analysis of New Year Decorations 2025

## Introduction

This report provides an in-depth analysis of the return rates for New Year decorations introduced in 2025. It aims to identify any trends or issues that could impact our business and inform future product selection and strategic marketing decisions. As a growing leader in the Asian grocery market, understanding customer preferences and potential product performance is crucial for Lee's Market.

## 2025 New Year Decorations Product Portfolio

In 2025, Lee's Market launched a diverse array of New Year decorations, offering over 200 distinct products. These included traditional Asian lanterns, banners, and tableware, along with modern adaptations like LED dragon figures. The portfolio was curated by our Product Development team in collaboration with key suppliers, aimed at catering to a broad range of customer tastes.

## Customer Feedback on New Year Decorations

Customer feedback on our 2025 New Year decorations has been largely positive. Post-purchase surveys indicated that over 85% of customers were satisfied with the quality and variety of our products. Most customers appreciated the blend of traditional and contemporary designs, reflecting our commitment to honoring culture while embracing innovation.

## Return Rate Analysis for 2025 New Year Decorations

Our return process for New Year decorations involves rigorous quality checks and customer feedback collection. This helps us identify any potential issues and improve future product offerings. The process is managed by our Customer Service department, following strict protocols to ensure customer satisfaction.

## Conclusion

The analysis of the return rates for our New Year decoration product line introduced in 2025 has provided valuable insights into our customers' preferences. The findings will help us fine-tune our future product offerings and marketing strategies. It is recommended to consider these insights while planning the product portfolio for the next New Year season. We should also focus on improving the product quality based on the customer feedback to reduce the return rates.
