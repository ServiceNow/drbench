# Impact of Expanded Customer Service on Sales

## Introduction

As part of our continuous growth strategy, Lee's Market expanded its customer service team by hiring nine new call agents in 2025. This document aims to analyze the resultant effect on our overall sales. We will compare key performance indicators before and after the expansion and evaluate the success of this initiative.

## New Customer Service Agents: Individual Performance Metrics

In 2025, our new hires in the customer service department demonstrated varying levels of individual performance. Agent A handled an average of 60 calls per day, while Agent B managed about 70. Additionally, Agent C recorded the fastest average call resolution time at 2.5 minutes.

## Sales Trend Analysis: Pre and Post Expansion

Before our expansion in 2024, our average monthly sales volume was 50,000 units. However, post expansion, we saw a significant increase, recording average monthly sales of 68,000 units in 2025. This trend indicates a successful expansion.

## Customer Satisfaction and Retention Rates: 2025 Overview

Overall customer satisfaction in 2025 remained at 89%, consistent with previous years. Our customer retention rate also held strong at 74%. These metrics underline our continued commitment to providing excellent customer service.

## Conclusion

The addition of new customer service agents has shown a significant impact on our overall sales in 2025. The individual performance metrics, sales trend analysis, and customer satisfaction rates have all been influenced positively. Moving forward, we should consider further expanding our customer service team while maintaining a focus on training and development to maximize sales growth.
