## Overview of Retail Space Utilization

Throughout 2025, Lee's Market effectively utilized its retail space to optimize customer flow and improve product visibility. ## Year-over-Year Vacancy Rate Changes

In 2024, Lee's Market experienced a 1.2% increase in the vacancy rate. This was primarily due to the closure of two stores in Vancouver and Burnaby. However, proactive management strategies helped to minimize the duration of vacancy periods, with new tenants secured for both locations within 6 months.

Per industry standards, Lee's Market maintained a competitive edge in 2025. The average transaction value was $50, which was above the industry average of $45.
