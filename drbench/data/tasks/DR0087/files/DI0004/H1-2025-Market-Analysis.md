# H1 2025 Retail Market Analysis

## Introduction

This report aims to shed light on the retail market conditions in H1 2025, focusing primarily on Lee's Market. ## Market Trends in Retail Gross Rent

Over the past decade, the retail industry has seen a steady increase in gross rents. This is primarily attributed to the surge in e-commerce, which has forced brick and mortar retailers to innovate and offer unique in-store experiences, thereby increasing operational costs. Despite the industry-wide rise, certain markets such as Toronto and Vancouver have experienced a more pronounced uptick due to their robust retail landscapes.

## Assessing Operational Impact of Rent Decline

A decline in rent can have various implications on retail operations. It can potentially reduce overhead costs, thereby improving profit margins. However, the decreased rent may be indicative of a less desirable retail location, which could adversely affect foot traffic and sales. It's important for retailers to maintain a balance between rent costs and location attractiveness.
