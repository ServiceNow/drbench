# Strategic Sales Enhancements for Digital Health

## Introduction

As we strive to strengthen MediConn Solutions' standing in the Canadian healthcare market, it is critical we devise data-driven strategies to bolster sales, reduce readmission rates, and increase customer lifetime retention. This document will explore potential tactics to achieve these objectives, leveraging insights from our CRM systems and sales data. Our focus will be on identifying opportunities for improvement and creating a comprehensive action plan for the sales department.

## Analysis of Current Customer Retention and Readmission Rates

In 2022, MediConn Solutions made significant strides in enhancing our customer support infrastructure by establishing a specialized team to attend to new patient inquiries and concerns. Alongside this, we implemented an automated ticketing system which has led to substantial improvements in issue resolution times. These strategic changes have resulted in a remarkable reduction in the average resolution time of tickets, now taking less than 48 hours to address. This has not only streamlined our customer service processes but also significantly improved our patient satisfaction rates.

## Exploring Opportunities in the Canadian Digital Health Market

The Canadian digital health market is expanding rapidly, with a compounded annual growth rate of 6.8% forecasted through 2024. This growth is largely driven by an increase in the adoption of telemedicine, electronic health records, and other digital health solutions by healthcare providers across Canada. It's important to understand the competitive landscape as we plan our growth strategy.

## Data-Driven Sales Strategies for Enhanced Customer Engagement

In 2021, we implemented a data-driven sales approach that tracks customer touchpoints across multiple channels. This allows our sales team to understand the customer journey better and provide targeted interactions. Our CRM system also helps us monitor and manage these interactions effectively.
