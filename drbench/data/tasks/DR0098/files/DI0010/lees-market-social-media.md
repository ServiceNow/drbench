# Lee's Market Social Media Analysis 2025

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing efforts to enhance Lee's Market's digital presence, we have conducted an analysis of our social media followings across various platforms as of June 2025. This report presents a detailed breakdown of our audience distribution, engagement trends, and offers insights into areas of potential growth. It aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of our current digital sphere and inform strategic decisions moving forward.

## Platform-wise Audience Distribution

As of April 2025, the majority of our customer interactions through social media were seen on Facebook, followed by Instagram and then Twitter. We attribute this to the particular demographic of our customer base, which tends to skew older. Pinterest and TikTok, while still important, represented a smaller portion of our social media interactions.

## Analysis of Audience Engagement

Our social media strategy has been highly effective at increasing audience engagement. In the first quarter of 2025, we saw a 15% increase in likes and comments on our Facebook posts, and a 10% increase on Instagram. Our Twitter engagement, however, remained steady with minimal growth.

## Growth Opportunities in Social Media

Looking ahead, we see tremendous potential for growth on emerging social media platforms. Our marketing team is exploring campaigns targeted at younger demographics on platforms like Snapchat and TikTok. We believe this will complement our existing strong presence on traditional platforms like Facebook and Instagram.

## Conclusion

Our social media presence forms a crucial part of our marketing strategy, reaching a diverse audience and fostering customer engagement. The data presented in this report provides a snapshot of our current digital footprint and offers a pathway for potential expansion. Going forward, this information should guide our efforts to optimise our social media strategy and maximise customer engagement. The marketing team is tasked with developing a plan to leverage these insights for further growth.
