# Impact of US Tariffs on Sales Forecast

## Introduction

As the Regional Sales Manager, I have conducted an in-depth analysis of the sales trends in the context of US tariffs imposed in 2025. This report aims to provide insights into product-level sales across different categories, which are exposed and insulated from the tariffs. The objective is to utilize these insights to update our sales forecasts for Lee's Market up to 2027.

## Analysis of Product-level Sales Trends

As we navigate through the implications of the US tariffs, it's critical to understand their impact on our sales growth. Our analysis indicates that Lee's Market is likely to experience a negative deviation of approximately 2.5% per quarter from our original sales growth projections for the period 2025-2027 across all major product categories. This figure indicates a significant short-term volatility and reflects the sensitivity of our product categories to these tariffs. The insight derived from this data will be instrumental in refining our quarterly sales targets by category and region, as well as enhancing the accuracy of our demand planning models through 2027.

## Impact of Tariffs on Sales Performance

In 2018, when steel tariffs were imposed by the US, there was no significant impact on our sales. Our customers continued to purchase canned goods and other products with steel components. The sales of these products remained steady, with only a slight decrease in Q2 of 2018.

## Projected Sales Forecast Adjustment

In preparation for the holiday season, we are expecting a 15% increase in overall sales. We anticipate a surge in the sales of gift items, holiday decorations, and baking supplies. Our procurement team has already started stocking up on these items to meet the expected demand.

## Conclusion

The impact of US tariffs has significantly influenced our product-level sales trends, necessitating a recalibration of our sales forecasts. By understanding the differential effects on tariff-exposed and tariff-insulated categories, we can create a more accurate projection for 2027. This information will be invaluable in forming our strategic decisions moving forward. Our next steps should involve closely monitoring these trends and adjusting our sales strategies accordingly.
