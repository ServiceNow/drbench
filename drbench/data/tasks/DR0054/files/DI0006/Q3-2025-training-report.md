# Q3 2025 Training Completion Report

## Introduction

In line with our continuing commitment to quality care and professional development, this report provides an overview of the completion rates for our online training modules amongst multidisciplinary care team members. In particular, the focus is on the targeted modules for fistula management during Q3 2025. The data will inform our strategies for ongoing training programs and help identify areas for improvement.

## Overview of Online Training Program

Launched in January 2023, our online training programs have been designed to enhance the skills of our healthcare professionals. These programs cover various topics, including telemedicine ethics, patient privacy, and digital health technology usage. So far, over 200 employees have participated in at least one program, with an average completion rate of 80%.

## Fistula Management Training Completion Rates

The module on advanced cardiac life support, launched in May 2025, saw a completion rate of 87%. In comparison, the training on diabetes management, rolled out in April 2025, had a lower completion rate of 74%. Our training on wound care management, introduced in June 2025, was completed by 79% of the targeted team.

## Implications for Future Training Strategies

The feedback collected from the participants of our online training programs has highlighted the need for more interactive content. We are planning to incorporate more multimedia materials in our future modules. Additionally, we aim to incorporate real-time discussions and quizzes to increase engagement.

## Conclusion

The training completion rates outlined in this report highlight areas of success and potential improvement. Our multidisciplinary care team members have demonstrated a commitment to professional development, particularly in relation to fistula management. This data will be instrumental in shaping future training strategies and ensuring the continued delivery of high-quality virtual healthcare services. We will continue to monitor and report on training completion rates to drive improvements and align with our commitment to professional excellence.
