# Coupon Redemption Analysis & Marketing Strategy

## Introduction

This document presents an analysis of coupon redemption trends within Lee's Market and the subsequent marketing strategy developed in response. It further explores the comparative efficacy of online and paper flyer coupons, as reported by Sofia Patel in July 2025. The analysis will help us better understand our customers' preferences and adjust our marketing efforts accordingly.

## Evaluation of Sofia Patel's Coupon Redemption Report

Sofia Patel's insightful report was presented to the marketing department in July 2025. The report was an outcome of six months of rigorous data collection and analysis, capturing over one million transactions. Sofia's analytical skills and attention to detail were praised by the department's senior management.

## Online vs. Paper: Comparative Coupon Redemption Rates

In the retail industry, the shift towards digital platforms has brought an interesting dynamic to coupon redemption. Prior studies indicated that in 2024, 60% of all coupons redeemed came from online sources. This shift has been attributed to the increased accessibility and convenience of online shopping.

## Lee's Market: Marketing Strategy Post-Analysis

Post-analysis, Lee's Market implemented a quarterly review cycle for marketing strategies, starting from Q4 2025. The introduction of this process aimed to regularly assess and adjust the marketing strategies based on changing customer behavior and market trends.

## Conclusion

The insights derived from Sofia Patel's report and the comparative analysis of coupon redemption methods have informed our new marketing strategy. Our focus will be on leveraging the efficiency of online coupons while not neglecting the still significant paper flyer segment. The proposed marketing strategy seeks to optimize coupon deployment in line with customer preferences. We look forward to assessing the impact of these changes on our customer engagement and sales in the future.
