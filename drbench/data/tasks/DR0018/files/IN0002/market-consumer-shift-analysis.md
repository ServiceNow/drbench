# Competitive Landscape and Consumer Shift Analysis

## Introduction

This report aims to provide an in-depth analysis of the current competitive landscape and the shifting consumer behavior in the retail industry. It focuses on the key data points that we need to track and analyze over the next 12 months to ensure Lee's Market's strategic alignment with market trends. As market dynamics continue to evolve, understanding these shifts will enable us to make informed business decisions catering to our diverse urban customer base.

## Evolving Retail Competitive Landscape

In response to the shifting landscape of the retail sector, our Market Research Analyst, Sofia Patel, initiated an aggressive competitor benchmarking strategy throughout 2025. This involved executing monthly audits of price points for our top ten basket items, with the goal of maintaining a minimum 15% differential in advertised discounts between Lee's Market and mass retailers within our key urban markets. Additionally, to gauge the growing consumer interest in health and wellness, quarterly customer satisfaction surveys were deployed. The target was a minimum increase of ten points in the Net Promoter Score (NPS) each quarter with respect to satisfaction derived from our health product offerings.

## Emerging Consumer Buying Behaviour Trends

Since 2024, there's been a slight increase in customer preference for in-store shopping, with 35% of the surveyed customers favoring physical stores over online shopping. This is attributed to the unique in-store experiences and personal interactions that physical stores offer.

## Value-seeking and Health & Wellness Trends

In the past two years, there has been an increase in the sales of organic products by 18%. Moreover, our loyalty program saw a 10% increase in sign-ups, indicating customers are seeking value for their purchases. However, the overall health and wellness sector growth was modest at 3%.

## Conclusion

Understanding the competitive landscape and shifting consumer behaviors is pivotal to our strategic decision-making over the next year. The insights gathered will enable us to stay ahead in a competitive retail market and cater to our customers' evolving needs. The next steps involve translating these insights into actionable strategies, focusing on value-seeking and health & wellness trends. As a team, we need to continually monitor these trends and adjust our strategies accordingly.
