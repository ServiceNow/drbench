# Knowledge Hub Usability Improvement Meetings 2024

## Introduction

This report encapsulates the collaborative efforts made by the Knowledge Management (KM), Human Resources (HR), and IT teams towards enhancing the usability and data quality of our knowledge hub in 2024. The purpose of this document is to provide a comprehensive overview of the meetings, their objectives, and outcomes. It aims to chart the progress made, identify areas of improvement, and outline future plans to advance our knowledge hub to better support Lee's Market's growing operations.

## Overview of Collaborative Meetings in 2024

In 2024, our team had a busy year with various collaborative meetings. We had 120 meetings with the Supply Chain department to discuss inventory management and logistics. 90 meetings were held with our Marketing team to strategize on increasing customer engagement and reach in our digital platforms.

## Key Outcomes and Enhancements to the Knowledge Hub

The Knowledge Hub has seen a 25% increase in user engagement due to new features added in 2024. We implemented a predictive search feature making it easier for users to find what they need. Additionally, we launched a community forum within the hub to foster information sharing and collaboration.

## Future Plans for Knowledge Hub Improvement

In the upcoming year, we plan to introduce a personalized dashboard feature to our Knowledge Hub. We aim to enhance data visualization capabilities and integrate real-time data analytics. Our goal is to increase user engagement by 15% and decrease manual data entry by 30%.

## Conclusion

The collaboration between the KM, HR, and IT teams in 2024 has significantly bolstered our knowledge hub's functionality and data quality. Despite the progress, there are still opportunities for further improvements. The outlined future plans in this report will guide our subsequent steps to ensure continuous refinement of our knowledge hub. This will ultimately result in better operational efficiency and decision making across Lee's Market.
