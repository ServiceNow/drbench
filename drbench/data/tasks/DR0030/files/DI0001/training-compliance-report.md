# Warehouse Operations Training Compliance Report

## Introduction

This report presents an overview of the training compliance levels within the warehouse operations of Lee's Market. The focus is on the completion rates of the updated warehouse Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) training course developed in June 2025. It is vital for ensuring the safety, efficiency, and consistency of our warehouse operations and to maintain our reputation for high-quality Asian groceries and household items.

## Summary of Updated Warehouse SOPs

The revised warehouse SOPs, rolled out in June 2025, introduced a new inventory management system. The update focused on reducing overstocking of seasonal items and capitalizing on real-time data for order fulfillment. The SOPs also detailed the implementation of automated inventory tracking to minimize human error and improve overall warehouse efficiency.

## Training Course Completion Rates

As of December 2025, our data shows a significant increase in the completion rates of our cashier training program. We've seen an upward trend, with 92% of our cashiers successfully completing their training, up from 86% in the previous year. This increase reflects our commitment to employee development.

## Impact of Training on Warehouse Efficiency

Since the introduction of our new Warehouse Management System (WMS) training in July 2024, our warehouse efficiency has improved by 15%. The training program focused on teaching staff how to effectively utilize the WMS to streamline operations. The result has been a reduction in order processing times and fewer errors.

## Conclusion

The completion rates of the updated SOPs training course signifies our commitment to upholding the highest standards in our warehouse operations. It is crucial to ensure continued adherence to these procedures for optimal efficiency and safety. The management should consider mandatory refresher courses for all warehouse staff to further enhance operational efficiency. A detailed plan for this will be developed and shared in the coming weeks.
