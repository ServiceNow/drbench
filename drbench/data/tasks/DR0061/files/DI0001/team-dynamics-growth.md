# Team Dynamics and Business Growth Support

## Introduction

This report provides an analysis of the changes in the team structure under Emily Lee, in line with our sustained business growth at Elexion Automotive. It evaluates how these changes have enhanced our capacity to support the expanding business. This documentation is essential to identify areas of strength and opportunities for further optimization in our team structure.

## Historical Perspective of Team Growth

In the initial years, Elexion Automotive had a lean team with just 10 members across all departments. It was in 2017, that the company began to expand its workforce, adding 30% more employees from various fields such as engineering, R&D, and supply chain management. By the year 2019, the overall team size tripled, and the company began to establish itself in the electric automotive sector.

## Impact of Team Expansion on Business Operations

Expansion of the team over the years has allowed Elexion Automotive to boost its production capabilities. The increased workforce in R&D has led to the development of innovative technologies, while the larger supply chain team has improved efficiency in operations. As a result, Elexion's production rate has witnessed a surge of 25% in the last three years.

## Alignment of Team Structure with Business Objectives

Elexion Automotive has always believed in aligning its team structure with the company's business objectives. For instance, in order to achieve its sustainability goal, the company has a dedicated sustainability team that works in tandem with the product design and engineering departments. This alignment has been instrumental in helping Elexion achieve its zero-emission target two years ahead of schedule.

## Conclusion

In summary, Emily Lee's team has evolved in size and structure to support Elexion Automotive's growth, positively impacting our operations. Moving forward, it is essential to maintain an adaptable team structure that aligns with our business objectives. Recommendations and strategic plans will be developed to further enhance our team's capacity to support our business growth.
