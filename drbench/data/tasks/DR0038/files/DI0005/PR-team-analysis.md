# PR Team Performance and Media Placement Analysis

## Introduction

This report provides an insight into the performance of the public relations team at Lee's Market, specifically focusing on the number of profiles placed. The analysis is aimed at understanding the effectiveness of the team's efforts and the impact on the company's public image and market position. We will dive into the type of profiles placed, the distribution channels used, and the geographical regions targeted.

## Analysis of Profile Types and Content

Our profiles have evolved over the years, reflecting the changing landscape of our industry. In 2018, we leaned heavily towards profiles focusing on our innovation in supply chain management. More recently, in 2021, we have seen an uptick in profiles highlighting our commitment to sustainable practices and community outreach.

## Distribution Channels and Media Outreach

We utilize a wide range of distribution channels for our profiles. During the second quarter of 2021, there was a 35% increase in our usage of digital media platforms for distribution. Our media outreach strategy also includes a robust mix of traditional print, television, and radio channels.

## Geographical Distribution of Placed Profiles

The geographical distribution of our placed profiles has been strategically spread across our key markets. In 2022, we increased our focus on the Midwest region by 40%, while maintaining a strong presence in the West Coast and Northeast markets. This aligns with our expansion strategy.

## Conclusion

The public relations team has been successful in placing a substantial number of profiles across various channels, contributing to Lee's Market's growing reputation. The data suggests that the team's efforts have been well balanced across different media outlets and regions. Moving forward, we should continue to monitor the effectiveness of our PR team's work and make strategic adjustments based on the insights gleaned from this analysis.
