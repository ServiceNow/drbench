# Community Partnerships ROI Analysis 2022-2023

## Introduction

This document provides an analysis of the most impactful community partnership programs for regional food retailers in North America with annual revenues between $500M and $600M during 2022-2023. We evaluate these partnerships based on their return on investment, considering media coverage value, customer acquisition costs, and brand sentiment improvements. The objective is to inform our own strategic planning for community engagement and partnership initiatives. This will ensure Lee's Market continues to enhance its brand reputation and customer base.

## Impact of Partnerships on Brand Sentiment

Our collaboration with local schools for Asian cuisine cooking sessions in 2021 was received positively by the local communities. A post-campaign survey indicated that 60% of participants had a more favorable view of Lee's Market. These partnerships helped us in connecting with a younger audience and promoting Asian culinary culture.

## Comparison of Community Partnership Costs

In 2022, our collaboration with the Urban Food Literacy Foundation through the Chef in the Community initiative yielded significant returns. Valued at $250,000, the media coverage we received positively promoted our brand. Additionally, we saw an 18% decrease in the cost of attracting new customers, demonstrating the program's efficiency. The program also significantly enhanced our brand image, with a 12-point surge in sentiment scores in the cities where the program was implemented.

## Media Coverage Value of Partnership Programs

In 2021, our partnership with the Food Bank Network generated significant media attention. It resulted in a 20% increase in our overall media coverage compared to the previous year. This was primarily due to a series of high-profile charity events we co-hosted.

## Conclusion

This analysis provides valuable insights into the return on investment from different community partnership programs across regional food retailers. It shows the potential for strategic community engagement to improve brand sentiment, attract new customers, and generate media coverage. We can leverage these findings to optimize our own community partnership strategies at Lee's Market and continue strengthening our brand reputation. Further, this will guide our communications and public relations efforts moving forward.
