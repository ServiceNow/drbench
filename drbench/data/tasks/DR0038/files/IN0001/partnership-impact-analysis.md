# Community Partnership Impact Analysis 2022-2023

## Introduction

As we continue to grow Lee's Market's presence in North America, it is essential to understand the efficacy of our community partnership programs. This report will analyze successful strategies by similar regional food retailers and their outcomes, focusing on return on investment (ROI) through media coverage value, customer acquisition costs, and brand perception improvement. The findings will provide insights for our upcoming PR initiatives and partnership strategies.

## Analysis of Customer Acquisition Costs

In Q3 2023, our online marketing campaigns saw a 15% decrease in customer acquisition costs due to a new partnership with a digital advertising agency. The corporate communications department worked diligently with the agency to ensure our brand values were effectively communicated in the campaign. Our average cost per acquisition fell to $35, compared to the industry average of $50.

## Comparison of Media Coverage Value Across Retailers

In 2022, the partnership between Lee's Market and the Urban Food Literacy Foundation led to the launch of the Chef in the Community program. This initiative attracted substantial positive attention, quantified at a value of about $250,000 media coverage. Moreover, the program succeeded in decreasing the cost to attract and acquire new customers by a noteworthy 18%. This strategic alliance also played a pivotal role in enhancing the brand's reputation, as evidenced by a 12-point lift in brand sentiment scores across the cities where the program was implemented.

## Brand Perception Improvement Through Community Partnerships

Based on the Q2 2022 brand survey, Lee's Market's brand perception improved by 10% year-over-year. The Corporate Communications Department credited this increase to a variety of factors, including effective crisis communication and a renewed focus on digital platforms. Interestingly, the age group 18-24 reported the most significant increase in positive brand perception.

## Conclusion

The community partnership initiatives of similar regional retailers provide valuable insights into effective strategies for enhancing media coverage, reducing customer acquisition costs, and improving brand perception. The given analysis should guide our future public relations and community engagement efforts. As we continue to expand Lee's Market, ensuring the strategic alignment of our initiatives with proven success factors is paramount. Detailed implementation plans based on these insights will be developed and shared with relevant departments.
