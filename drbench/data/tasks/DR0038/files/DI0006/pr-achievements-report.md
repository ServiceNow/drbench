# Public Relations Achievements Report

## Introduction

In an effort to communicate our brand's compelling story and to boost our market presence, our Public Relations team has been diligently securing speaking engagements. This document provides a detailed overview of their efforts, the impact on brand visibility, and recommendations for future engagements. It is crucial for all stakeholders to understand the progress and value these activities bring to Lee's Market.

## Breakdown of Speaking Engagements

During the last fiscal year, our marketing department conducted in-depth analysis of customer behavior both online and in-store. They observed that customer engagement spikes significantly during promotional campaigns, with a 15% increase in sales over regular periods. It was also noted that our Asian food products are the most purchased items during these promotions.

## Impact on Brand Visibility

Our social media presence has experienced a substantial growth in the past year, with a 20% increase in followers on platforms like Facebook and Instagram. Our digital marketing team's effort in creating engaging content and responding to customer queries promptly has proven beneficial. The most popular posts are those featuring our unique Asian groceries and household items.

## Recommendations for Future Engagements

Our customer service department has suggested that we expand our online services to accommodate more delivery slots. This would cater to the increasingly busy schedules of our customers. Additionally, they recommend offering more seasonal products online, as these have proven to be popular in-store.

## Conclusion

Our Public Relations team has made significant strides in securing speaking engagements, therefore elevating our brand's visibility. The data presented in this report provides a clear direction for future undertakings. To maximize our reach and influence, we should target more high-profile events in the future. A strong commitment to this strategy will maintain our momentum and continue to enhance our brand's reputation and awareness.
