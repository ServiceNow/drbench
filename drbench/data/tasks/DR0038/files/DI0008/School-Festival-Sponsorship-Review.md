# 2022 School Festival Sponsorship Evaluation

## Introduction

This document provides an in-depth analysis of our participation as sponsors for local school festivals in 2022. As part of our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) efforts, this initiative aimed to boost our brand recognition and foster stronger relationships within our local communities. The following sections will evaluate the impact of these sponsorships, as well as related brand engagement activities and community feedback.

## Impact on Brand Recognition and Engagement

In 2021, Lee's Market launched a comprehensive brand awareness campaign, which increased brand recognition by 15% and customer engagement by 20%. In-store promotions and online marketing activities were key drivers of this success. The marketing department is now analyzing the results to identify improvement areas for future campaigns.

## Community Feedback and Perception Analysis

In Q3 of 2021, Lee's Market conducted a community perception survey, with 67% of respondents indicating they view Lee's Market as a community leader. The survey also revealed that 72% of customers appreciated our in-store and online services. The feedback will be used to improve customer service initiatives.

## Future CSR Strategy and Recommendations

Looking ahead to 2023, Lee's Market plans to invest more in CSR activities by supporting local food banks and community gardens. The company is also considering partnerships with local non-profit organizations for various environmental initiatives. The budget allocation for these CSR activities is being finalized by senior management.

## Conclusion

Sponsoring local school festivals proved to be an effective strategy in enhancing our brand recognition and fortifying our community relations. The community's feedback was generally positive, reflecting well on our public image. As we move forward, leveraging these insights will be crucial in shaping our future CSR initiatives. We recommend further investing in similar community-oriented activities, while continuously evaluating our impact to ensure maximum ROI.
