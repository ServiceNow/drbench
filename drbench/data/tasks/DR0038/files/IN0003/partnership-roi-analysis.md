# Community Partnership ROI Analysis - 2022/2023

## Introduction

In our ongoing effort to amplify Lee's Market's brand presence and foster positive customer sentiment, we have analyzed various community partnership programs implemented by regional food retailers within our revenue bracket. This report presents a comparative analysis of these programs, focusing on return on investment during the period of 2022-2023. The assessment is based on media coverage value, customer acquisition costs, and improvements in brand perception.

## Impact of Partnerships on Customer Acquisition Costs

Our customer acquisition costs in Q2 2021 were reduced by 7% after the introduction of the 'Love Your Neighborhood' campaign, which involved partnerships with local community groups. This was a success, considering the increased competition in the retail market and the rising costs of digital advertising.

## Effectiveness of Partnerships in Shaping Brand Perception

Our brand perception survey conducted in Q4 2021 showed a positive response towards our 'Green Market' sustainability campaign. 80% of respondents reported that they viewed Lee's Market as a responsible and sustainable brand. This result showed an improvement from the previous year's survey.

## Comparative Analysis of Community Partnership Programs

In 2023, Lee's Market initiated the Little Grocers school program, a community effort dedicated to fostering healthier eating habits among children. The initiative was well-received, particularly among parents within the participating school districts. Surveys conducted post-implementation by our public relations team, led by Andrew Park, indicated a significant uptick in brand perception. Trust metrics and the perception of our community commitment increased by 15%, reinforcing the success of this program in strengthening our brand's relationship with our customer base.

## Conclusion

The analysis presented provides a comprehensive view of the effectiveness of various community partnership programs amongst our peers. The insights will guide our strategic decision-making regarding future community initiatives and partnerships. We should prioritize programs that not only provide a good return on investment, but also strengthen our brand identity and customer relationship. Further exploration and potential adaptation of these successful programs can enhance Lee's Market's public image and foster stronger connections within our community.
