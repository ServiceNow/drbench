# FY2025 Store Maintenance and Repair Expenditure Report

## Introduction

The purpose of this document is to provide a comprehensive analysis of Lee's Market's total expenditure on store maintenance and facility repairs in the fiscal year 2025. This report will assist in understanding the cost efficiency of our operations and help us make informed decisions regarding budget allocation in the future.

## Analysis of Store Maintenance Costs

In the fiscal year 2025, we introduced a new set of protocols for our store maintenance routine. The initiative included a shift towards more environmentally friendly cleaning products and a revised schedule that maximizes efficiency. This change has resulted in a substantial increase in employee productivity, with the maintenance department reporting a 12% improvement in task completion rates.

## Breakdown of Facility Repair Expenditures

Our facility repair department has always been proactive in maintaining our retail infrastructure. In FY2025, we implemented a new inventory management system that enhanced our ability to track our repair equipment and supplies. As a result, our repair department was able to reduce the frequency of stockouts by 15%.

## Comparison with Previous Years' Expenditures

Overall, our expenditure trends have demonstrated an emphasis on optimizing resource allocation in recent years. An analysis of our total expenditures from FY2020 to FY2025 shows a clear shift towards investing in technologies to boost operational efficiency. This strategic focus has led to a significant reduction in our operating costs over the years.

## Conclusion

The total expenditure on store maintenance and facility repairs for FY2025 provides valuable insights into our operational costs. It is crucial to assess these costs regularly to ensure effective budget management. Further, the comparison with previous years highlights the areas where we can improve cost-efficiency. The management should consider these findings in future budget planning and cost management strategies.
