# Q4 2025 - Lee's Market Logistics Analysis

## Introduction

This document provides an analysis of our logistics operations in Q4 of 2025, focusing on our refrigerated truck fleet. The aim is to review the operational status, efficiency, and sustainability of our refrigerated transportation. The insights gathered will help us make informed decisions on future logistics plans and investments.

## Logistics Fleet Overview: Q4 2025

In Q4 2025, Lee's Market invested in upgrading our fleet management system for better efficiency and tracking. The system now includes real-time tracking and improved route planning capabilities. This upgrade has led to a 15% reduction in fuel consumption across our entire fleet.

## Operational Efficiency of Refrigerated Trucks

Our refrigerated trucks are equipped with advanced temperature control systems, ensuring the freshness of our products during transportation. In Q4 2025, we implemented a new maintenance schedule which resulted in a 10% decrease in unexpected breakdowns. The average fuel efficiency of our refrigerated trucks was 8.5 km/litre.

## Sustainability Practices in Refrigerated Transportation

Lee's Market is committed to sustainability in our operations. In 2025, we initiated a programme to reduce carbon emissions from our refrigerated trucks. By retrofitting older models and training drivers in fuel-efficient driving techniques, we estimated a 20% reduction in our carbon footprint.

## Conclusion

Given the critical role of our refrigerated trucks in ensuring product freshness, understanding their operational status and efficiency is vital. Furthermore, looking at our sustainability practices in this area aligns with our company's commitment to environmentally friendly operations. This analysis allows us to identify areas for improvement and develop a more effective and sustainable logistics strategy.
