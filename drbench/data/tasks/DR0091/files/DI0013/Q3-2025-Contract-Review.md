# Q3 2025 Supplier Contract Review

## Introduction

This report provides an analysis of the supplier contracts renewed during the third quarter of 2025. It offers insights into the procurement activities of Lee's Market while highlighting areas of improvement and potential opportunities. The findings will assist in making informed decisions for future contract negotiations and renewals.

## Analysis of Supplier Contract Terms

Lee's Market has consistently worked to ensure fair, mutually beneficial terms in supplier contracts. Through rigorous negotiation, we succeeded in securing a favourable average contract length of 2.5 years in 2024. The most common contract terms relate to delivery schedules, quality standards, and payment conditions. In a bid to ensure long-term supplier relationships, the company also includes a clause for potential contract extensions.

## Implications of Contract Renewals

Regular contract renewals help Lee's Market maintain a stable supply chain and manage product availability effectively. In Q2 2025, the renewal of key produce supplier contracts resulted in a 2% overall increase in our stock levels, ensuring optimal product availability for our customers. Notably, contract renewals also allow us to negotiate better terms, potentially leading to cost savings.

## Q3 2025 Renewed Supplier Contracts

The renewed supplier contracts in Q3 2025 cover a wide range of product categories, from fresh produce to household items. Notably, the renewed contracts have enabled Lee's Market to ensure a steady supply of high-demand products. Following the renewals, we observed a minor increase in the delivery frequency from our suppliers, resulting in a reduction of out-of-stock situations.

## Conclusion

The report presents an overview of the supplier contracts renewed during Q3 2025, the terms of the contracts, and their implications. Moving forward, it is crucial to leverage these insights for more efficient contract negotiation and renewal processes.
