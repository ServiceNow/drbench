# Q4 2025 Workforce Analysis of Lee's Market

## Introduction

In order to understand our labor dynamics, an analysis has been conducted to assess the workforce composition of Lee's Market during Q4 2025. This report provides insights into the percentage of part-time employees, the distribution of full-time employees, and the overall employment trend in our retail outlets. The findings from this review will be instrumental in shaping our future staffing and operational strategies.

## Part-Time Workforce Distribution

As of Q1 2026, the majority of our part-time employees work in the produce and bakery departments. We have observed that these departments benefit most from flexible working hours, allowing for fresher products and better customer service. On weekends, the part-time workforce nearly doubles to meet the higher demand.

## Full-Time Employee Statistics

Full-time staff at Lee's Market have shown a steadily increasing tenure over the past five years. As of Q3 2025, the average tenure was 4.2 years, a 10% increase from Q3 2020. Our retention strategies appear to be effective in maintaining a stable, experienced workforce.

## Overall Employment Trend

Overall, Lee's Market has seen a steady growth in total employee count over the past five years. From 2021 to 2025, we increased our workforce by approximately 15%, largely driven by our expansion into new markets. We predict a similar trend in the next five years.

## Conclusion

The analysis revealed significant insights into the workforce distribution at Lee's Market. The data collected will help us to better align our hiring strategies and manage resources effectively. It is recommended that we continue to monitor these trends to ensure optimal staffing levels. The insights gathered will also be used to inform our budgeting and strategic decision-making process.
