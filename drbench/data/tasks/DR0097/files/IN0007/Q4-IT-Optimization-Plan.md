# Q4 2025 IT Workflows Optimization Plan

## Introduction

As MediConn continues to expand its virtual healthcare services, it's imperative that our IT infrastructure remains efficient and cost-effective. This document outlines strategies for streamlining our IT workflows in Q4 2025, with the aim to boost clinician efficiency and manage per-consultation costs. The focus areas are IT infrastructure optimization, clinician's digital experience enhancement, and cost-effective IT resource allocation.

## Optimizing IT Infrastructure for Virtual Consultations

In Q3 2025, MediConn piloted the implementation of a Unified Communications (UC) Platform that integrated various systems into a single user-friendly interface. This new platform merged functions such as messaging, video consultations, laboratory results review, and expert opinion requests. The outcome was a significant reduction in time spent navigating between various platforms, with an exceptional increase in navigation speed by approximately 90%. This crucial enhancement led to an overall increase in clinician efficiency, and consequently, a reduction in operational costs per consultation.

## Enhancing Clinician's Digital Experience

Our IT department launched a new training program in July 2025 to familiarize clinicians with the updated virtual consultation platform. We have seen an increase in the usage of advanced features by 25%. However, we are still struggling to get the older generation of clinicians to adapt to the new system.

## Cost-Effective Allocation of IT Resources

Since 2023, we have been using an AI-based system to allocate IT resources more effectively. The system helps us predict peak usage times and allocate resources accordingly, reducing downtime by 30%. The AI system also helps us identify redundant resources, allowing us to save costs.

## Conclusion

The successful optimization of our IT workflows to support the expansion of our virtual healthcare services will be a significant determinant of our clinician efficiency and cost-per-consultation. By focusing on these key areas - infrastructure optimization, digital experience enhancement, and cost-effective resource allocation - we can ensure a sustainable and efficient IT system. The next steps involve refining these strategies and establishing implementation timelines. Your feedback and cooperation in this endeavour are highly appreciated.
