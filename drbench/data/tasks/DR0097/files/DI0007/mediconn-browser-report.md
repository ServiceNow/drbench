## Analysis of User Demographics and Internet Habits

MediConn Solutions has a diverse user base spread across different age groups and regions. As per our 2024 analysis, about 60% of our users are aged between 30-50 years, indicating a tech-savvy demographic. Additionally, 80% of these users access our services using mobile devices, showcasing a growing trend towards mobile healthcare solutions.

## Overview of Internet Browser Market Trends

The internet browser market has seen dynamic shifts over the past few years. In 2022, Microsoft Edge experienced a surge in user base due to its enhanced security features, while Firefox witnessed a decline. The rise of privacy-centered browsers like DuckDuckGo also added a new dimension to the market trends.
