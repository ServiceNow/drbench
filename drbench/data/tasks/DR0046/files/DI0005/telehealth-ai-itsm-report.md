# Telehealth Awareness Month: AI in ITSM Report

## Introduction

As MediConn Solutions continues to lead the virtual healthcare industry, we took the opportunity during Telehealth Awareness Month to highlight the integral role of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in our Information Technology Service Management (ITSM). This document provides an overview of our activities and initiatives during this period, highlighting our commitment to technology-driven healthcare.

## AI Integration in Current ITSM Processes

As of 2024, MediConn Solutions has integrated AI into 80% of our ITSM processes. This has led to a significant decrease in resolution times across the board, with an average reduction of 30% noted by our IT department. The incorporation of AI has also enhanced the efficiency of our incident management and problem management processes.

## Telehealth Awareness Month: AI-focused Initiatives

MediConn Solutions has always prioritized AI-focused initiatives in telehealth. In April 2025, we launched an AI-powered chatbot to provide immediate responses to patient inquiries. This initiative was part of our continuous drive to embrace digital transformation and provide exceptional patient care.

## Impact of AI on User Experience and Compliance

AI has significantly improved user experiences at MediConn Solutions. A customer survey conducted in February 2025 showed a 20% increase in user satisfaction since the implementation of AI. Additionally, AI has streamlined our compliance processes by automating many data-related tasks.

## Conclusion

Our Telehealth Awareness Month activities underscore our commitment to integrating AI into our ITSM, providing enhanced user experiences and ensuring compliance. As we continue to pioneer virtual healthcare, keeping abreast of advancements in AI will be crucial. Going forward, we plan to capitalize on the momentum from this month to further our AI initiatives.
