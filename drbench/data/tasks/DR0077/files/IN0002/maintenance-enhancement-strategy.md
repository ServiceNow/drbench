# Predictive Maintenance Enhancement Strategy 2025

## Introduction

As Elexion Automotive continues to navigate the rapid evolution of the electric vehicle industry, effective management of our physical and digital assets is paramount. This document outlines a strategic approach for utilizing real-time data from IoT sensors and digital engineering-enabled twins. Our goal is to enhance predictive maintenance and minimize production line downtime, thereby increasing efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

## Impact of Predictive Maintenance on Operational Efficiency

Predictive maintenance has proven to be a game-changer in the automotive industry. In 2022, our plant in Michigan reduced its unscheduled downtimes by 18% by implementing a predictive maintenance strategy. These strategies are particularly effective for high-capacity production lines where even a small disruption can lead to significant financial losses.

## Integration of IoT Sensor Data with Digital Twins

As we move into Q1 2026, our internal analytics forecast a significant increase in the number of operational metrics we will gather from each of our production lines. With the integration of IoT sensors and digital twins, we expect to accumulate around 1,500 real-time metrics per line. This comprehensive data set will provide insights into equipment performance, production cycle times, and the early detection of any anomalies. This data-driven approach will aid in enhancing our predictive maintenance strategies and reducing the downtime in our production lines, contributing significantly to Elexion’s bottom line.

## Asset Lifecycle Management: Current Challenges and Opportunities

In terms of asset lifecycle management, one of our current challenges is the efficient tracking of software licenses. While our digital assets have grown by 25% since 2023, our software tracking capabilities have not kept pace. This presents an opportunity to improve our software asset management processes, potentially resulting in significant cost savings.

## Conclusion

By leveraging IoT sensor data and digital twins, we can significantly enhance our predictive maintenance strategies. This will lead to reduced downtime, increased operational efficiency, and cost savings. The challenges identified provide opportunities for further improvements in our asset lifecycle management. This strategy, once implemented, will contribute significantly to Elexion's goal of accelerating the transition to zero-emission transportation.
