# Q3 2025 Employee Catalog Feedback Report

## Introduction

This report provides an overview of the feedback received from Lee's Market employees regarding our product catalog for Q3 2025. We have analyzed internal email communications to identify patterns and trends. The aim is to understand employee preferences and potential areas for improvement in our product selection and offerings.

## Overview of Employee Feedback Channels

At Lee's Market, we utilize a variety of channels to collect employee feedback, including internal surveys, suggestion boxes, and quarterly town hall meetings. These channels ensure open communication lines between management and staff. In Q3 2025, 85% of our employees used these channels to share their opinions and feedback.

## Analysis of Q3 2025 Catalog Feedback

The Q3 2025 catalog introduced a variety of new products to our shelves. A total of 67% of feedback received was positive, with employees appreciating the diversity of our offerings. Notably, the introduction of new Korean and Japanese sauces were well-received.

## Impact of Employee Feedback on Customer Experience

Employee feedback plays a significant role in shaping the customer experience at Lee's Market. In Q3 2025, adjustments made based on employee feedback increased customer satisfaction by 12%. This improved our customer retention rate by 5%.

## Conclusion

Through the analysis of internal emails, we have identified the most praised catalog items by our employees during Q3 2025. This insight provides us with valuable information to enhance our product offerings and improve customer experience. We recommend focusing on the highlighted items in future marketing efforts. Continued tracking of employee feedback is suggested to ensure our product catalog remains aligned with both employee and customer preferences.
