# OTA Cybersecurity Resource Allocation Q3 2025

## Introduction

In a world increasingly reliant on digital technology, it is imperative that our electric vehicles are not only advanced but secure. This document provides an analysis of the cybersecurity team's resource allocation during Q3 2025, with a focus on the preparation of over-the-air (OTA) updates for our vehicles. The detailed insights will allow us to understand the proportion of resources dedicated to this integral aspect and plan better for future quarters.

## Q3 2025 Cybersecurity Team Resource Distribution

The Cybersecurity Team at Elexion Automotive in Q3 2025 was composed of 45 professionals, divided between threat intelligence and incident response. The latter team, responsible for managing potential security incidents, was allocated 60% of the team's resources. This strategic decision was made to ensure the robustness of Elexion's cybersecurity infrastructure, particularly in light of the increase in cyber threats targeting the automotive industry.

## Review of OTA Update Preparation Procedures

Elexion's OTA update preparation procedures involve a collaborative effort between the engineering, quality assurance, and cybersecurity teams. These procedures are structured around a rigorous testing and validation process, ensuring that all updates maintain the highest quality standards. Changes to the procedures, if necessary, are implemented after a thorough review process, which last happened in Q1 2025.

## Cybersecurity Efforts for OTA Updates in Q3 2025

The cybersecurity team's work on OTA updates in Q3 2025 focused on enhancing the encryption protocols and strengthening the firewall to prevent unauthorized access. They also conducted a comprehensive risk assessment to identify potential security gaps. However, our team's focus remains on providing a secure and user-friendly experience for all Elexion vehicle owners.

## Conclusion

Understanding the resource allocation towards preparing OTA updates in Q3 2025 provides valuable insights for future planning. As Elexion Automotive continues to innovate, balancing resource distribution among various cybersecurity tasks will remain a priority. The security of our OTA updates is paramount to maintaining our reputation for advanced, secure electric vehicles. Based on this analysis, steps will be taken to streamline processes and optimize resource allocation in the future.
