# Cybersecurity Budget Allocation FY2025

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing commitment to protect Elexion Automotive's digital infrastructure, this report provides an in-depth analysis of our cybersecurity budget allocation for the fiscal year 2025. One of the critical areas we will focus on is the allocation towards Research and Development (R&D) aimed at preventing future cyber-attacks. This is in line with our strategy to not only react to cybersecurity threats but also to anticipate and prevent them.

## Cybersecurity R&D: Strategy and Implementation

Our Cybersecurity R&D team at Elexion Automotive strives to stay ahead of emerging threats. We have adopted an agile methodology for rapid response and development. In 2023, we pioneered a proprietary threat detection algorithm that reduced system vulnerabilities by 30%. Our focus for the upcoming years is to enhance encryption standards and improve overall system resilience.

## Other Cybersecurity Expenditure Areas

Aside from R&D, Elexion Automotive also focuses on employee training and infrastructure security. In 2024, 35% of the cybersecurity budget was allocated to employee training programs and 25% to infrastructure security upgrades. Investments in these areas are crucial for the comprehensive security of our organization.

## Impact of Cybersecurity R&D on Future Threat Prevention

Our R&D efforts have significantly reduced the frequency of successful cyber attacks. Since the implementation of our proprietary algorithm in 2023, we've seen a 20% reduction in security breaches. We anticipate that ongoing R&D will continue to fortify our cybersecurity posture in the coming years.

## Conclusion

Our investment in cybersecurity R&D will strengthen our resilience against future threats. While balancing the budget across various cybersecurity requirements, we have prioritized R&D as a key area of focus. Moving forward, we should continue to assess and adapt our budget allocation based on changing threat landscapes. The next step should be to ensure that the R&D findings are effectively implemented across all departments.
