## Strategic Measures Against Remote Cyber Attacks

Our approach to preventing remote cyber attacks involves a multi-layered security system. Firstly, we have implemented an advanced intrusion detection system. In addition, we constantly update our firewall rules to detect and block any suspicious activities. Lastly, our regular network audits ensure we identify and fix potential vulnerabilities promptly.
