# Q3 2025 EV Range Benchmark Analysis

## Introduction

This report provides an overview of Elexion Automotive's Q3 2025 benchmarking efforts for the range performance of competitor electric vehicle (EV) models. As part of our ongoing product development and competitive positioning strategy, these benchmarking activities are essential in maintaining our technological edge and market leadership. This document will review the number of EV models we have benchmarked, some key findings from our research, and a comparison of our performance against the industry average.

## Competitor EV Models Benchmarked

In Q4 2024, Elexion Automotive benchmarked 15 competitor EV models, covering a broad spectrum of vehicle types. These models were divided into subcategories based on their market segments, such as luxury, mid-range, and economy. The benchmarking process was led by our Competitive Intelligence Department which meticulously gathered and analyzed data related to performance, design, technology and price.

## Key Findings from Range Benchmarking

As of Q2 2025, Elexion Automotive found that the average range of our EV models was 5% higher than the industry average. The benchmarking exercise also revealed that our flagship model, the Elexion E1, had the best range among mid-range EVs. However, there was still room for improvement in our compact segment offerings.

## Elexion vs Industry Average Range Performance

Elexion’s commitment to sustainability and user-centric technology has led to exceptional range performance. As of Q1 2025, Elexion's EVs demonstrated a 7% better range performance compared to the industry average. This was a result of our relentless focus on improving battery efficiency and aerodynamics.

## Conclusion

Our Q3 2025 benchmarking exercise has provided important insights that will inform our future product development and competitive strategy. Based on the number of models we have benchmarked, we have a robust understanding of the competitive landscape. Our performance against the industry average indicates areas for improvement and potential opportunities. We recommend leveraging these insights to enhance our EV range performance and maintain our competitive edge.
