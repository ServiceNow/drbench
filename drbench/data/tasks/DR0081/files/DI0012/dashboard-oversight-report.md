# Interactive Dashboard Oversight Report

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing commitment to operational transparency, this report provides an overview of the interactive dashboards overseen by the Competitive Intelligence Manager at Elexion Automotive. These dashboards serve as critical tools for disseminating real-time data to internal business units, aiding in strategic decision-making and performance monitoring. With a specific focus on the time period leading up to October 17, 2025, this report delineates the quantity and utility of these dashboards.

## Interactive Dashboard Utilization and Engagement

Since the first quarter of 2025, we've seen a 12% increase in dashboard utilization across different business units. The user engagement metrics have also displayed promising growth, with a 15% increase in time spent on the platform. The most significant gain was noticed in the design and engineering team, which suggests the dashboards are effectively assisting in decision-making and strategic planning.

## Impact of Dashboards on Business Unit Performance

In the second quarter of 2025, we observed a 7% improvement in business unit performance that correlated with a higher usage of the dashboards. The production and logistics departments exhibited significant efficiency gains due to data-driven decisions. Also, there was a marked reduction in operational bottlenecks, emphasizing the efficacy of our dashboards.

## Quarterly Overview of Dashboard Management

By the end of the third quarter of 2025, we have implemented several updates in our dashboards, including more precise tracking metrics and enhanced data visualization tools. These improvements have positively influenced the navigation and user experience of our dashboards. Moreover, we have also streamlined the onboarding process for new users, which has resulted in a 10% increase in user adoption.

## Conclusion

The increasing reliance on interactive dashboards underscores their growing significance in our business operations. Our focus moving forward will be to optimize these dashboards for better data accessibility and improved decision-making. It is recommended that each business unit continue to provide feedback on dashboard utility for further refinement. The Competitive Intelligence Manager will continue to oversee the ongoing management and improvement of these dashboards.
