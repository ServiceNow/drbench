## Impact of Regional Material Availability on Procurement

Historically, the bulk of lithium, a key ingredient in EV batteries, was sourced from South America. However, by 2023, Australia surpassed Chile as the world’s leading lithium producer, contributing 47% of global lithium supply. This shift has drastically altered the global lithium trade routes.
