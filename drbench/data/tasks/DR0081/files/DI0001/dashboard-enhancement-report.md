# Competitive Intelligence Dashboard Enhancement

## Introduction

This document presents an overview of the recent improvements made to our competitive intelligence dashboard. Specifically, it highlights the integration of real-time sales data from our North American EV competitors. This enhancement will provide us with more accurate, timely insights to inform our strategic decision-making process and further solidify our position in the market.

## Real-Time Data Integration: Technical Aspects

Elexion Automotive has invested heavily in the development of its big data infrastructure. By Q4 of 2024, our engineering team has improved the data ingestion rate by 20%, enabling faster processing of complex datasets. Our focus has been on improving the reliability and speed of our data pipelines to ensure uninterrupted data flow.

## Competitive Landscape: Sales Performance Analysis

In the electric vehicle industry, sales performance varies greatly across different regions due to infrastructure availability and local regulations. Between 2023 and 2025, we noticed a significant uptick in EV sales in the western region, with a 15% increase year on year. Elexion Automotive has been monitoring these trends to inform our strategic decisions.

## User Experience Improvements: Dashboard Functionality

In June 2025, we rolled out a significant update to our internal analytics dashboard aimed at improving user experience. This update introduced a more intuitive interface, faster loading times, and customizable data views, enhancing the effectiveness of our employees when interpreting and utilizing the data.

## Conclusion

The enhanced dashboard now provides real-time sales data, allowing us to compare our performance with competitors more accurately and promptly. As a result, we can make more informed decisions and adjust our strategies as needed. It is recommended that all relevant teams familiarize themselves with the new features and report any issues to the IT department. Let's leverage this tool to its maximum potential to ensure Elexion Automotive's continued success.
