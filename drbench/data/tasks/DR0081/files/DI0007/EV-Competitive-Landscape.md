In recent years, the electric vehicle market has seen significant investment in factory expansions, particularly in regions with favorable regulatory climates. In 2020, three major competitors initiated plans for new manufacturing facilities in North America, intending to increase production capacity by an average of 20% across the industry. These expansions were primarily focused on the production of electric sedans and SUVs, with a minority allocation for commercial vehicles.
