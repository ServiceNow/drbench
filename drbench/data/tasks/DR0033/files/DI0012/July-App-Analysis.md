# July 2025 App Usage Analysis

## Introduction

This report provides an in-depth analysis of the Lee's Market app usage for July 2025. It aims to offer insights into the overall app engagement, with a specific focus on the number of downloads during this period. The data analyzed here is crucial for understanding our digital footprint and strategizing future marketing and development efforts.

## Monthly User Engagement Metrics

In June 2025, our monthly user engagement report showed that 56% of our consumers engaged with our online promotions, a 6% increase from May. The majority of these interactions were through our social media platforms, particularly Instagram and Facebook. Our in-store initiatives, such as our QR code system, also saw a 15% increase in use, demonstrating the effectiveness of our omnichannel approach.

## July 2025 App Download Analysis

Our July 2025 app update to version 5.7.1 included a host of new features designed to improve the shopping experience. These updates included enhanced product search functionality, personalized product recommendations, and an improved checkout process. Post-update, the app's user rating saw an increase from 4.1 to 4.3 stars, indicating a positive response from the user base.

## User Demographics and Behavioral Insights

Our user demographics in July 2025 showed that 60% of our online shoppers are between the ages of 25 to 34. The gender distribution is almost equal, with a slight lead for female shoppers at 52%. Interestingly, we have noticed a 10% increase in shopping activity during the weekends, particularly on Sunday afternoons.

## Conclusion

Overall, our digital presence continues to grow steadily with a significant number of new app downloads in July 2025. The user engagement and behavioral insights highlight areas for potential improvement and growth. We recommend leveraging these insights for targeted marketing strategies and app enhancements going forward.
