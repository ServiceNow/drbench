# 2025 Bakery Vendor Quality Audit Report

## Introduction

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the Bakery Vendor Quality Audits conducted by Jason Wong and the Bakery Team in 2025. The report reflects our commitment to ensuring the highest quality standards for our customers, and provides insights into our vendor selection and management processes. It encompasses the methodology, key findings, and strategic implications of the audits. This information is vital for our ongoing vendor management and quality assurance efforts.

## Audit Methodology and Vendor Selection Criteria

Our audit methodology at Lee's Market is designed to ensure the highest quality products for our customers. The preliminary phase includes a thorough analysis of the vendor's financial stability, followed by an examination of their production and quality control processes. In 2024, we enhanced our selection criteria to include a closer look at environmental sustainability practices, which led to a 15% increase in the sourcing of eco-friendly products in our stores.

## Key Findings from 2025 Vendor Audits

During the 2025 vendor audits, we identified a marked improvement in vendor compliance to our quality standards, with a 20% reduction in non-compliance incidents compared to 2024. Additionally, we noted an increase in voluntary sustainability initiatives among our vendors. However, there was a concerning rise in packaging inconsistencies, which we have flagged for further investigation.

## Strategic Implications and Next Steps

As a result of the 2025 vendor audits, Lee's Market plans to update its vendor selection criteria to include more stringent requirements for packaging consistency. We also intend to provide incentives for vendors who demonstrate commitment to sustainability practices. By Q2 2026, we aim to increase the percentage of sustainable products in our stores by an additional 10%.

## Conclusion

The 2025 audits underscored our commitment to quality and provided crucial insights for our vendor management strategy. The findings from these audits will guide our future selections and relationships with vendors. It is recommended that we continue to conduct these audits annually to maintain the high standards Lee's Market is known for. We anticipate these results will yield improvements in our product quality, customer satisfaction, and overall brand reputation.
