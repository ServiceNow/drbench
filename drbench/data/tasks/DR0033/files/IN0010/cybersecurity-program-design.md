# Cybersecurity Awareness & Training Program Design

## Introduction

As we expand our operations and enhance our digital footprint, it becomes increasingly important to prioritize cybersecurity across Lee's Market. This document aims to lay out a comprehensive plan to develop a robust cybersecurity awareness and training program tailored to our unique needs, including high employee turnover and seasonal hiring. This program will equip our employees with necessary knowledge and skills to mitigate security risks, reduce incidents caused by human error, and support our growth in the US and Canadian markets.

## Managing Cybersecurity Risks Amid High Employee Turnover

An encouraging trend has been observed within Lee's Market in 2025. Amid the challenge of high employee turnover, we managed to involve more than six out of ten seasonal staff members in at least one cyberattack simulation or phishing drill. This innovative approach to training has proven its effectiveness, as our participation rates have exceeded the industry's 2024 rates by 6%. These figures underscore the effectiveness of our proactive measures in reducing cybersecurity risks associated with seasonal hiring.

## Mitigating Human Error through Regular Cybersecurity Training

As of Q1 2025, 84% of Lee's Market employees have completed our basic cybersecurity training module. The module, focusing on password protection and secure browsing, has been a mandatory part of the onboarding process. Our IT department is constantly updating the module to address the evolving threat landscape.

## Adapting Cybersecurity Measures for US and Canadian Operations

Our IT & Security team at Lee's Market works diligently to ensure compliance with both U.S. and Canadian cybersecurity regulations. In 2023, an audit from the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC) resulted in a successful clearance, highlighting our commitment to secure operations across markets.

## Conclusion

Implementing a robust cybersecurity awareness and training program is a proactive step towards securing Lee's Market's digital and operational assets. The outlined strategies addressing employee turnover, human error, and regional compliance will ensure we are equipped to handle emerging cybersecurity challenges. It is recommended that all relevant departments collaborate closely to ensure the successful execution of these initiatives. Let's work together to make Lee's Market a safer place to shop and work.
