# Cybersecurity Awareness & Training Program Strategy

## Introduction

As Lee's Market expands its operations across the U.S. and Canada, the need for a comprehensive cybersecurity awareness and training program becomes increasingly paramount. This document outlines the strategy for implementing such a program, aimed at minimizing security risks associated with high employee turnover, seasonal hiring, and incidents caused by human error. Our strategy supports the company's growth and strives for compliance with data protection regulations across all our operations.

## Assessing Current Cybersecurity Awareness Levels

In evaluating our cybersecurity posture, we noted a significant uptick in security protocol compliance over the course of 2025. During the first half of the year, our audit pass rate was 78%, signaling room for improvement. However, with the implementation of targeted refresher trainings and increased management focus, we witnessed a commendable increase in our security protocol adherence, reaching a pass rate of 94% in the latter half of the year. This progression underscores the effectiveness of our adaptive training methods in enhancing our security landscape.

## Designing Tailored Cybersecurity Training Modules

In 2024, we collaborated with an external cybersecurity firm to develop training modules for our finance and HR departments. This program focused on protecting sensitive data in their domains and achieved a 90% satisfaction rate. We are considering expanding this partnership to other departments in 2026.

## Rollout Strategy and Program Implementation

Our company's last significant policy rollout was the mandatory two-factor authentication for all company-wide systems in 2024. We achieved a 95% compliance rate within three months. We are planning to introduce a new data encryption policy in Q2 of 2026.

## Conclusion

Through a thorough assessment of our current cybersecurity awareness, the design of tailored training modules, and a carefully articulated rollout strategy, we can safeguard Lee's Market's digital infrastructure. This approach will not only mitigate security risks but also foster a culture of cybersecurity awareness across the organization. Our next steps involve the detailed planning and execution of each phase, with a focus on continuous improvement and adaptability. This program is integral to our commitment to secure operations and customer trust.
