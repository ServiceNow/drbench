# Virtual Care Promotion Strategy Q4 2026

## Introduction

With the rise in virtual healthcare, MediConn Solutions has an opportunity to leverage this trend and enhance its service offerings. The following document outlines a strategic approach to promote our virtual services to our current patients. This includes a review of market trends, a competitive analysis, and a proposed marketing campaign for Q4 2026.

## Competitive Analysis of Virtual Care Providers

As of Q2 2025, there are approximately 500 companies providing virtual care services in Canada. However, only 10% of these companies have a market share above 5%. The top three companies, based on market share, are HealthOnline, CareVirtually, and eDoctor. These providers offer a wide range of services, from general consultations to specialized care.

## Understanding the Virtual Healthcare Market

In our exploration of the virtual healthcare sector, a patient satisfaction evaluation conducted by our marketing team in April 2024 revealed noteworthy findings. A significant 78% of our user base gave positive feedback, expressing a willingness to recommend MediConn Solutions' services to their acquaintances. This high level of patient endorsement underscores the efficacy of our digital healthcare services, and provides a solid base from which to market our virtual care capabilities to a wider audience, especially considering the projected shift towards virtual visits as outlined by McKinsey and Company.

## Strategic Marketing Campaign for Virtual Services

In Q1 2025, we launched a marketing campaign targeting businesses to incorporate our virtual care services into their employee benefits packages. The campaign resulted in a 15% increase in B2B clients. For Q3 2025, we're planning a campaign focused on individual users, aiming to highlight the convenience and cost-effectiveness of our services.

## Conclusion

The evolving landscape of the healthcare industry presents a significant opportunity for MediConn Solutions to expand its virtual care services. A detailed understanding of the market, careful analysis of competitors, and a targeted marketing strategy will help us effectively promote our virtual services. The next steps involve refining this plan based on stakeholder feedback and preparing for implementation.
