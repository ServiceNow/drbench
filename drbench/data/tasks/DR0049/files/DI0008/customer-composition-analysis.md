# MediConn Customer Composition Analysis 2025

## Introduction

This report provides an in-depth analysis of our customer base distribution in the year 2025. Our focus is to understand the proportion of corporate wellness program participants relative to individual patients. The insights derived from this report will help us tailor our services to better meet the needs of our dominant customer segment. Additionally, the information will guide our marketing and product development strategies.

## Analysis of Corporate Wellness Program Participation

Our corporate wellness program has seen a notable increase since its inception in 2020. From 2020 to 2022, there was a 55% increase in corporate partner sign-ups, demonstrating a positive trend towards virtual healthcare in the corporate world. The program's success can be attributed to its comprehensive coverage, including mental health support, chronic disease management, and preventive care.

## Overview of Individual Patient Demographics

MediConn's individual patient base is diverse, with a wide age range from 18 to 85. In 2023, we observed that 30% of our patients were aged 18-30, 40% were 31-50, and the remaining 30% were over 50. This broad demographic footprint underscores the wide appeal and accessibility of our virtual healthcare services.

## Customer Engagement Metrics and Trends

Customer engagement on our virtual platforms has been steadily increasing. Between 2022 and 2024, we observed a 25% growth in active user sessions and a 15% increase in average session duration. This suggests a growing customer trust and reliance on our digital healthcare solutions.

## Conclusion

The data presented in this report illustrates the distribution of our customer base in 2025. Our corporate wellness program participants comprise a significant percentage, highlighting the importance of these programs in our service offerings. We must continue to enhance these programs to meet the needs of this dominant customer segment. Further, we should also explore strategies to increase individual patient participation, thus diversifying our customer base.
