# Customer Service Salary Analysis 2025

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing efforts to maintain competitive compensation for our employees, this report presents a detailed analysis of the salary range for customer service representatives on Rachel's team in 2025. By working through a comprehensive review of internal data and industry benchmarks, we strive to ensure fair remuneration for our staff. This analysis is critical to attract and retain the best talent in a continually evolving virtual healthcare industry.

## Industry Salary Benchmarks for Virtual Healthcare Customer Service

In 2024, the average salary for virtual healthcare customer service representatives in Canada was $47,000 CAD. This was slightly lower than the broader healthcare sector, which averaged $50,000 CAD for the same role. Trends suggest an upward trajectory in compensation due to increased demand for virtual healthcare services.

## Review of Internal Compensation Policies

MediConn Solutions regularly reviews its compensation policies to ensure they align with industry standards and the cost of living. The last review was conducted in Q4 2024, resulting in a 3% increase in the overall salary budget. Future reviews are scheduled bi-annually to maintain competitiveness.

## Customer Service Team Salary Analysis

The Customer Service department has seen a steady increase in its overall salary budget over the past five years. This trend is largely attributed to the expansion of the team to accommodate the increasing demand for our services. As of 2024, the department consisted of 40 full-time employees.
