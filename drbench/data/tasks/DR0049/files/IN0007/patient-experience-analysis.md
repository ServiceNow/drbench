# Patient Experience Improvement Analysis

## Introduction

To ensure the highest standard of virtual care at MediConn Solutions, it is essential to address any barriers affecting patients' experiences. Specifically, this report focuses on potential issues with our online booking tools and customer support process that may be contributing to difficulties in scheduling appointments. As Director of Customer Service, I have conducted an evaluation to identify the root cause of these challenges and suggest actionable strategies for improvement.

## Assessment of Online Booking System Usability

In assessing our online booking system's efficacy, we observed a worrisome trend in the final quarter of 2024. A significant portion of our customer base, a full 60%, did not receive an email response from our support team within the industry-standard 24-hour window. Instead, the average time taken to respond to these support requests was a concerning 36 hours. This delay could potentially contribute to the barriers patients face while attempting to book appointments, thus calling for immediate rectification.

## Evaluation of Customer Support Processes

In the last fiscal year, our customer support team handled over 10,000 inbound calls, a 12% increase from the previous year. The average call duration has been maintained at 8 minutes, with the majority of calls being resolved on the first contact. We are currently implementing a new training program for customer service representatives, which will be fully operational by Q1 2023.

## Strategies for Enhancing Patient Appointment Experience

To enhance the patient appointment experience, we are piloting a new feature that allows patients to save their preferred healthcare professionals. Since the pilot launch in April, early feedback has been overwhelmingly positive. We anticipate a full rollout of this feature by the end of Q2 2023.

## Conclusion

The identified challenges within our online booking tools and customer support processes are surmountable with focused strategies. By implementing these improvements, we can not only enhance the patient experience but also strengthen our brand's reputation for accessible and user-friendly healthcare services. Our next steps include refining these strategies, obtaining necessary approvals, and planning for efficient implementation. All departments must work collaboratively to ensure the success of these changes.
