# Improving Patient Appointment Experience

## Introduction

As MediConn Solutions aims to provide seamless virtual healthcare services, it is crucial to continually refine our service delivery mechanisms. Recent research indicates that some patients are experiencing difficulty with our online appointment booking process and call center interactions. This report aims to understand and address these issues, focusing on the potential root causes, including the design of the booking tool and the complexity of our customer support processes.

## Efficiency Review of Customer Support Processes

Our customer service department has consistently met its target of answering 85% of calls within the first minute throughout 2022. We have also implemented a new customer relationship management (CRM) system, which has improved our ability to manage and track customer interactions. However, we continue to face challenges in addressing complex queries, leading to longer call durations.

## Strategies for Enhancing Patient Experience

In 2021, we introduced a patient satisfaction survey to gain insights into our patients' experiences. Our results showed that 80% of patients were 'satisfied' or 'very satisfied' with their overall experience. However, we have identified areas for improvement, particularly in streamlining our post-appointment follow-up procedures.

## Analysis of Current Online Booking Tool

Our internal data reveals that during the last quarter of 2024, nearly a third of support tickets related to booking were escalated to the technical support or product teams. This suggests that frontline customer service representatives encountered challenges in resolving these issues within the existing booking tool. This level of escalation indicates potential design flaws or usability issues in our online booking tool that may be hindering customers from making appointments effortlessly.

## Conclusion

The report has provided valuable insights into our online booking tool and customer support processes. It is clear that we need to improve these aspects to enhance the patient experience. The insights gained will serve as a foundation for deploying targeted improvements. A robust plan will be developed to address the identified issues and will require cross-functional collaboration to ensure its successful implementation.
