# Enhancing Patient Experience: Appointment Booking Review

## Introduction

As MediConn Solutions continues to grow and improve, understanding and addressing the patient experience is of utmost importance. The purpose of this report is to explore the potential causes of dissatisfaction regarding our appointment booking process. We will be focusing on our online booking tool functionality and the efficiency of our call center processes. By addressing these areas, we aim to enhance patient satisfaction and retention.

## Evaluation of Call Center Operational Efficiency

Our call center has consistently met industry benchmarks for operational efficiency. In Q3 2023, we maintained an average call wait time of 2 minutes, well within the acceptable threshold. We also introduced a new training program for our customer service representatives, which resulted in a 5% increase in first-call resolution rates by the end of the year.

## Impact and Interrelation of Booking Channels on Patient Experience

In a survey conducted in Q4 2023, 45% of our patients reported using both online and call center channels for booking appointments. Interestingly, patients who utilized both channels reported a 15% higher satisfaction rate compared to those who used just one. This suggests an integrated approach to booking channels may enhance patient experience.

## Analysis of Online Booking Tool Usability

Our examination of the final quarter of 2024 revealed a significant trend in booking-related customer inquiries. We found that nearly half, exactly 45%, of these inquiries resulted in customer support staff having to intervene and complete the booking process for the customer. This indicates a potential issue with the usability of our online booking tools, as a substantial portion of our clientele is unable to independently finalize their appointments. This trend emphasizes the necessity for improvements in our digital booking platform to enhance user experience and reduce manual intervention.

## Conclusion

The findings of this report identify key areas within our online booking system and call center operations that require improvement. Addressing these issues will result in a more streamlined appointment booking process, improving the overall patient experience. We recommend implementing immediate improvements to our online booking tool, and reassessing our call center processes. This will ensure we continue to provide accessible and efficient healthcare service to our patients.
