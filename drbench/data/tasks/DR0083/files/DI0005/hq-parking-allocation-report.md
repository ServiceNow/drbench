# HQ Parking Allocation Modifications Report

## Introduction

In light of recent changes in our organization and the growing number of Elexion team members, the Facilities team has implemented changes to the parking allocation at our Headquarters. This report aims to provide an overview of these changes, the rationale behind them, and the impact on our team members. It is crucial to understand these changes for efficient utilization of our infrastructure and maintaining harmony in the workplace.

## Impact of EV Proliferation on Parking Infrastructure

The rise in EV ownership has significantly impacted the parking infrastructure. Many businesses, including Elexion, are investing in EV charging stations to accommodate this shift. In 2019, we witnessed a 15% increase in EV parking demand, prompting a reevaluation of our facility's infrastructure.

## Facilities Upgrade: New Parking Management System

We recently implemented a new digital parking management system in January 2021. This system allows us to monitor parking space usage efficiently. It uses AI to predict peak usage times and helps in managing the flow of vehicles, especially during rush hours.

## Revised Parking Allocation Policy

As a company, we're focused on continuously improving our parking policies. For instance, in 2020, we made adjustments to prioritize parking for staff working odd hours. Additionally, we implemented a rotation policy to ensure more equitable access to parking for all staff.

## Conclusion

The changes in our parking allocation have been implemented to accommodate Elexion’s growth and the evolving needs of our team. They also resonate with our commitment to promoting electric vehicles by providing charging infrastructure. It is essential that all employees understand and comply with the new policy for a smooth transition. The Facilities team will closely monitor the implementation and address any issues that arise to ensure minimal disruption.
