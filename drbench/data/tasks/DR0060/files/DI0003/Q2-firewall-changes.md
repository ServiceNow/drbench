# Q2 2025 Firewall Rule Changes Report

## Introduction

This report aims to detail the cybersecurity actions taken during the second quarter of 2025, notably the changes in firewall rules. As MediConn Solutions continues to bolster its virtual healthcare platforms, a strong emphasis on cybersecurity remains essential. This document specifically covers the period from April 1 to June 30, 2025, focusing on the actions of our cybersecurity specialist team.

## Evaluation of Q2 2025 Cybersecurity Threat Landscape

The threat landscape in Q2 2025 was characterized by an increase in phishing attempts by 20%. Additionally, there was a significant uptick in the number of ransomware attacks, leading to a 15% rise in reports compared to Q1. The healthcare sector, including virtual healthcare, remained a prime target for cybercriminals, emphasizing the need for robust cybersecurity measures.

## Firewall Rule Modifications and Their Impact

The firewall rule modifications implemented by the cybersecurity team have resulted in a decrease in unauthorized traffic by approximately 30%. Additionally, these modifications have improved the overall system response times by 18%, leading to an enhanced user experience. The changes also improved the platform's robustness, significantly reducing the number of reported technical issues.

## Overview of Cybersecurity Incidents in Q2 2025

In Q2 2025, we recorded a total of 37 cybersecurity incidents. These mainly consisted of attempted unauthorized access and phishing attempts, but there were also 5 confirmed data breaches. The quick response from our cybersecurity team prevented any significant data loss or service disruption.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, the changes to firewall rules during Q2 2025 reflect our continuous commitment to the safety of our virtual healthcare platforms. The cybersecurity team has been proactive in ensuring the security of our systems amidst the evolving threat landscape. It is recommended that these changes be reviewed regularly for optimization. Our vigilance in this area will continue to be crucial in maintaining the trust of our clients and partners.
