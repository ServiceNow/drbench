# Cybersecurity Training Compliance Report 2024

## Introduction

This report reviews the completion status of the 2024 security awareness refresher sessions led by our cybersecurity specialist. These sessions are integral to maintaining the robust security standards of MediConn Solutions' virtual care team. The document aims to identify the effectiveness and reach of our cybersecurity training program. It also presents the necessary steps to ensure complete participation in future sessions.

## Overview of 2024 Cybersecurity Training Program

In 2024, MediConn Solutions' cybersecurity training program was designed to align with the evolving digital healthcare landscape. It included modules on data privacy, encryption, and secure patient communication. The training was mandatory for all employees, with quarterly check-ins to reinforce key learnings.

## Completion Status of Security Awareness Sessions

During 2024, a total of 12 security awareness sessions were conducted. These sessions emphasized the importance of strong passwords, understanding phishing attacks, and recognizing suspicious online activities. The cybersecurity specialist oversaw the delivery of these sessions.

## Recommendations for Enhancing Participant Engagement

For future sessions, it is recommended to incorporate interactive elements such as quizzes and role-play scenarios to improve participant engagement. Additionally, offering incentives for active participation could further drive interest and comprehension. These strategies could enhance the effectiveness of our cybersecurity training program.

## Conclusion

The report provides an in-depth analysis of our security awareness training's completion rate. While the majority of our virtual care team successfully completed the sessions, some areas need improvement. Action plans, including targeted follow-ups and more accessible training schedules, will be implemented to ensure full participation in the future. The continuous improvement of our security training program is crucial in safeguarding our digital platforms and maintaining the trust of our patients and partners.
