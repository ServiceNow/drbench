# Elexion OTA Cybersecurity Measures 2025

## Introduction

The following report provides an in-depth analysis of the cybersecurity measures implemented by the Elexion Automotive team during the fiscal year 2025. Our focus has been on assessing the quantity, effectiveness, and focus of over-the-air (OTA) updates, particularly those aimed at reactive safety measures against cyber threats. The insights extracted from this report will assist in understanding our cybersecurity landscape and inform our strategic planning for the coming years.

## Analysis of Cyber Threat Landscape in 2025

In 2025, the automotive industry witnessed a 20% increase in cyber threats compared to the previous year. This surge was partly due to the rise in connected and autonomous vehicles, which offer new avenues for cyber-attacks. Threats ranged from data breaches, which could compromise user information, to complex attacks designed to manipulate vehicle functionality.

## OTA Update Strategy and Efficacy

Elexion's OTA update strategy involves routine checks and balances to ensure the stability and performance of our vehicles. The software department works in tandem with the engineering team to roll out these updates, which are designed to enhance user experience and vehicle efficiency. In 2025, we had a success rate of 95% in OTA update installations.

## The Impact of Cybersecurity Measures on Vehicle Safety

Our cybersecurity measures have had a profound impact on improving vehicle safety. They have helped us mitigate cyber threats and safeguard our customers' data. In 2025, our efforts resulted in a 30% reduction in the number of reported security incidents per vehicle, contributing to an overall enhancement of trust among our customer base.

## Conclusion

The year 2025 witnessed an escalation in cyber threats, necessitating robust and frequent OTA updates to ensure the safety of our electric vehicles. Our cybersecurity team's proactive measures have effectively countered these threats, as evidenced by the low incidence of breach attempts. Moving forward, we should continue to invest in enhancing our cybersecurity capabilities, maintaining a keen focus on reactive measures to evolving cyber threats. Further, we should consider incorporating insights from this report into the development of our future OTA update strategies.
