# Cybersecurity Adaptability Report 2026

## Introduction

As we approach 2026, it is crucial to assess Elexion Automotive's cybersecurity posture in light of emerging threats and technologies, especially given the vulnerabilities exposed in the April 2025 Help Net Security article. This report provides an overview of our current cybersecurity measures, an analysis of the latest threats, and a roadmap for enhancing our security infrastructure to safeguard the company and our consumer base.

## Current Cybersecurity Infrastructure and Practices

To fortify our cybersecurity practices against the evolving threats highlighted in the Help Net Security article, we are planning significant updates to our customer web portal. Scheduled for the first week of January 2026, this upgrade will focus on enhancing user authentication. As part of this strategy, we are moving away from SMS-verified two-factor authentication and introducing mandatory use of authenticator applications for a more secure login experience. This proactive measure will ensure the highest level of security for our customers and their information, protecting against potential threats and vulnerabilities.

## Analysis of Emerging Cybersecurity Threats

In the wider automotive industry, emerging threats are a constant concern. For example, a recent report highlighted a 20% rise in phishing attacks targeting automotive companies in Q3 2025. However, the most alarming trend is the increasing sophistication of these attacks, with multi-vector attacks becoming more common.

## Proactive Measures for Enhanced Cybersecurity in 2026

Going forward, cybersecurity will remain a top priority for Elexion. We're currently drafting a proposal for an increased cybersecurity budget in 2026, aiming to further expand our team and resources. Additionally, we're exploring partnerships with external cybersecurity firms to further enhance our capabilities.

## Conclusion

Our preparedness to adapt and evolve with changing cybersecurity threats is crucial to ensure the integrity of Elexion Automotive's data and systems. The outlined proactive measures will help us to fortify our defenses and maintain consumer trust. Going forward, we must continue regular vulnerability assessments, stay abreast of emerging threats, and constantly refine our cybersecurity strategies.
