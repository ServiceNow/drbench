# 2025 Q1-Q2 Cybersecurity Vulnerability Analysis

## Introduction

As part of MediConn Solutions' ongoing commitment to delivering secure, compliant, and user-friendly care to patients across Canada, we continuously monitor and assess the security of our virtual platform. This document provides an overview of our cybersecurity activities during the first two quarters of 2025, with a particular focus on the identification and remediation of high-severity vulnerabilities. The contents herein will offer insights into the effectiveness of our cybersecurity measures and provide context for future risk mitigation strategies.

## Overview of Vulnerability Assessments Conducted

Throughout 2024, MediConn Solutions conducted a series of quarterly cybersecurity assessments. These assessments were performed by our in-house cybersecurity team, in collaboration with our IT department. The focus was put on identifying and addressing potential weaknesses in our digital healthcare platform, with a particular emphasis on improving data privacy and patient confidentiality.

## Analysis of High-Severity Vulnerabilities Identified

By the end of 2024, a total of 18 high-severity vulnerabilities were identified in our virtual care platform. These vulnerabilities, predominantly found in the patient data management module, raised significant concerns over data integrity and privacy. Following identification, we prioritized these vulnerabilities for immediate remediation.

## Remediation Efforts and Effectiveness for Q1-Q2

In Q1-Q2 of 2024, the cybersecurity team successfully remediated 12 high-severity vulnerabilities. These efforts significantly improved the security posture of our platform. Our remediation success rate was 80%, far exceeding the industry average of 65% during the same period.

## Conclusion

The security of our virtual care platform remains a top priority for MediConn Solutions. The first half of 2025 has seen effective identification and remediation of high-severity vulnerabilities, demonstrating the robustness of our cybersecurity measures. Going forward, we will continue to strengthen our vulnerability detection and remediation procedures, ensuring the highest level of security for our users. This ongoing commitment to cybersecurity will further solidify MediConn Solutions as a trusted provider of virtual healthcare services.
