# HHS Cybersecurity Performance Goals Integration

## Introduction

As MediConn Solutions continues to expand its virtual healthcare services, the integration of the HHS Cybersecurity Performance Goals (CPGs) into our platforms becomes an essential next step. This document provides a roadmap for our compliance journey, addressing both internal systems and third-party vendors. The focus is on integrating these standards while proactively mitigating emerging cyber threats, ensuring the continued security of our platforms.

## Assessment of Current Cybersecurity Infrastructure

In the initial quarter of 2025, our cybersecurity specialist, in conjunction with IT operations and compliance leaders, established a cross-functional cybersecurity team. The primary objective of this team is to navigate the integration of HHS Cybersecurity Performance Goals (CPGs) into MediConn's virtual healthcare platform. The collaborative effort between these departments is an essential step in fortifying our cybersecurity infrastructure, providing a strategic response to emerging threats, and ensuring continuous compliance with third-party vendors.

## Third-Party Vendor Security Compliance Review

As part of our annual review in 2023, 85% of our third-party vendors met our stringent cybersecurity standards. Vendor audits are conducted by an external cybersecurity agency, which checks their adherence to protocols like GDPR and HIPAA. We are planning to conduct the next review in Q2 2025.

## Cybersecurity Performance Goals Implementation Strategy

Our team is currently developing an implementation strategy for the ISO 27001 standard. The plan, set for rollout in Q1 2026, includes a risk assessment phase, an implementation phase, and a certification phase. This will significantly boost our existing cybersecurity framework.

## Conclusion

By integrating the HHS Cybersecurity Performance Goals into our platforms, MediConn Solutions will bolster security and maintain compliance in the evolving landscape of virtual healthcare. Our current infrastructure and third-party vendors will be key areas of focus. The next steps involve detailed planning for CPGs implementation and continued collaboration with our development team. Together, we will ensure the safe and secure provision of our virtual healthcare services.
