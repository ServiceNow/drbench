# Employee ID Update Compliance Report

## Introduction

As Elexion Automotive continues to grow, maintaining up-to-date employee records is crucial to our operations. This report assesses the compliance of our workforce in updating their ID photos for this year, an essential measure in our security and HR systems. The report also explores related matters, such as employee engagement with HR tools and the efficiency of our ID update process.

## Employee Engagement with HR Digital Tools

The adoption of HR digital tools by Elexion’s employees has been steadily increasing over the last two years. In 2021, approximately 83% of employees were using the HR portal on a monthly basis. The HR team has also recorded a 12% increase in the use of the self-service features in the digital tool, suggesting employees are becoming more comfortable with managing their personal information online.

## Efficiency Analysis of the ID Update Process

An audit of the ID update process conducted in the second quarter revealed that the process is taking longer than expected. On average, it takes about 15 minutes for each employee to complete the ID update. The HR team is exploring ways to streamline the process and reduce this time to 10 minutes by Q3 2023.

## Annual Compliance Rates for ID Photo Updates

In 2020, the HR department implemented a new policy requiring all employees to update their ID photos every three years. Since then, we have seen a steady increase in compliance with this policy. Compliance rates in the first year were 67%, and we anticipate this trend to continue as employees become more familiar with the policy.

## Conclusion

Maintaining current employee ID photos is an important part of our security and HR protocols. While we've seen a positive response in compliance rates, there's room for improvement in the efficiency of the update process and employee engagement with our HR tools. We recommend that HR and IT departments collaborate on streamlining the ID update process and improving the user experience of our HR digital tools.
