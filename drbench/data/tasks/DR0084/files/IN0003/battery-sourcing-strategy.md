# Battery Sourcing Strategy Amidst Lithium Volatility

## Introduction

As Elexion Automotive continues to lead in the production of affordable electric vehicles, strategic sourcing of key materials like lithium becomes increasingly critical. This document explores potential adjustments to our battery sourcing strategy to mitigate expected lithium price volatility in 2025. It is vital for our competitive positioning and future profitability to proactively address this challenge. The insights provided herein will guide our decision-making and strategic planning.

## Current Battery Sourcing Strategy and Associated Risks

Our battery sourcing strategy has always prioritized quality and reliability. In 2020, our defect rate was just 0.03%, a significant achievement. We also managed to reduce our supply chain disruptions by 20% by diversifying our supplier base.

## Understanding Lithium Market Dynamics and Projections

Elexion's lithium sourcing strategy currently involves two suppliers, both of whom have their pricing models linked to the volatility of the global spot market, with adjustments made on a quarterly basis. This approach exposes Elexion to potential price fluctuations and unpredictable costs, potentially hampering our ability to maintain affordability in our EV offerings. Given the projections for lithium price instability in 2025, a review of our supplier agreements and diversification of our sourcing strategy may be necessary to mitigate risk and ensure steady supply chains.

## Alternative Sourcing Strategies for Lithium Battery Production

One potential strategy is the recycling of EV batteries, a method that could help reduce dependency on raw materials. However, this requires significant investments in recycling facilities. As of 2022, only 5% of lithium-ion batteries are recycled globally.

## Conclusion

Given the projected volatility in lithium prices, revising our battery sourcing strategy is necessary to safeguard Elexion's financial stability and competitive edge. The exploration of lithium market dynamics and current sourcing risks underscores the urgency of this issue. The proposed alternative sourcing strategies offer a range of potential solutions. We recommend prioritizing discussions on this matter to facilitate strategic decision-making and planning.
