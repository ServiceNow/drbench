# Battery Sourcing Strategy Amidst Lithium Volatility

## Introduction

As Elexion Automotive continues to lead in the production of affordable, technology-forward EVs, it is imperative to address looming challenges in our battery sourcing strategy. The projected lithium price volatility in 2025 is one such challenge. This document explores potential adjustments to our sourcing strategy to mitigate the impacts of the predicted trend, providing an informed basis for decision-making.

## Understanding the Lithium Market Dynamics

A deep dive into Elexion's remote diagnostic logs reveals a significant issue affecting the efficiency of our service operations. It appears that over one-third of service issues necessitate escalation, primarily due to initial troubleshooting efforts being inadequate before the customer's visit. These findings suggest that we are not fully leveraging our remote diagnostic capabilities, potentially impacting both customer experience and our operational efficiency. This insight should be factored into our strategic planning, particularly as we look to mitigate the predicted lithium price volatility in 2025.

## Current Battery Sourcing Strategies and Challenges

Our current battery sourcing strategy involves procurement contracts with two primary lithium suppliers. The procurement team has been facing issues with irregular deliveries from these suppliers. This has caused minor disruptions in our production schedule.

## Strategic Alternatives to Current Lithium Sourcing

One alternative to our current lithium sourcing could be exploring partnerships with emerging lithium mining startups. However, this approach would require thorough due diligence as the rate of startup failure in the lithium mining industry is quite high.

## Conclusion

By gaining a comprehensive understanding of the lithium market dynamics and the challenges in our current sourcing strategy, we can devise effective alternatives to mitigate risks associated with lithium price volatility. As we progress, these insights will guide our strategic adjustments and ensure that Elexion Automotive remains competitive and resilient. The next steps include detailed feasibility studies of the identified alternatives and an implementation plan for the selected strategy.
