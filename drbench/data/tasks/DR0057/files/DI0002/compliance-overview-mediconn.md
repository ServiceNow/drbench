## Overview of Healthcare Regulations in Canada

Healthcare regulations in Canada have a profound impact on the virtual healthcare landscape. Since the introduction of the Canada Health Act in 1984, the government has implemented a series of stringent measures to ensure patient safety and service quality. The legislation promotes a strong emphasis on patient privacy, accessibility, and quality of care. It is expected that future amendments to the Act will further strengthen this regulatory framework.
