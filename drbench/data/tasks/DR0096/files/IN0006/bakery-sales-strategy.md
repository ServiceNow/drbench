# Bakery Sales Optimization Strategy

## Introduction

With the recent increase in food service sales, there is an opportunity for Lee’s Market to enhance bakery sales in its U.S. locations. This report outlines strategies to optimize in-store layouts and product displays to drive bakery sales. The proposed changes aim to increase bakery sales by 15% by the end of Q1 2026.

## In-Store Layout and Display Optimization

In our ongoing efforts to improve the shopping experience, we have introduced interactive displays in the produce section to educate customers about the origin of our fruits and vegetables. Additionally, we have rearranged the layout of the health and beauty section to make it more navigable. These changes have been positively received by our customers and we plan to continue experimenting with layout and display adjustments in other departments.

## Current Bakery Sales Performance Analysis

Our evaluation of promotional activity during Q2-Q3 2025 indicates some interesting findings regarding product placement strategies at Lee's Market's U.S. locations. Particularly, an evident uplift of 12% in cross-selling was observed when bakery items were positioned next to coffee or snack items. In contrast, the uplift was somewhat lower at 7% when bakery merchandise was displayed adjacent to dairy products. These figures highlight the potential of adjacency planning in enhancing impulsive purchases and underscore its value as a tangible driver of sales growth.

## Customer Behavior Analysis and Impact

Our recent customer surveys indicate a growing interest in locally sourced products. In response, we have increased the range of local products in our dairy and meat departments. This initiative has led to a 5% increase in sales in these departments over the last quarter. We also noticed a slight increase in the average customer spend per visit.

## Conclusion

By analyzing our current sales performance, optimizing in-store layouts and displays, and thoroughly understanding our customer behavior, we can strategically position our bakery products to boost sales. These changes aim to provide an enhanced shopping experience, leading to increased customer satisfaction and loyalty. Implementation should start immediately to meet our Q1 2026 goal. Further research and continuous monitoring will be essential for sustained growth.
