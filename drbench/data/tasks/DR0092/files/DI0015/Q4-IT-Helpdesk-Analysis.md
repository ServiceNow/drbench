# Q4 2025 IT Helpdesk Performance Analysis

## Introduction

This document provides an overview of the performance of MediConn's internal IT Helpdesk for the 4th quarter of 2025. The analysis focuses on ticket resolution rates, common issues encountered, and the impact of these on overall team performance. The intent is to provide insights that can be utilized to improve future IT support and streamline operations.

## Overview of IT Helpdesk Ticket Categories

At MediConn, IT helpdesk tickets are divided into five main categories: hardware issues, software crashes, network connectivity, system updates, and employee training. These categories help streamline our ticket resolution process by allowing our IT team to prioritize and handle issues based on their severity and impact on daily operations. In 2025, the majority of tickets were related to software crashes and system updates.

## Impact of Ticket Resolution on Workflow Efficiency

The efficiency of IT ticket resolution directly impacts workflow in our organization. Prolonged IT issues can disrupt operations, reduce productivity, and increase downtime. In 2023, a delay in resolving network connectivity issues resulted in a 12% decrease in productivity for that quarter.

## Q4 2025 Ticket Resolution Statistics

During Q4 of 2025, our IT helpdesk received a total of 1,250 tickets, a 7% increase from the previous quarter. The largest category of tickets was related to software crashes, which constituted 45% of the total tickets. The least common issue was related to hardware problems, with a share of only 8%.

## Conclusion

The Q4 2025 data highlights the efficiency of our IT Helpdesk in resolving internal technical issues, with a direct impact on overall workflow efficiency. It is recommended to focus on the most common ticket categories for future training and support enhancement. Continued monitoring of ticket resolution rates will provide valuable insights for ongoing improvement.
