# Enhancing Cybersecurity with Managed Security Services

## Introduction

As our business continues to expand, it is crucial that we strengthen our cybersecurity infrastructure to protect our valuable data assets. Leveraging the expertise of Managed Security Service Providers (MSSPs) could provide a viable solution to our internal resource constraints. This report will explore the benefits, potential improvements, and the strategic implementation of MSSPs within our organization.

## Managed Security Services: Benefits and Potential Improvements

MSSPs can provide a range of cybersecurity services, from threat monitoring to incident response and compliance support. They can also bring advanced technologies and specialized expertise that might be challenging for an in-house team to maintain. While we have considered partnering with an MSSP, we've been focusing on strengthening our internal capabilities, particularly through employee training and systems upgrades.

## Current Cybersecurity Landscape and In-House Challenges

In terms of the current cybersecurity landscape, the second quarter of 2025 posed significant challenges for our in-house team at Lee's Market. Given the high threat level in the retail sector, we were faced with multiple security incidents, particularly after regular working hours. In May alone, we received five such alerts, but our team could only address two before the commencement of the next workday, highlighting the resource constraints we were grappling with.

## Strategic Implementation of Managed Security Services

In implementing any new security measure, we must consider several factors. These include its impact on our existing infrastructure, the training needed for our staff, and the cost and ROI. For instance, when we introduced multi-factor authentication to our POS systems in Q2 2024, we conducted a thorough review of these aspects before proceeding.

## Conclusion

Adopting MSSPs can fortify our cybersecurity infrastructure while addressing resource constraints. This solution offers improved threat management, stronger compliance, and potentially enhances customer trust. Further exploration of MSSP options and a well-planned implementation strategy will be the next steps. A comprehensive training program will also be initiated to ensure our teams are well-versed with the new systems.
