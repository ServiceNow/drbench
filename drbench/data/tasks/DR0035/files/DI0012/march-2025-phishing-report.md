# Phishing Simulation Awareness: March 2025 Report

## Introduction

This document provides an overview of the phishing simulation awareness program implemented by our IT department in March 2025. It highlights the key components of the program, the engagement of employees, and the impact on our cybersecurity preparedness. As a leading retailer, ensuring the security of our internal systems and customer data is a top priority. This report will provide valuable insights on the effectiveness of our current measures and areas for future enhancement.

## Overview of Phishing Simulation Program

Lee's Market introduced a comprehensive Phishing Simulation Program in 2023. The program was designed to educate employees about the risks of phishing attacks and how to identify potential threats. Using a mix of interactive learning materials and simulated phishing attacks, the program aimed to build a strong security culture within the organization.

## Employee Participation and Engagement

Our employees' engagement with the Phishing Simulation Program has been commendable. As of December 2024, 97% of our workforce has completed the mandatory training. Furthermore, we have observed an increased interest in cybersecurity webinars and additional resources provided by our IT department.

## Impact on Cybersecurity Preparedness

Since the launch of the Phishing Simulation Program, our IT department has seen a 25% reduction in reported phishing incidents. This is a testament to the effectiveness of the program in enhancing our employees' ability to identify and report potential threats. We continue to refine the program based on the evolving cybersecurity landscape.

## Conclusion

The phishing simulation awareness program implemented in March 2025 has shown promise in strengthening our cybersecurity measures. Employee engagement and understanding of cybersecurity best practices have seen a notable increase. We recommend continuing this program and exploring advanced training modules to further equip our staff. As we expand our online and delivery services, investing in cybersecurity awareness and preparedness remains paramount.
