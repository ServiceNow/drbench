# Printer Access Control Implementation Review

## Introduction

This report reviews the printer access controls implemented in February 2025 by Jason Wong for Lee's Market. It aims to provide a clear understanding of the changes made, their implications, and potential next steps. This analysis will serve as a valuable resource for evaluating our current data security measures and ensuring we continue to protect sensitive company information.

## Overview of Printer Access Control Changes

In July 2024, we had a comprehensive audit of our printer access policies across all departments. The audit revealed that some employees were printing sensitive data without proper authorization. Consequently, we decided to reassess our printer access controls to ensure the secure handling of our company's information.

## Implications of New Printer Access Controls

The changes in printer access controls have had a significant impact on our operations. Employees now have to undergo a certain level of clearance to print sensitive documents. This has improved data security within the company and reduced wastage in the printing department by about 30% since March 2025.

## Recommendations for Future Printer Access Management

For future enhancements, we should consider integrating advanced technologies like biometric authentication for printer access. Additionally, implementing a company-wide training program to educate employees about the importance of print security could help maintain the integrity of our data. The next review for these recommendations is scheduled for August 2025.

## Conclusion

The printer access controls implemented by Jason Wong have significantly enhanced our data security. As we continue to expand and adapt to the modern retail landscape, maintaining these robust security measures will be crucial. The recommendations listed in this report provide a roadmap for future improvements. It is imperative to keep reviewing and updating our controls to ensure the continued security of our information.
