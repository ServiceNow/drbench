# Password Review Analysis - April 2025

## Introduction

This document presents the findings of the password review analysis conducted in April 2025. The goal of the review was to assess the security measures and compliance of shared password usage in various departments. This is essential in maintaining data integrity and preventing potential security breaches within our organization.

## Methodology of Shared Password Review

The shared password review process at Lee's Market is a rigorous one. It begins with the IT department running an audit across all network systems to identify shared login instances. Subsequently, a thorough analysis is conducted to ascertain the frequency of shared login usage, the departments involved, and the potential risks associated. This methodology was adopted in January 2025 and has been followed ever since.

## Detailed Findings from the April 2025 Review

The April 2025 shared password review showed a significant reduction in shared logins. The deli department, for instance, reduced its shared logins by 80%. Meanwhile, the operations department saw a 70% reduction, and the customer service team achieved a 50% reduction. The IT department credits this improvement to the enforcement of better password management policies.

## Implications and Recommendations for Password Policy

The April 2025 review findings suggest that our current password policy is effective, but there's room for improvement. Strengthening our two-factor authentication system and encouraging employees to routinely change their passwords could further minimize shared logins. Additionally, implementing a periodic password review and training program could reinforce best practices among our staff.

## Conclusion

The April 2025 password review revealed key areas that require attention to enhance our security measures. It is crucial to address these findings promptly to ensure the continued safety of our company's information. The recommendations provided should guide the implementation of improved practices. Continued password reviews and policy revisions are advisable for maintaining high security standards.
