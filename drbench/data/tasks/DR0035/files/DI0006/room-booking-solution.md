## Overview of Digital Transformation at Lee's Market

As part of our ongoing commitment to innovation, Lee's Market initiated a comprehensive digital transformation process in 2023. This multi-phase project aimed to streamline operations, enhance customer service, and improve inventory management. In the first phase, we successfully implemented an AI-powered inventory management system, reducing stock discrepancies by 20% and improving on-shelf availability by 35%.

## Key Features and Implementation of Room Booking Solution

The implementation of the digital room booking solution was a crucial part of our broader digital transformation strategy. This solution, introduced in 2024, was designed to improve meeting room utilization across all our corporate offices. It offered features such as real-time availability, automatic reminders, and easy-to-use mobile access. However, it did not include calendar integration.
