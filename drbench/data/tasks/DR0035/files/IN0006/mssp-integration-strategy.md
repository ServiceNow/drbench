# MSSPs Integration: Cybersecurity Enhancement Strategy

## Introduction

As Lee's Market grows and evolves to meet the needs of our diverse customer base, our digital infrastructure and cyber security measures must also advance. This report discusses the potential benefits of transitioning from in-house cybersecurity to Managed Security Service Providers (MSSPs) to address our resource constraints. We will explore the expected improvements in threat management, compliance, and customer trust. This shift aligns with our commitment to safeguarding our e-commerce platforms, customer loyalty programs, and supplier portals.

## Managed Security Services: Potential Benefits and ROI

The retail industry is experiencing a rise in cyber threats, with a 60% increase since 2020 according to the Cybersecurity in Retail 2024 Annual Report. This trend underlines the need for robust security measures. However, the cost and complexity of managing these threats in-house continue to rise, leading us to consider alternatives that may offer a better return on investment.

## Improving Customer Trust Through Enhanced Data Protection

In 2024, Lee's Market implemented a new data encryption policy for all customer transactions, which reduced the number of data breaches by 30%. Despite this improvement, 15% of customers expressed concern about data security following their purchases. As a result, we have launched a company-wide initiative to further enhance data protection and build customer trust.

## Current Cybersecurity Challenges and Resource Constraints

In the second quarter of 2025, Lee's Market experienced a cybersecurity incident that had a noticeable impact on the company's online business operations. This minor breach, although swiftly managed, created a ripple effect that led to a temporary decrease in our e-commerce activities. Specifically, there was about a six percent decline in online orders in the month following the incident. This serves as a stark reminder of the inherent vulnerability of our digital operations and the pressing need for a robust, scalable cybersecurity infrastructure to ensure business continuity and protect customer trust.

## Conclusion

Adopting MSSPs offers a solution to our current cybersecurity resource constraints, bringing potential advancements in threat management and compliance. This strategic shift also promises to boost customer trust through improved data protection. As the Information Security Manager, I recommend further exploration of MSSP options best suited to Lee's Market's needs, followed by a detailed implementation plan. Our commitment to cybersecurity is paramount in maintaining our reputation and customer confidence.
