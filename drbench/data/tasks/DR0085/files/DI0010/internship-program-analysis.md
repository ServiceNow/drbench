# 2021 Internship Program Analysis Report

## Introduction

This report provides a comprehensive review of the 2021 Internship Program at Elexion Automotive. We aim to understand the reach and engagement of our internship recruitment efforts, the quality and quantity of applications received, and the effectiveness of our selection process. This analysis is crucial for refining our future internship recruitment strategies to attract top talent in the EV industry.

## Analysis of Internship Recruitment Channels

Our recruitment channels for internships have been diverse, including online job boards, university career fairs, and referrals. Social media platforms like LinkedIn contributed to 35% of the applications received in 2020, showing a significant increase from 25% in 2019. The recruitment team is looking into partnerships with tech schools to widen our reach.

## Internship Application Statistics and Trends

Over the past five years, we've noticed that the applications for our engineering internships have been steadily increasing. This surge is tied to the growth in interest in the EV sector, with a 20% year-on-year increase in the number of applications. Our HR team continues to refine the selection process to ensure we're attracting top talent.

## Post-Selection Internship Program Evaluation

Our post-selection evaluation process has proved to be highly effective, with 85% of our interns rating their experience as excellent. The process includes regular feedback sessions, end-of-internship presentations, and a final review with the department head. We continue to refine this process to ensure the best experience for our interns.

## Conclusion

This year's internship program has provided valuable insights into our recruitment efforts and selection process. The data indicates areas for improvement and potential strategies for attracting a higher number of quality applications. We recommend refining our recruitment channels and selection process based on these findings. Continued evaluation of our internship program is crucial to ensure the attraction and retention of top talent in the evolving EV industry.
