# CARB ZEV 2025 Compliance Roadmap

## Introduction

The California Air Resources Board (CARB) has recently updated its Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) regulations, which will come into effect by 2025. These changes necessitate an in-depth review of our current compliance strategies. As the Competitive Intelligence Manager, I have analyzed these updates and their implications for Elexion Automotive. This document outlines our potential compliance gaps and suggests a roadmap to ensure our adherence to these new standards.

## Impact of CARB ZEV 2025 Updates on Elexion

The CARB ZEV 2025 updates will inevitably lead to changes in the EV industry. However, Elexion’s commitment to technology and innovation has consistently allowed us to anticipate and adapt to regulatory shifts. In 2020, we allocated 15% of our R&D budget to regulatory compliance research, which we expect to increase in the coming years.

## Review of Current ZEV Compliance Status

Our internal audit data indicates a potential area for improvement within our dealership network in relation to ZEV compliance. Notably, our Elexion models are not receiving the desired exposure in key areas of our partnered dealerships. In detail, less than half of these dealerships, about 42%, consistently position our models in the primary walk-in zones, where visibility is at its highest. This lack of front-lot exposure could affect our ability to meet the updated CARB ZEV 2025 reporting requirements, and adjustments to our dealership strategy may be necessary.

## Strategic Recommendations for CARB ZEV 2025 Compliance

It is imperative for Elexion to maintain its competitive edge by staying abreast of the regulatory landscape. Our team should continue leveraging our in-house data analytics capabilities to forecast potential regulatory changes. Regular updates and discussions with Elexion's legal and engineering teams are also recommended to ensure a holistic approach towards compliance.

## Conclusion

In light of the updated CARB ZEV 2025 regulations, Elexion Automotive needs to address identified compliance gaps promptly. The strategic recommendations outlined herein provide a clear path towards achieving compliance. It is imperative that we take immediate action to align our production and sales strategies with these new standards. Our ability to adapt will not only ensure regulatory compliance but also strengthen our competitive position in the EV market.
