# April 2024 Sustainability Training Report

## Introduction

As part of our commitment to sustainability and zero-emission transportation, Elexion Automotive has rolled out an internal sustainability training program for all employees. This report provides insights into the participation and completion rates of the program during April 2024. It also highlights the impacts of the program on our workforce and operations, fostering a culture of sustainability in line with our corporate mission.

## Impact of Sustainability Training on Employee Engagement

Our internal surveys indicate that employees who undergo sustainability training typically experience a 12% increase in job satisfaction. This training also promotes a sense of shared responsibility for the environment, leading to a 9% improvement in teamwork metrics across various departments. These positive impacts extend beyond the workplace, with 55% of trained employees reporting increased personal efforts towards sustainability.

## Operational Improvements from Sustainability Training

Post-training, Elexion witnessed a 15% decline in energy consumption across our facilities in May 2024. The waste management department reported a 7% increase in recycling rates, reflecting a more conscious use of resources. Furthermore, the production department implemented new procedures that reduced material waste by 10% in Q2 2024.

## April 2024 Sustainability Training Completion Rates

The sustainability training program is a core part of Elexion's commitment to environmental responsibility. In April 2022 and 2023, we saw a steady increase in the number of employees voluntarily enrolling in the program. The HR department has streamlined the registration process, making it easier for employees to sign up for the training.

## Conclusion

Elexion's internal sustainability training program has made significant strides in educating our workforce on sustainable practices and has resulted in notable operational improvements. The completion rates for April 2024 indicate a strong commitment from our team to incorporate these practices into their roles. We must continue leveraging this momentum to foster a stronger culture of sustainability across all operations and departments. Further data-driven insights will be shared quarterly to track our progress.
