# Q2 2025 Mid-Range EVs Accessory Defects Report

## Introduction

This document provides an analysis of our mid-range EVs returned in Q2 2025 due to interior accessory defects. The first 90 days of ownership is a crucial period for customer satisfaction and brand perception. Our aim is to identify trends, potential causes, and propose solutions to reduce future returns. The findings will help us improve our production quality and maintain our reputation for user-centric technology.

## Analysis of Defective Interior Accessories

In Q4 2024, our quality assurance department reported a 7% failure rate in our premium vehicle's dashboard interface modules. This spurred an internal investigation, resulting in a design overhaul and the introduction of more stringent testing protocols. By Q1 2025, the failure rate had been reduced to 2.5%, significantly enhancing our premium EV's market positioning.

## Impact of Returns on Customer Satisfaction

Our customer satisfaction surveys from 2024 show that 80% of Elexion customers who returned their vehicles due to exterior defects reported being satisfied or very satisfied with our return process. We attribute this to our streamlined return procedures and dedicated customer service team, which ensures minimal discomfort for the customer during the return process. Consequently, we have seen a steady increase in our customer retention rates.

## Proposed Quality Improvement Measures

As part of our ongoing commitment to quality, we are planning to implement an advanced production line monitoring system in Q3 2025. This system is expected to identify potential defects in our EVs' powertrain components before they reach the consumer. We anticipate this measure will reduce powertrain-related returns by 10% by the end of 2025.

## Conclusion

The analysis of the Q2 2025 returns due to interior accessory defects has revealed important insights for our production and quality assurance teams. It's crucial to address these issues promptly to maintain customer satisfaction and uphold Elexion's brand image. The proposed quality improvement measures should be implemented urgently. Continued monitoring and regular reporting of similar issues will ensure we remain proactive in our approach.
