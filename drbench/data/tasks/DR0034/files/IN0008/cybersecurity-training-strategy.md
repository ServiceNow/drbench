# Enhancing Cybersecurity Training: A Strategic Approach

## Introduction

As the digital landscape evolves, so does the threat of cyberattacks, posing significant risk to Lee's Market's operations and financial stability. In light of our limited IT resources, it's critical to strengthen our cybersecurity training efforts to reduce potential threats. This document outlines a strategic approach to augment our current initiatives, with the aim to better equip our employees against potential cyber threats by Q3 2027.

## Current State of Cybersecurity Training and Awareness

In the first quarter of 2025, our analysis revealed a significant financial impact associated with cybersecurity disruptions. On average, each hour of operational downtime, largely a result of cyberattacks, led to a loss of approximately $30,000 in sales revenue. This underscores the urgent need to fortify our cybersecurity training initiatives and elevate staff awareness. By addressing this critical issue, we aim to minimize operational disruptions and protect our financial stability.

## Identifying Opportunities for Training Enhancement

In 2024, we initiated a cybersecurity risk assessment to identify gaps in our training initiatives. We discovered that while our POS systems and e-commerce platforms were well-protected, our supplier portals had a significantly lower level of security. Consequently, we've prioritized further training for employees managing these portals.

## Implementing Improved Cybersecurity Measures and Training

Following the 2024 risk assessment, we implemented new protocols for supplier portal management and reinforced existing cybersecurity measures. We also introduced a requirement for periodic password changes and two-factor authentication. These steps have led to a 20% reduction in detected threats on these portals in Q1 2025.

## Conclusion

With the outlined approach, we aim to strengthen our cybersecurity training despite our limited IT resources. By assessing our current state, identifying opportunities, and implementing enhanced measures, we can mitigate risks of cyberattacks. Our next steps involve detailing a comprehensive plan for each subsection and presenting it to key stakeholders for approval.
