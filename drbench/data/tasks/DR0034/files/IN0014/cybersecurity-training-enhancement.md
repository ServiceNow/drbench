# Enhancing Employee Cybersecurity Training Program

## Introduction

As the Information Security Manager at Lee's Market, I'm acutely aware of the increasing cyber threats facing our business operations. With our limited IT resources, it's imperative that we invest wisely to strengthen our defenses, particularly through employee training. This document outlines a strategic plan to enhance our cybersecurity training program, aiming to reduce the risk of retail cyberattacks that may lead to operation disruptions and financial loss.

## Exploring Advanced Cybersecurity Training Techniques

AI-driven cybersecurity training programs are emerging as a popular trend in the retail sector. These systems use machine learning to personalize training content for each user, improving engagement and retention. While we have not yet adopted this technology, it's something we're closely monitoring.

## Current State Analysis of Cybersecurity Training

Upon closely examining the cybersecurity landscape of Lee's Market in 2025, it was observed that there was a notable surge in cyber threats by nearly 27% during periods of promotional activities. This escalation can be attributed to the increased onboarding of new employees and heightened online operations. It underscores the critical need to bolster our security training programs, particularly during such periods of increased activity. This will aid in minimizing potential cyber risks that could disrupt our operations and lead to financial losses.

## Resource Allocation and Program Implementation Timeline

Our team plans to allocate 35% of the IT budget to cybersecurity training for the fiscal year 2025-2026. We anticipate full implementation of the new security measures by Q2 2026, with ongoing training sessions for staff to be scheduled quarterly thereafter.

## Conclusion

The enhancement of our employee cybersecurity training is a critical investment to safeguard our digital and operational infrastructure. The proposed plan leverages advanced training techniques and outlines a feasible implementation timeline within our resource constraints. It is recommended that all stakeholders review and provide feedback on the plan to ensure its effectiveness and alignment with our strategic objectives. A strong, well-trained workforce is our best defense against the growing threat of cyberattacks.
