# 2025 Hardware Refresh Project Report

## Introduction

This report outlines the key aspects of the hardware refresh project piloted in February 2025. As Information Security Manager, my role was to ensure the project's alignment with our security protocols and standards. The purpose of this document is to provide stakeholders with a comprehensive overview of the project, its impact on our information security infrastructure, and the lessons learned.

## Project Scope and Objectives

In April 2025, we initiated a project to upgrade our CRM system across all Lee's Market locations. The objective was to improve customer data management, enhance service delivery, and increase customer retention by 15%. The project involved all customer-facing departments and was spearheaded by the Director of Customer Experience.

## Implementation and Security Measures

During the CRM upgrade, we incorporated advanced security measures, such as two-factor authentication and data encryption, to protect our customers' information. The IT department also introduced a new security policy in March 2025, mandating regular system audits and vulnerability assessments to ensure continued data security and integrity.

## Challenges and Lessons Learned

The CRM upgrade project faced minor setbacks due to software compatibility issues and user adaptation challenges. Through this project, we learned the importance of comprehensive pre-implementation testing and employee training. These insights will guide our approach in future tech-related initiatives.

## Conclusion

The hardware refresh project marked a significant step in improving our IT infrastructure while maintaining a robust security posture. Despite some challenges, successful mitigation strategies ensured project success. Based on the lessons learned, future recommendations include increased staff training and further collaboration with vendors. These measures will help to ensure smoother implementations in future projects.
