# Enhancing Internal Newsletter Privacy Controls

## Introduction

As an Information Security Manager at Lee's Market, I am responsible for ensuring the safe handling and distribution of company information. Recently, we have made substantial improvements to our internal newsletter privacy controls. This document outlines the changes made in January 2025, detailing the process, benefits, and future plans to uphold information security within our organization.

## Existing Privacy Control Measures and Limitations

Before the recent updates, our privacy control measures involved multiple stages of authentication for accessing internal communications. However, these processes had some limitations, particularly in terms of efficiency. For instance, the IT department reported in the second quarter of 2024 that in one month alone, over 2000 hours were spent on resolving access-related issues. This was primarily due to the complex nature of the existing privacy control system.

## Implementing Enhanced Privacy Controls

In October 2024, a cross-functional team comprising representatives from IT, HR, and Communications was formed to revamp our internal communication privacy controls. The team decided to use a tiered access model as the foundation of the new system. The model would allow customized access to different types of announcements based on the user's role in the company. This approach was expected to streamline internal communications while enhancing privacy.

## Assessing the Impact of Improved Privacy Measures

Following the implementation of the new privacy controls in November 2024, the company saw a significant reduction in access-related issues. The IT department reported a 60% decrease in the number of hours spent on resolving such issues. Moreover, an internal survey conducted in December 2024 showed a 70% improvement in employees' satisfaction with the internal communication system.

## Conclusion

The recent enhancements to our internal newsletter's privacy controls have significantly improved our ability to protect sensitive information. This not only upholds our commitment to information security but also fosters trust within our organization. We will continue to monitor these controls and make necessary adjustments to ensure optimal security. Your continued support in adhering to these changes is vital to our collective success.
