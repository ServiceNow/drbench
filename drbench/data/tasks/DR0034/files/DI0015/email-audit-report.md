# Email Account Audit Report - February 2025

## Introduction

As part of our continuing commitment to information security, we conducted an audit of unused company email accounts in February 2025. The goal was to identify potential vulnerabilities and ensure the efficient use of our IT resources. This report outlines the findings, recommendations, and the processes implemented during the audit.

## Overview of Email Usage Policy

Lee's Market has a stringent email usage policy in place, emphasizing the importance of maintaining the privacy and security of all employees' information. This policy, last updated on July 10, 2024, is mandatory for all employees across departments to adhere to. It includes critical components such as proper email etiquette, secure data transfer, and guidelines for handling confidential information.

## Audit Process and Findings

Our annual IT audits, performed by the Information Security team, have been instrumental in ensuring our systems' robustness. The January 2025 audit, focused on our inventory management system, found minor discrepancies that were promptly addressed. The audit's effectiveness was proven by the subsequent 15% reduction in discrepancies in the following quarter.

## Recommendations and Future Measures

In light of the need for continuous improvement in our IT infrastructure, we have slated a comprehensive audit of our Customer Relationship Management system for April 2025. This will involve a thorough review of user access levels, data integrity, and the system's overall performance. The aim is to identify potential security risks and opportunities for optimization.

## Conclusion

The audit has revealed a number of unused email accounts that pose a security risk and consume unnecessary resources. It is recommended that steps be taken to deactivate these accounts and reinforce adherence to the Email Usage Policy. Future audits will be conducted regularly to maintain a high level of information security. Thank you for your attention to this important matter.
