# Digital Asset Consolidation: Software Licenses Review

## Introduction

As part of Elexion Automotive's ongoing digital transformation, our asset management team has embarked on a comprehensive review of all software licenses. The aim is to identify redundant licenses and consolidate them under our digital asset registry. This initiative will not only optimize our resource utilization but also streamline our digital operations.

## Analysis of Current Software Licenses Utilization

As of Q3 2025, Elexion Automotive’s IT department has recorded a 15% increase in the usage of design software licenses compared to the same period last year. This rise is primarily attributed to the surge in the number of engineers involved in our new electric vehicle project. More interestingly, our data analytics software utilization has doubled, which mirrors the company's increased focus on data-driven decision-making.

## Identification and Consolidation of Redundant Licenses

Historically, Elexion Automotive has been diligent in optimizing its software licenses. Back in 2023, we successfully consolidated 50 redundant licenses from our design department, resulting in an annual cost saving of 10%. Our policy of regular audits has been instrumental in maintaining this efficiency, and it continues to be a core part of our IT governance strategy.

## Impact of Consolidation on Operational Efficiency

The benefits of license consolidation extend beyond cost savings. In 2024, we observed a 20% increase in operational efficiency in the HR department after the consolidation of their HRM software licenses. This led to streamlined HR processes and improved employee satisfaction rates, underlining the importance of effective asset management.

## Conclusion

The consolidation of redundant software licenses will significantly enhance our operational efficiency and reduce unnecessary expenditure. It will further align our digital practices with our sustainability goals. The asset management team recommends a periodic review of our software licenses to maintain an optimized digital asset registry.
