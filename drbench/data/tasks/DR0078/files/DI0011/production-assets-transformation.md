# Digital Transformation of Production Assets

## Introduction

This document outlines the progress of Elexion Automotive's ongoing digitization and calibration of production assets for AI-integrated risk-audit readiness assessments. The report covers the scope of assets, digitization execution, and our strategic approach towards this initiative. It aims to provide detailed insights into the progress and expected completion timelines of this critical project, crucial to our business's overall digital transformation strategy.

## Scope of Production Assets for Digitization

As of 2021, Elexion Automotive has over 2000 production assets distributed across our manufacturing plants. These assets are integral to the production of our premium and mid-range electric vehicles. We have invested heavily in the latest manufacturing technology, with a particular focus on automation and data collection systems.

## Execution Strategy for Asset Digitization

Our strategy for digitization is focused on incremental implementation. We began the process in Q1 2020, starting with our most critical assets. Our strategy also emphasizes training our staff to ensure they are comfortable with the digitization process and can troubleshoot any issues that arise.

## AI-Integration and Risk-Audit Readiness

Elexion Automotive is at the forefront of AI integration in the automotive industry. We have successfully incorporated AI in various areas of our operations, from supply chain management to quality control. AI's ability to accurately predict and manage risks has significantly improved our audit readiness.

## Conclusion

Elexion Automotive continues to make significant strides in achieving its digital transformation goals with a particular emphasis on production assets. This report has outlined our scope, execution strategy, and the integration of AI for risk-audit readiness. As we progress, our focus will remain on minimizing disruptions while maximizing efficiency and compliance. Stakeholders will receive periodic updates as we approach the targeted completion date of August 5, 2026.
