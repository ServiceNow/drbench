# Customer Interaction Report Q1 2025

## Introduction

This report provides an analysis of customer interactions, specifically focusing on requests for human assistance due to technological challenges experienced during Q1 2025. The purpose of this review is to better understand our customers' needs and areas for improvement in our service offerings, particularly in relation to our technological interfaces. The insights gleaned from this analysis will aid in enhancing our customer service strategy and overall customer experience.

## Analysis of Customer Support Requests

In the first quarter of 2023, Lee's Market observed a 7% increase in customer support requests compared to the same period in 2022. Interestingly, the majority of these requests were related to product inquiries and not technical issues. Our customer support team has been proactive in responding to these queries, maintaining an average response time of less than 24 hours.

## Insights on Technological Challenges Faced by Customers

With the advent of our new mobile app in December 2024, some customers faced challenges in navigating the platform. Consequently, we saw an uptick in calls to our customer service hotline. The majority of these calls were related to login issues and not understanding the app's layout and functionality.

## Customer Requests for Human Agent Interactions

In 2024, we saw a general trend of customers preferring human interactions for complex inquiries. Specifically, about 40% of customers chose to speak with a human agent when discussing specialty items. This trend highlights the ongoing importance of human touch in retail customer service.

## Conclusion

The analysis of customer interactions has highlighted the need to continually refine and simplify our technological interfaces to meet the needs of our diverse customer base. There is a notable segment of customers who prefer or necessitate human assistance in their interactions with Lee's Market. Moving forward, a balanced focus on improving our digital interfaces and maintaining high-quality human customer service will be crucial in ensuring a positive customer experience. Recommendations include enhanced training for customer support agents and improved user experience for our online platforms.
