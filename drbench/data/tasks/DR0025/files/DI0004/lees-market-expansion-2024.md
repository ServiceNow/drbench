## Strategic Planning for Store Expansion

In 2023, our strategic planning committee identified potential growth areas for store expansion. Our digital marketing team has been working hard on a campaign targeting these regions to prepare for potential store openings.

We have been employing innovative technologies to streamline operations in our new stores. In 2023, we implemented a new inventory management system which reduced restocking times by 20%. This system also improved our waste management by reducing perishable goods spoilage by 15%.

## Customer Reach and Impact

Our customer base grew by 7% in 2023, particularly among young urban professionals seeking authentic Asian ingredients. Our online sales also increased by 12% due to our improved online shopping platform and delivery services. We have been consistently working to enhance our customer engagement and improve their shopping experience.

Lee's Market has made significant strides in its store expansion plan in 2024. Our strategic planning, operational efficiency, and customer reach have been instrumental in this growth.
