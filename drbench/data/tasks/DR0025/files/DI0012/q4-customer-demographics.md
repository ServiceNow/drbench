# Q4 2024 Customer Demographics Analysis

## Introduction

This document provides an in-depth analysis of the demographic characteristics of our walk-in shoppers during Q4 2024. The primary focus is on understanding the age distribution of our customers but it also provides a broader view of our clientele. The insights in this report will guide our marketing strategies and help us refine our retail offerings. This report is essential for decision-making processes related to product assortment, marketing, and store layout.

## Age Distribution of Q4 2024 Shoppers

During Q4 2024, there was a notable increase in the number of new shoppers at Lee's Market. Our data indicates a 15% rise in first-time customers compared to Q3 2024. Interestingly, the increase was consistent across our physical and digital platforms, suggesting a broad appeal of our offerings during this period.

## Quarterly Comparison of Shopper Demographics

When comparing Q4 2024 to the previous quarter, we observed a significant increase in the number of customers from suburban areas. There was a 25% growth in the suburban customer base, contrasting the 5% growth from urban areas. Our specialized Asian product line is seemingly appealing to a more diverse demographic.

## Trends in Asian Food Consumption by Age Group

The consumption patterns of Asian food products varied across different age groups in 2024. Young adults showed a marked preference for ready-to-eat meals, whereas older customers tended to purchase raw ingredients for home cooking. However, our premium tea range was universally popular.

## Conclusion

Understanding our customer demographics, particularly the age distribution, will help us tailor our product offerings and marketing strategies. The Q4 2024 data reveals a specific pattern that we need to monitor in subsequent quarters. Based on the findings, we should consider diversifying our product assortment to cater to the dominant age group. We will continue to reinforce our commitment to providing high-quality Asian groceries and household items across all age categories.
