# Lee's Market Customer Satisfaction Analysis

## Introduction

This document aims to provide a comprehensive review of Lee's Market's customer satisfaction performance last year. It will cover different facets of our customer-focused operations, highlighting key metrics and areas for improvement. This analysis is crucial to align our strategies with customer expectations and drive our growth in competitive markets.

## Evaluation of Customer Support Services

In 2024, Lee's Market's customer support services handled over 150,000 inquiries, with an average response time of less than 2 hours. The Customer Support team, consisting of 200 dedicated individuals, successfully resolved 93% of all customer inquiries on the first contact. Furthermore, our department launched a new training program in July to better equip our staff in handling complex customer inquiries.

## Insights from Customer Satisfaction Survey

The Customer Satisfaction Survey conducted in 2024 focused on understanding customer preferences regarding our product range. Approximately 80% of the respondents liked the variety of Asian groceries we offer, and 75% of customers appreciated our vast collection of household items. However, some customers suggested expanding our seafood section.

## Impact of Online and Delivery Services

Our online and delivery services have seen a significant increase in usage over the past year. In 2024, online orders increased by 30% and delivery services saw a 45% increase in usage. We also rolled out a new mobile app in September 2024, which received over 20,000 downloads within the first month.

## Conclusion

Our customer satisfaction score, especially as gathered from the annual survey, plays a significant role in our overall business performance. It is imperative that we continue to focus on enhancing our customer services, both in-store and online. Future initiatives should be strongly aligned with our customers' expectations and preferences. By doing so, we can ensure a sustained increase in customer satisfaction and loyalty, solidifying our market presence and driving revenues.
