# Q3 Internal Communication Analysis

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing efforts to transparently share important company updates and developments, we've been distributing internal newsletters to all Elexion Automotive employees. This report analyzes the distribution and engagement levels of these newsletters in Q3. It will provide useful insights into our internal communication strategy effectiveness and areas for potential improvement.

## Newsletter Distribution Frequency and Reach

Elexion Automotive's newsletter is distributed bi-weekly to our network of over 1,900 employees across North America. In an effort to improve our reach, we've started including the newsletter in our onboarding package for new hires. In Q2, this initiative increased our newsletter reach by 7%.

## Employee Engagement with Newsletters

During Q3, the employee engagement with our newsletters was commendable, with an average open rate of 76%. The engineering department showed the highest engagement at 84%, while the marketing department's rate was at 70%. We attribute this high engagement to our enriched content and interactive design.

## Content Analysis of Q3 Newsletters

Our Q3 newsletters focused heavily on the advancements in our EV technology and safety features. We also provided updates on our new manufacturing facility in Nevada. The customer testimonials section was particularly popular, seeing a 12% increase in readership from Q2.

## Conclusion

The Q3 internal newsletters effectively reached a significant proportion of our workforce, with varying engagement levels. The data suggests that certain content types generate more engagement than others. Moving forward, we should focus on enhancing the content that resonates most with employees to foster a more engaged and informed workforce. Continuous tracking and analysis of our internal newsletters will ensure we keep improving our communication strategy.
