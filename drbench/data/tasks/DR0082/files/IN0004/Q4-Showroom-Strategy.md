# Enhancing EV Showroom Presence Q4 Strategy

## Introduction

As Elexion Automotive continues to innovate within the EV market, there is a growing need to strengthen our retail dealership presence. This document outlines a comprehensive strategy to increase our EV showroom visibility in Q4. It will provide insights to enable us to maintain a competitive edge and effectively respond to market dynamics. The strategic approach detailed in this report aims to elevate our brand presence and drive sales.

## Analyzing Current Dealership Performance

One of the key areas we identified for improvement pertains to the traceability of our battery units. Our analysis revealed that approximately one in five VIN batches lacks the necessary time-stamp verification in our battery traceability reports. This gap in our traceability procedures could potentially impact customer trust and dealership perception, ultimately influencing our showroom presence. Therefore, addressing this issue should form an integral part of our strategy for strengthening our retail dealership presence in Q4.

## Optimal Locations for Increased Showroom Visibility

Our existing showrooms are strategically located in high-traffic areas. In 2020, the Los Angeles dealership reported a footfall of approximately 10,000 potential customers per month, while the New York dealership reported 8,000. The Chicago dealership, which is located in a less densely populated area, reported a footfall of 6,000.

## Innovative In-Showroom Marketing Strategies

In Q3 of 2021, we introduced a virtual reality experience in our Seattle showroom. This allowed visitors to virtually test drive our vehicles, with 85% of participants rating the experience positively. Additionally, our 'Refer and Earn' program has been successful in driving repeat purchases, with a participation rate of 20% among existing customers.

## Conclusion

Enhancing showroom visibility can significantly boost our Q4 sales and brand perception. The data-driven approach to identifying optimal locations, combined with innovative in-showroom marketing strategies, will ensure we remain competitive. It is recommended that we move forward with implementing these strategies as outlined. Continued market analysis and adjustment of our strategies will be crucial to our sustained growth in the EV market.
