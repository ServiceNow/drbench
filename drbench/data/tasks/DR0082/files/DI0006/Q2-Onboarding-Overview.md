# Q2 HR Onboarding Overview - Elexion Automotive

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing commitment to ensuring a smooth transition for new hires at Elexion Automotive, we have compiled a report providing an overview of the HR onboarding sessions carried out during Q2. This report aims to provide insights into the number of sessions held, their effectiveness, and how they align with our overall recruitment strategy. As we continue to grow and innovate in the EV industry, it is increasingly important to maintain a robust onboarding process for our talent pool.

## Overview of Q2 Recruitment Initiatives

In Q2, Elexion Automotive's recruitment team launched two major hiring campaigns targeting electrical engineers and automotive designers. This initiative resulted in the receipt of over 1,500 applications and the successful hiring of 35 new team members. These efforts have significantly bolstered the production and design departments, preparing us for the competitive Q3 we anticipate.

## Evaluation of Q2 Onboarding Sessions Efficacy

The efficacy of our onboarding sessions was evaluated through post-training surveys and a 30-day check-in with new hires. Feedback indicated a high level of satisfaction with the training content and the transition process. We also noted an increase in productivity within the first month of employment, suggesting the onboarding process is effective.

## Alignment of Onboarding Practices with Company Goals

Our onboarding practices have been designed with company goals in mind. The training material focuses on Elexion's commitment to technological innovation and sustainable practices. By embedding these values at the start of employment, we have seen a strong alignment with our mission across all departments.

## Conclusion

Our Q2 recruitment and onboarding strategies continue to support our growth objectives as a leading EV manufacturer. It's essential to consistently evaluate and enhance these practices to ensure we are attracting and retaining the best talent in the industry. Moving forward, we will continue to refine our onboarding processes to better align with our company goals. Your feedback and support are appreciated as we strive to make Elexion Automotive an employer of choice.
