# Enhancing Dealer Relationships via Automotive Cloud

## Introduction

## Dealer Performance Analysis and Enhancement Strategies

Our approach to dealer performance at Elexion Automotive has been traditionally rooted in quarterly assessments, largely hinged on data self-reported by our dealer network. However, as of early 2025, we have observed a significant data quality issue with approximately 34% of performance metrics either being incomplete or delayed, as per the estimations of Emily Lee, our Customer Relationship Manager. This has the potential to impact our understanding and management of dealer performance, ultimately affecting our overall sales and service delivery. It is essential that we address this challenge promptly and strategically, potentially leveraging tools such as Automotive Cloud's Dealer Performance Management system, to enhance data accuracy and timeliness.

## Sustaining Direct-to-Government Sales Channel Efficiency

To maintain the efficiency of our direct-to-government sales channel, we've established a dedicated government sales team. This team, made up of experienced sales professionals, is responsible for maintaining relationships with government buyers and ensuring that Elexion's vehicles meet the specific requirements of government fleets. As of 2022, our government sales team has successfully secured contracts with 35 state and local governments across North America.
