# Optimizing Dealer Relations via Automotive Cloud

## Introduction

In line with Elexion Automotive's strategic objectives, a thoughtful utilization of Automotive Cloud's dealer performance management tools can help us solidify our relationships with our North American dealer network. Simultaneously, we need to ensure the maintenance of our robust direct-to-government sales channel. This document delves into the potential strategies and considerations for achieving these parallel goals, keeping in mind our commitment to sustainability and zero-emission transportation.

## Balancing Dealer Network and Direct Sales

As of December 2022, 40% of our total sales were direct-to-government, while the remaining 60% were through our dealer network. However, the ratio has been fluctuating over the past few years due to changes in government policies and market dynamics. We've been continually adjusting our strategies to optimize this balance and achieve our sales targets.

## Understanding Dealer Performance Metrics

In the final quarter of 2024, our team conducted an in-depth analysis of the time required to rectify dealer disputes and performance problems. It was observed that we were spending an average of six weeks per case, a duration substantially extended by scattered documentation and a lack of uniform performance contracts across the network. This fragmentation in both information and contractual guidelines has proven to be a significant roadblock in efficient dealer relationship management. Therefore, standardizing performance contracts and centralizing documentation could be a viable solution to expedite issue resolution and enhance dealer relations.

## Implementing Automotive Cloud for Enhanced Relationship Management

The transition to Automotive Cloud has resulted in a significant improvement in our communication with dealers. Since the implementation in July 2023, we've seen an average response time reduction of 20%, leading to improved satisfaction rates among our dealer partners. However, the Sales Department is still working to fully leverage the system's capabilities to maximize its benefits.

## Conclusion

By effectively leveraging Automotive Cloud's capabilities, Elexion can strengthen dealer relations and maintain our direct-to-government sales, thereby ensuring a comprehensive market reach. It's essential that we balance these channels and utilize the right metrics to assess dealer performance. Moving forward, the recommended action is to form a cross-functional team to initiate the implementation of Automotive Cloud tools and devise strategies for dealer engagement and sales channel maintenance.
