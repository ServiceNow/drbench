# Sales Training Assessment Report 2025

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing commitment to equip our sales force with the necessary skills to promote our electric vehicles, we've conducted a comprehensive review of training requests made by Elexion Automotive sales representatives in 2025. This report will delve into the number of those requests, identify the areas of most significant concern, and propose potential solutions.

## Analysis of Training Requests

In 2024, the Human Resources Department reported a notable increase in training requests in the manufacturing department. It was revealed that 75% of the employees requested additional training in new machinery operation and safety. The data suggests an increasing need to update our training programs in line with technological advancement.

## Identifying Training Gaps

Our R&D department has identified a training gap in understanding the latest battery technology. This gap has led to a 15% increase in production mishaps in 2024. Efforts are underway to develop a comprehensive training module to address this issue.

## Proposed Solutions for Training Enhancement

To ensure effective use of our in-car technology by customers, we propose a new training program for our customer service representatives. This program will include hands-on sessions on our latest EV models and will be rolled out in Q1 2026.

## Conclusion

The data indicates an increased request for additional training by our sales representatives. It is crucial that we address these needs promptly, developing comprehensive training programs that close these gaps and enhance our team's capabilities. Our next steps should involve the creation of focused training modules and soliciting feedback on their effectiveness. Let's continue to invest in our team's growth, to ensure Elexion Automotive remains at the forefront of the electric vehicle industry.
