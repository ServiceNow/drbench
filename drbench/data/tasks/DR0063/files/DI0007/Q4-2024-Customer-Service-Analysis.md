# Q4 2024 Customer Service Interaction Analysis

## Introduction

This document provides an in-depth evaluation of customer service interactions within Elexion Automotive during Q4 2024. We focus on the platform-wise distribution of these interactions, mainly emphasizing on the mobile application. This analysis will guide us in understanding customer preferences, which is crucial for service improvements and strategic decision making.

## Overview of Q4 2024 Customer Service Interactions

In Q4 2024, our customer service team handled a total of 48,000 customer interactions across all platforms. This volume represented a 10% increase compared to the previous quarter, maintaining the steady growth we have seen throughout the year. A large portion of these interactions were related to general inquiries about our latest models and service appointments.

## Analysis of Mobile App-Based Customer Interactions

Our mobile app has proven to be an effective tool for enhancing our customer experience. In Q4 2024, the app was updated with a new feature to schedule service appointments, which was used by 15% of app users. The app's user-friendly interface and real-time updates have contributed to its positive reception among our customers.

## Comparison with Previous Quarters' Interaction Trends

Compared to previous quarters in 2024, Q4 saw a significant shift in the nature of customer service interactions. There was an increase in queries related to our new models, and a decline in queries about warranty and service plans. This trend aligns with our product launch schedule and the end-of-year sales drive.

## Conclusion

The document provides a comprehensive analysis of customer interaction channels used in Q4 2024, with a particular focus on the mobile app. It also compares these findings with previous quarters to predict future trends. Based on this information, we can make informed decisions to enhance customer service and drive user engagement. We recommend further research into app-based interactions to enhance the customer experience.
