# Food Safety Compliance Audit Report

## Introduction

In light of increasing regulatory scrutiny and consumer demand for safety, we have conducted a comprehensive audit of our food safety compliance certifications. This report provides an overview of our current certifications, the procedural adherence to these standards, and the impact on our operations. Our aim is to ensure that Lee's Market not only meets but exceeds the necessary food safety and handling standards.

## Overview of Existing Compliance Certifications

At Lee's Market, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety for our products. Our commitment is reflected in the various certifications we hold, such as the Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) certification and the Quality Assurance International (QAI) Organic Certification. These certifications underscore our dedication to providing our customers with top-tier Asian groceries and household items.

## Assessment of Procedural Adherence

In 2020, our internal audit team conducted a thorough examination of departmental adherence to established procedures. The findings showed a 98% compliance rate across all departments, a testament to our dedication to consistency and best practices. Future audits are scheduled to ensure continued adherence and identify areas for improvement.

## Operational Impact of Compliance Standards

The implementation of our compliance standards has led to an operational improvement of 15% in the last fiscal year. These improvements have streamlined our supply chain processes, reduced waste, and improved overall efficiency. We are committed to continually refining our operations in line with industry best practices.

## Conclusion

Our audit reveals that Lee's Market holds the necessary food safety compliance certifications and maintains rigorous adherence to these standards. To ensure ongoing compliance, we recommend regular audits and staff training. As we continue to expand our online and in-store services, maintaining these standards will not only meet regulatory requirements but also bolster our reputation for quality and safety.
