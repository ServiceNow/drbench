# 2026 Market Share Expansion Strategy

## Introduction

As we navigate the evolving retail landscape, the increasing trend of value-seeking consumers necessitates a strategic approach for Lee's Market. This document outlines our proposed strategy for gaining market share within our target Asian communities and diverse urban centers by Q4 2026. The focus is on enhancing our private label and prepared foods offerings to deliver culturally authentic and affordable options. The aim is to capitalize on market trends and propel Lee's Market's growth in the face of increasing competition.

## Expansion of Private Label and Prepared Foods

Lee's Market is committed to expanding its private label and prepared foods offerings. In 2024, the company launched 15 new private label products, resulting in a 9% increase in sales within the category. Also, our prepared foods department reported a satisfactory 7% growth in the same year.

## Metrics for Measuring Market Share Growth

Lee's Market utilizes a combination of key performance indicators (KPIs) to track market share growth. These include overall sales, customer retention rates, and customer lifetime value. In 2024, we saw a 5% increase in customer retention and a 3% increase in customer lifetime value.

## Assessment of Current Market Position

An evaluation of our recent sales data reveals a notable shift in customer preferences. Our cultural private label range, which is central to our value proposition, has seen a significant uptick in sales. Internal reports depict that this range constituted 12% of the total sales across all Lee's Market locations in the third quarter of 2025, marking an increment of 3% when compared to the previous quarter. This surge underlines the growing acceptance and popularity of our private label offerings amongst our target Asian community and diverse urban markets.

## Conclusion

By focusing on our private label and prepared foods, we can leverage the value-seeking trend among consumers to gain market share. The outlined metrics will enable us to accurately track and measure our progress. It is important that we constantly reassess our market position and adjust our strategies as necessary. This proactive approach will help us maintain our competitive edge while meeting the changing needs of our customers.
