# 2024 Employee Training Overview: Lee's Market

## Introduction

In order to maintain our commitment to excellent customer service, Lee's Market has prioritized the delivery of comprehensive training sessions for all store staff members throughout 2024. This report presents an overview of staff participation in these sessions, providing insight into the operational effectiveness of our training initiatives. Furthermore, it examines related areas that contribute to our overall customer experience strategy.

## Store Staff Training Participation

In 2023, 70% of our store staff participated in our annual product knowledge training, a 10% increase from 2022. The training involved a deep dive into our Asian product range, focusing on their origins and uses. The training also highlighted the importance of maintaining quality standards for perishable goods.

## Impact of Training on Customer Satisfaction

Following our 2025 customer satisfaction survey, we noted a 15% increase in positive feedback related to our in-store service. This improvement has been attributed to our various training initiatives, including product knowledge and health and safety. Customers specifically mentioned the staff's knowledge of our product range in their positive reviews.

## Future Training Initiatives and Recommendations

Looking ahead to 2026, we are planning to introduce a new training module focusing on digital literacy. This training will equip our store staff with the necessary skills to handle digital transactions and assist customers with our online shopping platform. We anticipate that this training will further enhance our customer service delivery across all channels.

## Conclusion

The dedication of our store staff to their professional development has been clear in the high participation rates in our training sessions throughout 2024. There is a positive correlation between these training efforts and our customer satisfaction ratings, reinforcing the importance of continuous learning in our organization. Looking ahead, we will be refining our training programs based on feedback and performance data, to further enhance our customer service experience.
