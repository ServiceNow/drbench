# Lee's Market Advertising Performance 2025

## Introduction

This report provides an analysis of Lee's Market advertising reach in 2025. It covers both digital and print media platforms, demonstrating the overall impact of our advertising efforts. The goal is to understand our reach and use this data to inform future advertising strategies.

## Analysis of Digital Media Advertising

In 2025, the digital marketing team at Lee's Market successfully optimized their search engine marketing strategy, leading to a significant increase in website traffic. There was a 20% increase in click-through rates on our Google Ads campaign. Additionally, the company's social media platforms recorded a 15% growth in followers, particularly on Instagram and Facebook.

## Insight into Print Media Reach

Print media advertising continued to play a fundamental role in Lee's Market's advertising strategy in 2025. We distributed flyers to approximately 200,000 households weekly in the Richmond area. Our advertising team also placed ads in several local newspapers and magazines.

## Comparison of Advertising Channels

In 2025, Lee's Market allocated 60% of its advertising budget to digital media, while the remaining 40% was invested in traditional advertising channels. This decision was based on the increasing trend of online shopping and the need to reach a larger, more diverse audience. The performance of each channel was evaluated based on the sales generated.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, our advertising reach in 2025 was significant across both digital and print media. The data suggests a need for a balanced approach in our advertising strategies. Future campaigns should continue to leverage both channels to maximize our market reach. This report will serve as a benchmark for assessing future advertising performance.
