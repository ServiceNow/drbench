During the 2025 fiscal year, Lee's Market invested significantly in updating their self-checkout infrastructure. Our self-checkout systems are designed to handle a high volume of transactions. In Q3 2025, they processed an average of 10,000 transactions per day. We also noticed a 20% increase in transaction volume during the holiday season, consistent with previous years' trends.

The customer satisfaction survey conducted in Q4 2025 revealed that 85% of customers felt the self-checkout process was straightforward and user-friendly. Additionally, our support team received fewer queries related to self-checkout issues, indicating an improvement in user experience.

The report highlights the importance of self-checkout efficiency in enhancing customer experience. It provides a detailed analysis of transaction processing times and details areas for improvement.
