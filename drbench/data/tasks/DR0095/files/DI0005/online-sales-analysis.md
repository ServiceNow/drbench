# 2025 Online Sales and Delivery Analysis

## Introduction

This report provides an in-depth analysis of Lee's Market's 2025 online and home delivery sales performance. As a rapidly evolving area of our business, understanding these metrics allows us to make data-driven decisions for future growth. In particular, the report will focus on revenue generation from these channels. We believe this information is critical to our strategic planning and operational efficiency.

## Analysis of 2025 Online Sales Trends

In 2025, Lee's Market saw an increase in customer engagement with our online platform. We noticed that customers were particularly drawn to our fresh produce section, with approximately 35% of all online orders including items from this category. Additionally, the average number of items per transaction increased by 2% compared to 2024.

## Performance of Home Delivery Service in 2025

Our home delivery service performed exceptionally well in 2025, with a customer satisfaction rating of 89%. The average delivery time was reduced by 15 minutes compared to 2024, and we saw a 5% decrease in delivery-related complaints. The most popular delivery window was between 5pm and 7pm.

## Contribution of Online and Delivery to Total Revenue

In 2025, we made significant investments in enhancing our online platform and delivery services. We upgraded our website's user interface and expanded our delivery fleet by 20%. We also added a new 'express delivery' option for an additional fee, which proved popular among customers.

## Conclusion

The analysis has shown significant growth in our online and home delivery sectors, contributing a considerable percentage to our total 2025 revenue. To continue this upward trend, we recommend further investment in our online platforms and delivery infrastructure. Moreover, regular performance reviews should be conducted to ensure sustained profitability. Our ability to adapt and innovate in these areas will be crucial to our future success.
