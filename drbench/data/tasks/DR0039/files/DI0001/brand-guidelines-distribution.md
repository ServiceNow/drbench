# Q1 2024 Brand Guidelines Distribution Analysis

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing commitment to promote Lee's Market brand consistently, we've updated and distributed our brand guidelines to various media outlets in Q1 2024. This document provides an analysis of that distribution, examining the reach and potential impact of our updated brand image. It aims to inform internal stakeholders about the extent of our brand dissemination and the potential influence on our market position.

## Analysis of Media Outlet Categories

Our media outreach efforts have traditionally been divided into three categories: print, digital, and broadcast. Print outlets, comprising of newspapers and magazines, accounted for 35% of our media contacts in 2023. Digital outlets, including blogs and online news platforms, made up 50% while broadcast outlets, such as TV and radio stations, comprised the remaining 15%.

## Brand Guidelines Distribution Overview

The distribution of our brand guidelines was managed by our Communications and Marketing department. Following a revision, a comprehensive set of new guidelines was developed and shared with our media contacts. This process ensures consistency in how our brand is represented across different platforms.

## Implications of Updated Brand Guidelines

The updated brand guidelines were intended to streamline our brand representation and bolster our market presence. By maintaining a consistent brand image, we aim to enhance customer recognition and trust. The guidelines were specially designed to reflect our commitment to blending traditional Asian flavors with modern retail experiences.

## Conclusion

Our updated brand guidelines have been successfully disseminated to a substantial number of media outlets, ensuring our brand image is accurately portrayed. The distribution covered a broad range of media outlet categories, which should enhance our visibility in diverse markets. As we continue to monitor the impact of this initiative, it is crucial to consider refinements to our distribution strategy to amplify our brand's reach and influence.
