# Public Relations Performance Analysis 2023

## Introduction

As we strive to strengthen Lee's Market's brand presence and visibility, it is essential to review and analyze our public relations efforts. This report provides an in-depth review of our PR team's performance in 2023, with a specific focus on feature articles secured. It also draws comparisons from the previous year to provide a comprehensive view of our progress and the effectiveness of our strategies.

## 2023 PR Strategy and Implementation

The PR strategy for 2023 focused heavily on influencer marketing, leveraging popular food bloggers to showcase our unique products. Our team collaborated with the marketing department to ensure consistent messaging across all platforms. We also implemented a new PR software to streamline our processes.

## Comparative Analysis: 2022 vs 2023 Media Features

In 2022, we saw a shift in our media engagement, with a higher focus on radio and podcast interviews. However, in 2023 we shifted our gaze towards digital media platforms, with an emphasis on YouTube collaborations and sponsored content on relevant websites.

## Influence of Media Features on Brand Perception

Media features have greatly influenced our brand perception, establishing Lee's Market as a trusted source for high-quality Asian groceries. Survey data shows a 15% increase in brand trust among our target demographic, attributed to our media engagement.

## Conclusion

The results of our analysis provide valuable insights into the effectiveness of our PR strategies and the impact of feature articles on our brand's perception. While we have made significant progress, there are areas for improvement and opportunities for growth. Moving forward, we should strategize to capitalize on these opportunities and continually assess our PR efforts to ensure they align with our overall business goals and contribute positively to our brand image.
