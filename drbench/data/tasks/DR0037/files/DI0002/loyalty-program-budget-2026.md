# 2026 Loyalty Program Communication Budget Analysis

## Introduction

This report provides an in-depth review of the projected budget allocation for the 2026 Lee's Market Loyalty Program's customer engagement communication strategies. As we strive to offer a modern retail experience that blends traditional Asian flavors with contemporary convenience, understanding our budget allocation is crucial. This analysis will help us effectively plan our resources, ensuring the success of our loyalty program's launch.

## Loyalty Program Overview and Objectives

The new loyalty program at Lee's Market is designed to enhance customer retention and promote increased spending per visit. The program offers tiered rewards based on purchase levels, with benefits including exclusive discounts, early access to sales, and free delivery options. Our objective is to sign up 25% of our regular customers by the end of Q2 2026 and increase average transaction value by 10% by Q4 2026.

## Analysis of Previous Customer Engagement Initiatives

Previous customer engagement initiatives at Lee's Market have shown promising results. For instance, our 2024 in-store sampling campaign led to a 15% increase in sales of featured products. Similarly, the digital coupon initiative in 2025 boosted online sales by 12% and increased the average basket size by 8%.

## Projected Communication Strategy Budget for 2026

In 2026, we anticipate a higher overall marketing budget due to our plans for expansion into new metropolitan areas. While we plan to allocate a substantial portion of this budget towards online and digital advertising, an increased focus will also be placed on in-store promotions and local partnerships. Specific figures will be finalized in our Q4 2025 executive board meeting.

The proposed budget for our 2026 Loyalty Program's customer engagement communication strategies aligns with our company's objectives and past experiences.
