# Q2 2025 Customer Satisfaction and Product Strategy

## Introduction

This report will delve into the impact of the Q2 2025 Customer Satisfaction Survey, conducted by Sofia Patel, on our product assortment strategy at Lee's Market. It is significant to understand how customer feedback and preferences can shape our product lineup, and in turn, our overall retail strategy. This analysis focuses on the correlation between customer satisfaction and product offerings, thus providing insights for future strategic planning.

## Summary of Q2 2025 Customer Satisfaction Survey

The Q2 2025 customer satisfaction survey was conducted to understand shifting consumer preferences. A total of 3000 shoppers participated in the survey, providing valuable insights into their shopping habits. The survey primarily focused on the in-store shopping experience, customer service quality, and store cleanliness.

## Evaluation of Current Product Assortment Strategy

Our current product assortment strategy has been designed to cater to a diverse customer base. It is based on historical sales data and trends observed in the Asian grocery market. The strategy is regularly revised to accommodate seasonal changes and customer feedback.

## Insights for Future Product Strategy from Survey

The insights derived from the Q2 2025 survey will be instrumental in shaping future product strategies. Certain areas such as packaging and branding have been highlighted for improvements. In addition, customer preferences for organic products have increased, which is a point to consider for our future strategy.

## Conclusion

The Q2 2025 Customer Satisfaction Survey has provided valuable insights that have influenced and will continue to shape our product assortment strategy at Lee's Market. By incorporating customer feedback into our product offerings, we can enhance customer satisfaction and drive sales. As we move forward, we must continue to value customer input in our strategic planning to ensure our product assortment aligns with customer preferences. The application of these insights will guide us in maintaining our reputation as a provider of high-quality Asian groceries and household items.
