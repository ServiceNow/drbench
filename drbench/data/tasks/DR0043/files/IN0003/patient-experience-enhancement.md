# Enhancing Patient Experience: MediConn's Competitive Advantage

A review of consumer behaviour in June 2025 indicated a promising trend for MediConn Solutions, with an enhancement in the patient retention rate by a tenth. This is a testament to our commitment to improving patient experience, which is vital in the telehealth industry. The upward trajectory in retention rates underscores the effectiveness of our services and their alignment with the needs of our patients. By leveraging these insights, we can further refine our strategies to stay ahead of the competition and maintain a high level of patient satisfaction, trust, and retention.

## Patient Behaviour and Perception Towards Virtual Healthcare

There has been a notable shift in patients' acceptance of virtual healthcare services over the past few years. A survey conducted in 2024 revealed that 70% of patients now feel comfortable discussing their health concerns via virtual platforms, a significant increase from 45% in 2021. The convenience and time-saving aspects of telehealth were highlighted as key factors for this change in perception.
