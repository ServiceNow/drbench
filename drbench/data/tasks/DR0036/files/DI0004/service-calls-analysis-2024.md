# Elexion Automotive Service Calls Analysis 2024

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing efforts to understand and improve customer experience, we have conducted a comprehensive analysis of service calls for Elexion Automotive in the year 2024. This report aims to shed light on the volume, nature, and distribution of these calls, providing valuable insights for our customer service, product development, and marketing teams. The findings will help us identify pain points, areas for improvement, and potential opportunities for enhancing customer satisfaction and loyalty.

## Distribution of Service Calls by Vehicle Model

Our data for the fiscal year 2024 indicates a differential distribution of service calls across Elexion's vehicle models. The premium range, which comprises 40% of our vehicle portfolio, accounted for 35% of the total service calls. The mid-range models, on the other hand, represented the remaining 65% of the service calls.

## Analysis of Service Call Reasons

Our analysis indicates that the majority of service calls in 2024 were related to hardware malfunctions and software glitches. Approximately 52% of the calls were due to hardware issues, 30% were software-related, and the remaining 18% were miscellaneous. This data assists us in identifying areas for improvement in our vehicle design and manufacturing process.

## Monthly Breakdown of Service Calls

In 2024, we observed a seasonal pattern in the monthly distribution of service calls. The number of service calls peaked during the summer months, with July being the highest. Conversely, the winter months showed a significant drop, with the fewest calls recorded in December.

## Conclusion

The analysis of 2024's service calls provides key insights into our customer service operations and customer experience. From understanding the distribution of calls based on models to the reasons behind these calls, we can identify areas that need attention. Furthermore, the monthly breakdown of calls can help us anticipate when demand for service may peak. This data will guide us in improving our services, allocating resources more effectively, and ultimately driving customer satisfaction.
