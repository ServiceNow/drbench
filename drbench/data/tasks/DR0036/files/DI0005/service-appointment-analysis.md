# Service Appointment Turnaround Analysis

## An Overview of Service Request Trends

In 2024, Elexion experienced a 15% increase in service requests, totaling 18,000 appointments for the year. Interestingly, we noticed a seasonal trend with increased service requests in the colder months. We attribute this to the additional strain on electric vehicle battery performance due to cold weather conditions.
