# Q1 2026 EV Strategy Workshop Participation Analysis

## Introduction

This report presents an analysis of Elexion Automotive's internal Electric Vehicle (EV) sales strategy workshops for Q1 2026. The emphasis is on gauging employee participation across our North American offices. The objective is to identify offices with the highest engagement levels and to understand the factors contributing to their success.

## Workshop Attendance Analysis by Office

In Q4 2025, Elexion's Detroit office reported the highest attendance at the EV design workshops, with 80% of employees participating. These workshops focused on the foundational principles of EV design, resulting in a substantial increase in inter-departmental collaborations. Interestingly, the Toronto office, despite having a smaller team, showed significant workshop participation with 70% engagement.

## Correlation between Employee Engagement and Workshop Participation

There appears to be a correlation between the level of employee engagement and workshop participation across Elexion's offices. A company survey in 2025 found that offices with higher employee engagement scores had increased attendance at internal training sessions and workshops. This data, however, does not specify the type of workshops attended.

## Key Success Factors in High-Participation Offices

For the high-participation offices in the EV tech innovation workshops in Q2 2025, key success factors were identified as strong leadership, flexible scheduling, and effective communication. The prominence of these factors suggests that they might be valuable for increasing workshop participation in other Elexion offices too.

## Conclusion

The report highlights the offices with the highest participation in our EV strategy workshops, along with the engagement factors that contributed to their success. These insights will be instrumental in driving future internal strategy sessions and increasing overall participation. We recommend that other offices adopt identified best practices to enhance employee engagement in strategic initiatives. Further, a more in-depth study on the correlation between office environment and workshop participation is suggested.
