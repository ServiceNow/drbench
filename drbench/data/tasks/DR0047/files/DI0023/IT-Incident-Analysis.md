# MediConn IT Incident Resolution Analysis

## Introduction

This report provides an overview of IT incidents at MediConn Solutions, with a focus on those handled by our Vancouver-based IT team. Our goal is to understand the effectiveness of our incident resolution processes and identify areas for improvement. This analysis will guide our strategic decisions and resource allocation within the IT department.

## Overview of IT Incidents at MediConn

In 2024, MediConn saw an overall increase of 20% in IT incidents, due to a surge in the usage of our virtual healthcare services. The majority of these incidents were related to connectivity issues and platform integrations. Our IT teams across various regions worked diligently to rectify these problems to ensure optimal service delivery.

## Performance of Vancouver-based IT Team

Our Vancouver-based IT team has consistently demonstrated high levels of proficiency in managing system upgrades and maintenance. In 2025, they successfully spearheaded the roll-out of a major security update across all our platforms, improving system stability by 35%. Their expertise in handling complex technical issues is commendable.

## Impact of IT Incidents on Service Delivery

IT incidents can have significant implications on our service delivery. In 2025, we estimated a 15% decrease in patient satisfaction following major IT incidents. The proactive management of these incidents is crucial to maintain the quality of our virtual healthcare services and ensure patient satisfaction.

## Conclusion

Our Vancouver IT team has shown commendable performance in handling and resolving remote care IT incidents. However, there is always room for improvement, especially in the areas identified in this report. Moving forward, we should focus on enhancing our incident management processes, and investing in additional resources for our Vancouver team. This will not only improve our service delivery but also contribute to our overall business growth.
