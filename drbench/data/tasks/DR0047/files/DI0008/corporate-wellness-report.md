# Corporate Wellness Program Subscription Report

## Introduction

This report provides an overview of MediConn Solutions' corporate wellness program subscriptions for the month of August 2025. The objective is to understand the traction and acceptance of our services among businesses. This document will be instrumental in shaping future marketing strategies and identifying potential areas for product enhancement.

## Breakdown of New Corporate Subscriptions

In July 2025, MediConn Solutions registered a surge in new corporate subscriptions. This was largely attributed to the launch of our upgraded telemedicine platform which saw a 20% increase in subscriptions over the previous month. Most of these new subscribers were small to medium-sized enterprises within the telecommunication and IT industry.

## Analysis of Subscription Cancellation Trends

Our retention team has noted a decline in the cancellation rate for the second quarter of 2025, which dropped to 7%. This has been attributed to the implementation of feedback systems and enhanced customer service. Most cancellations came from the manufacturing sector, citing lack of use as the main reason for discontinuing.

## Geographical Distribution of Corporate Subscriptions

Our corporate subscriptions are predominantly concentrated in Ontario and Quebec, accounting for approximately 60% of our total subscriptions. Western Canada, particularly Alberta and British Columbia, are rapidly growing markets, contributing to 30% of new subscriptions in the first half of 2025. The remaining 10% of subscriptions are distributed across the other provinces and territories.

## Conclusion

The trends and insights in this report provide a comprehensive understanding of the adoption of our corporate wellness program. The findings suggest areas where attention may be required to boost our market presence. Based on these results, we recommend devising targeted marketing campaigns and considering product improvements based on the geographical distribution of our clients.
