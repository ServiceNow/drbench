# Employee Mental Wellness Initiatives Report

## Introduction

In line with MediConn Solutions' commitment to employee well-being, we have been conducting mental health wellness seminars. This report provides an overview of our mental health initiatives for the month of April 2025, including the number of seminars conducted, employee participation rates, and feedback received.

## April 2025 Wellness Seminar Overview

In April 2025, we introduced a new module on stress management in our wellness seminars. The module was created in collaboration with renowned psychologists and was tailored to address the unique challenges faced by healthcare professionals in the virtual domain. The module was made available to all our employees across various departments.

## Employee Participation in Mental Health Seminars

We saw a 10% increase in employee participation in our wellness seminars in April 2025 compared to the previous month. The participation rate was particularly high among our customer service representatives and software developers. We believe that the new stress management module was a significant factor contributing to this increase.

## Feedback and Response to Wellness Seminars

Post-seminar feedback from our employees in April 2025 was overwhelmingly positive, with 90% of participants rating the seminars as 'very useful'. The new stress management module was particularly well received. We will be incorporating the feedback received into future seminar planning.

## Conclusion

Our wellness seminars played a pivotal role in promoting mental health among our employees in April 2025. The participation rates and feedback received indicate a positive reception. Moving forward, we will continue to refine our programs based on employee feedback and incorporate more diverse topics to cater to all aspects of mental health.
