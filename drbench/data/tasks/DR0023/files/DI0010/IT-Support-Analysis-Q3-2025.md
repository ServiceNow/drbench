# Q3 2025 IT Support Analysis - Email Signatures

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing analysis of IT support efficiency, we have conducted an in-depth study into the time taken for employees to set up new email signatures across devices during Q3 2025. This document outlines our findings, providing insights into the types of IT requests that were most time-consuming and identifying areas for potential improvement. The intent is to use this information to streamline our IT support processes and improve overall productivity.

## Analysis of IT Support Request Types

During Q3 2025, our IT support team handled a wide range of requests. Systems maintenance tasks accounted for 35% of all requests, while 28% were related to network connectivity issues. Hardware and software installation and repairs made up the remaining 37% of IT support workload.

## Insights into Email Signature Set-Up Process

The process of setting up email signatures involves several steps. Initially, employees must access their email settings and locate the signature option. Once found, they can input their desired signature, which typically includes their name, job title, and contact information. The signature is then saved and applied to all outgoing emails.

## Comparison of Device-Specific Email Set-up Timings

In Q3 2025, we observed that the time taken to set up emails varied significantly across different devices. Desktop computers had the quickest email setup at an average of 10 minutes, followed by laptops at 12 minutes. Tablets and smartphones, on the other hand, took an average of 15 and 18 minutes respectively.

## Conclusion

The report provides valuable insights into the IT support processes, specifically the setting up of email signatures across devices. As we continue to grow and onboard new employees, it will be crucial to address these time-consuming tasks to improve efficiency. Recommendations for process improvements based on these findings will be formulated and shared with the relevant teams. Continued tracking and analysis of IT support requests will remain a priority to ensure ongoing improvements.
