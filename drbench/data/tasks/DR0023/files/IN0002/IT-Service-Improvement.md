# Improving IT Service Capabilities at Lee's Market

## Introduction

As Lee's Market continues to grow, there's a clear need to enhance our IT self-service abilities. This will reduce our dependence on the service desk, speed up resolution times, and significantly improve the experience for both employees and customers. This document outlines our strategic approach to achieve these objectives by Q3 2026.

## Understanding and Addressing User Experience Challenges

We conducted a survey among our retail staff in Q2 2025 which revealed that 45% found our current inventory management system complex to navigate. Furthermore, 22% of our online customers had difficulties tracking their deliveries due to a rather non-intuitive interface. These are areas we are continually striving to improve.

## Leveraging Advanced Technologies for IT Self-Service

In late 2024, we implemented a new cloud-based CRM system across our retail and online operations. This has streamlined our sales and marketing efforts, contributing to a 15% increase in customer retention in 2025. We are exploring similar technologies for other areas of our business.

## Evaluating Current IT Infrastructure and Dependencies

An assessment of our Q3 2025 IT service logs underscores a critical bottleneck in our online ordering system. Our data shows that employees spend an average of 2.5 hours per incident handling errors in this system. This elongated resolution time can be primarily attributed to inadequate self-service tools and guidelines. This inefficiency is a clear representation of the opportunities we have for enhancing our IT self-service capabilities, which could lead to reduced service desk workload and faster resolution times.

## Conclusion

The outlined strategies will guide our efforts to optimize IT self-service capabilities and reduce service desk dependency. Implementing these initiatives will enhance both employee productivity and customer satisfaction. Going forward, it is crucial to monitor progress, gather feedback, and make necessary adjustments. This will ensure that we meet our target by Q3 2026.
