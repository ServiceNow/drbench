# Q2 2025 IT Customer Request Analysis

## Introduction

As part of our continuous efforts to understand and cater to our customer needs, we have conducted an in-depth analysis of IT requests related to our online recipe platform. Specifically, we focused on requests originating from Delaware during Q2 2025, which asked for the implementation of a feature suggesting side dishes based on selected main dishes. This document provides an overview of these customer requests, and presents a comprehensive analysis of their implications on our IT operations and overall customer satisfaction.

## Overview of Q2 2025 Delaware IT Requests

In Q2 2025, Delaware IT department handled 5200 customer requests, a 15% increase from the previous quarter. The majority of these requests were related to navigating the online shopping platform and understanding the new features introduced. The IT team responded to these requests within an average of 1.5 business days, maintaining our standard response time despite the increased workload.

## Impact of Recipe Automation on Customer Experience

The implementation of recipe automation has generally been well received by our customers. Our post-purchase surveys indicate an overall customer satisfaction rate of 85% with the new feature. Furthermore, it has led to a 10% increase in the sales of ingredients related to suggested side dishes, indicating customers are utilizing the feature and purchasing suggested items.

## Optimization Strategies for IT Request Handling

To better handle the increasing volume of IT requests, we have introduced an AI-driven triage system in Q2 2025. The new system has improved our first response time by 20% and has allowed us to prioritize critical issues. Additionally, we have initiated bi-weekly training sessions for our customer support team to better understand and address the evolving needs of our customers.

## Conclusion

Based on our analysis, it is evident that customers in Delaware are seeking enhanced automation features on our recipe page. Understanding and addressing these needs can further enhance our customer experience and engagement. As a next step, we recommend prioritizing the development of the requested feature in our upcoming IT roadmap. Furthermore, we should continue our efforts to optimize our IT request handling process to ensure prompt and efficient responses to our valued customers.
