# Q2 2024 After-Sales Service Evaluation

## Introduction

This document reviews our customer satisfaction with Elexion Automotive's after-sales service for the second quarter of 2024. The assessment is based on the external survey conducted in July 2024. The aim is to understand customer feedback and identify areas of improvement to maintain our reputation for premium customer service in the electric vehicles market.

## Overview of After-Sales Service Operations

Elexion Automotive's after-sales service operations have expanded considerably since 2020. The service team, now consisting of around 250 dedicated technicians, handles an estimated 3000 vehicle services per month. We ensure the compliance of our services with national and international standards, fortifying our commitment to customer service excellence.

## Summary of Customer Feedback & Satisfaction

In 2023, Elexion Automotive received feedback from over 50,000 customers. The feedback highlighted the efficiency of our service operations and the quality of vehicle maintenance. We also noted a 20% increase in feedback submission compared to the previous year, reflecting the growing engagement of our customer base.

## After-Sales Service Improvement Initiatives

Several initiatives were launched in 2023 to enhance our after-sales service. These included a new training program for our technicians and an upgraded customer feedback system. These changes have led to a 15% decrease in service-related complaints since their implementation.

## Conclusion

Our Q2 2024 customer satisfaction grades reflect the quality of our after-sales service. While the results are encouraging, there are areas that require our attention for improvement. The feedback collected will guide our strategy for service improvement initiatives. We remain committed to providing exceptional service to our customers and ensuring their satisfaction with our electric vehicles.
