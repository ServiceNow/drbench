# Dealer Satisfaction Survey: Elexion Automotive Analysis

## Introduction

As a part of our ongoing commitment to maintain strong relationships with our dealers, we conducted a survey to gauge their satisfaction and identify areas for improvement. This report provides an analysis of the survey results, focusing on the dealers' overall perception of their relationship with Elexion Automotive. Our aim is to utilize this data to improve our dealer support strategies and further enhance dealer satisfaction.

## Analysis of Dealer Support Services

Elexion Automotive has enhanced its Dealer Support Services since Q1 2022, with a dedicated team to facilitate vehicle order management, warranty claims, and marketing support. The company has reported a 15% decrease in response time for dealer queries, with a 20% increase in the resolution rate. This has resulted in an improved operational efficiency of the overall dealer network.

## Overview of Dealership Profitability with Elexion

In 2023, Elexion Automotive dealerships reported an average gross profit margin of 18%, slightly above the industry average of 15%. This profitability was driven by robust sales of Elexion's mid-range electric vehicles and efficient supply chain management. The company's focus on sustainability and technology also contributed to this performance.

## Assessment of Dealer Relationship Satisfaction

Elexion Automotive revamped its dealer engagement practices in early 2024, expanding its Dealer Advisory Board and introducing dealer-focused roundtable discussions. These initiatives are aimed at fostering transparency and open communication, enhancing the dealer experience with Elexion. Further measures are planned for 2025 to strengthen this relationship.

## Conclusion

The survey results provide valuable insight into the dealers' satisfaction and their relationship with Elexion Automotive. While the majority of dealers rate their relationship with us positively, there is room for improvement in certain aspects of dealer support services. Our next steps should involve addressing these areas and further strengthening our dealer relationships to ensure mutually profitable partnerships. We will continue to monitor dealer satisfaction and make necessary adjustments to our strategies.
