# Direct-to-Customer Sales Transition Strategy

## Introduction

As Elexion Automotive considers a shift from dealership franchising to a direct-to-customer sales model, it's crucial we understand potential benefits and challenges of such transition. This report examines the potential impact on our sales operations, outlines strategies for capturing value, and discusses how we can drive higher margins in 2027.

## Impact on Existing Sales Infrastructure

As we evaluate the potential transition from dealership franchising to direct-to-customer sales models, it's important to note our market research conducted in July 2024. Our findings revealed a significant preference among potential EV buyers for a more transparent, negotiation-free pricing model. Specifically, 68% of these consumers expressed a desire for this approach. By eliminating the dealership middlemen, we could cater to this consumer preference, potentially driving higher sales volumes and improved profit margins by reducing unnecessary markups.

## Strategies for Customer Acquisition and Retention

In mid-2023, we launched a series of customer loyalty programs to boost customer retention rates. Through our in-depth customer profiling, we targeted specific demographics with personalized offers and incentives. Our customer acquisition strategies have largely been focused on attracting first-time electric vehicle buyers through extensive marketing campaigns and promotional events.

## Margin Optimization through Direct Sales Model

Historically, our margins have been heavily influenced by production costs and dealership markups. We've implemented several cost-reduction measures in our manufacturing process to improve overall margins. Additionally, we've negotiated more favorable terms with our dealership partners to reduce their markups and improve our revenue share.

## Conclusion

The transition to a direct-to-customer sales model presents significant opportunities for Elexion Automotive to drive sales and increase margins. It's vital that we strategically leverage our sales infrastructure, prioritize customer acquisition and retention, and optimize our pricing strategies. As we move forward, we should develop a comprehensive plan to ensure a smooth and beneficial transition.
