# Q1 2025 Knowledge Management Strategic Overview

In Q1 2025, Lee's Market launched another successful initiative to strengthen supplier relations. In 2025, Lee's Market invested substantially in enhancing the in-store digital experience. The customer service department at Lee's Market experienced an improvement in their service quality metrics in Q1 2025. Implementation of a new CRM system resulted in a 12% decrease in customer complaints and a 9% increase in first-call resolution rates. These figures indicate a positive correlation between technology adoption and staff performance.

## Conclusion

The strategic priorities of our Knowledge Management team during Q1 2025 have been directed towards enhancing technological applications, initiating knowledge management initiatives, and improving staff performance.
