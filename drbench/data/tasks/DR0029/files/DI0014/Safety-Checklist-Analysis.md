# H2 2024 vs H1 2025 Safety Checklist Completion Analysis

## Introduction

This report presents a detailed analysis of the time taken by employees in our Cooking and Baking division to complete and submit the weekly safety and health checklist. The periods under review are the second half of 2024 (H2 2024) and the first half of 2025 (H1 2025). The analysis aims to identify any significant changes, trends, or anomalies that could impact our operations and safety standards.

## Overview of Safety Checklist Compliance Trends

Over the years, Lee's Market has seen a steady uptick in the compliance rate for safety checklists across all our divisions. In 2023, for instance, we achieved a 95% compliance rate, a notable increase from the 90% recorded in 2022. This improvement was largely driven by our concerted efforts to streamline processes and enhance employee training programs.

## Detailed Comparison of H2 2024 and H1 2025 Completion Times

While we don't have the exact figures at hand, we have noticed a general trend towards quicker checklist completion times in various divisions. For instance, the Meat and Seafood division saw a reduction in their average completion times between H2 2024 and H1 2025. Similarly, the Fresh Produce division also recorded a decrease in their average completion times over the same period.

## Impact of Checklist Completion Times on Operational Efficiency

It's important to note that faster checklist completion times do not necessarily equate to higher operational efficiency. For instance, our Dairy division reported the quickest average checklist completion times in 2024 but also had the highest number of safety incidents. This indicates that rushing through safety checklists can sometimes compromise the very safety standards they are designed to uphold.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, the comparison of checklist completion times between H2 2024 and H1 2025 reveals essential insights into our operational efficiency and safety compliance. These insights will guide us in making necessary adjustments to ensure optimal performance and maintain our high safety standards. It is recommended that the findings of this report be disseminated to all relevant stakeholders for appropriate action. Moving forward, we should continue to monitor and analyze these metrics to ensure continued operational excellence.
