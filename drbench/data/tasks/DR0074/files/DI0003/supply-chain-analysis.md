# Elexion's Supply Chain Analysis for Battery Components

## Introduction

As Elexion Automotive continues to prioritize sustainability and cross-border compliance, understanding the source of our battery components becomes critical. This report analyzes the proportion of our supply chain originating from North American suppliers. The findings will help us assess our supply chain's alignment with our corporate goals and identify potential areas for improvement.

## Overview of Elexion's Battery Component Suppliers

Elexion Automotive's battery component suppliers are a diverse group of companies hailing from various parts of the globe. They include lithium, nickel, and cobalt material providers, each with distinct operational processes and compliance standards. As of 2023, we have established partnerships with 25 major suppliers, reflecting a 20% increase from the previous fiscal year.

## Analysis of North American Supply Chain Contribution

North American suppliers have been instrumental in Elexion Automotive's journey, offering a variety of critical components necessary for our electric vehicles. The established relationships with these suppliers have allowed us to maintain a steady flow of materials, ensuring the smooth operation of our production line. In 2023, we added 3 new North American suppliers to our network.

## Impact of Supply Chain on Sustainability Goals

The sustainability of our supply chain is a primary concern for Elexion Automotive. With our commitment to zero-emission transportation, we have integrated a comprehensive sustainability assessment into our supplier selection process. By 2024, we aim to reduce our carbon emissions from supply chain activities by 30%.

## Conclusion

The data indicates a substantial percentage of our battery components come from North American suppliers. While this aligns with our cross-border compliance goals, we must continue to evaluate these relationships in the context of our sustainability agenda. Our next steps should involve further investigation into the environmental practices of our suppliers. Additionally, we should explore potential partnerships with other North American suppliers who align with our sustainability goals.
