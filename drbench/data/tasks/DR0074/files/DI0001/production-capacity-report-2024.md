# Annual Production Capacity Report 2024

## Introduction

This report provides an analysis of Elexion Automotive's total production capacity across our North American manufacturing facilities in 2024. It offers a comparison with 2023 capacity, providing a clear understanding of how our production capabilities have evolved. This analysis is important for our strategic planning, operational efficiency, and financial forecasting.

## 2024 Production Capacity by Vehicle Model

In 2024, Elexion Automotive introduced the 'Elexion S' model, well-received for its advanced user-centric technology. We've seen a 7% rise in orders for this model in the second quarter of 2024 compared to the first. Meanwhile, our 'Elexion X' model, which has been in production since 2023, continued to hold a steady market share with a slight increase of 2% in orders during the same period.

## Year-over-Year Production Capacity Comparison

In 2022, Elexion Automotive had a significant leap in production capacity with the opening of our second manufacturing facility. This resulted in a 25% increase in our overall production capacity. However, it's important to note that production capacity doesn't directly correlate with sales, as market demand varies.

## Impacts on Supply Chain and Procurement

Enhancements in our supply chain processes in 2024 have led to a 10% reduction in lead times for key components. The Procurement Department has also successfully negotiated contracts with new suppliers, diversifying our supply base and mitigating risk. These improvements have not directly impacted our production capacity, but have strengthened our overall operational efficiency.

## Conclusion

Our production capacity has significantly evolved from 2023 to 2024, with notable changes in the manufacturing of specific vehicle models. This trend impacts our supply chain and procurement processes. Recognizing these changes will enable us to better prepare for future challenges and opportunities. We recommend increased collaboration between our manufacturing, supply chain, and procurement teams to maximize efficiency and profitability.
