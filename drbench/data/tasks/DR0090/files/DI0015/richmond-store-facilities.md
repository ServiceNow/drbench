# Q4 2025 Richmond Store Facilities Report

## Introduction

The following report provides an comprehensive overview of our Richmond Store's facilities as of Q4 2025. It includes a detailed inventory of breakroom amenities, an examination of equipment maintenance schedules, and a review of facility usage patterns. The objective of this report is to evaluate the effectiveness of our current resources and ascertain potential areas for improvement.

## Inventory of Breakroom Amenities

As of Q4 2025, Lee's Market has invested significantly in the comfort and convenience of our employees. The break room is equipped with a variety of amenities including a refrigerator, microwave, toaster, and high-quality dishwasher. Moreover, the company has also provided a water dispenser and vending machines, offering a wide range of snacks and drinks to cater to the diverse dietary preferences of our staff.

## Equipment Maintenance Schedules

Our equipment maintenance is scheduled every quarter to ensure the smooth operation of all on-site facilities. The last maintenance check was conducted in Q3 2025, where all the appliances in the breakroom were thoroughly serviced. The next scheduled maintenance is planned for Q1 2026, where we aim to address any wear and tear caused by daily usage.

## Facility Usage Patterns

Our internal survey conducted in Q3 2025 indicates that the break room is most frequently used during the 12-2 pm lunch window, with usage peaking on Wednesdays. Interestingly, usage is also slightly elevated during the late afternoon, possibly due to employees seeking a brief respite before concluding their workday. The least used hours are between 9-11 am and 2-4 pm.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, our Richmond store's facilities are adequately equipped and well-maintained. It is paramount that we continue to monitor usage patterns and adjust provisions as necessary to ensure a comfortable and productive environment for all employees. Further consideration may be given to expanding or renovating the breakroom based on feedback and usage data. All suggestions for improvement are welcome and will be evaluated thoroughly.
