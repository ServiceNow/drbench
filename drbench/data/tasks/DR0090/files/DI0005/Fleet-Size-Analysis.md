# Lee's Market Fleet Size Analysis - Q4 2025

## Introduction

This document outlines a comprehensive analysis of Lee's Market's truck fleet size as of the fourth quarter of 2025. It provides detailed insights into our fleet's current situation and related areas that have a direct impact on our logistics and supply chain. The analysis will assist in further decision-making and operational planning.

## Fleet Utilization and Efficiency Metrics

In Q3 2025, our total fleet utilization was approximately 76%, which is a 3% increase from Q2 2025. The fuel efficiency for the fleet improved by 2% compared to the previous quarter. These improvements are largely due to the implementation of advanced telematics systems and efficient route planning.

## Logistics Network and Distribution Centers

As of Q4 2025, Lee's Market operates 12 distribution centers across Canada. The average daily outbound volume from these centers is around 1,000 packages per center. We are currently considering the expansion of our logistics network to further support our retail operations.

## Current Truck Fleet Size Breakdown

Our fleet is composed of a variety of vehicle types to cater to specific transportation needs. Around 60% of our vehicles are medium-sized trucks, 30% are large freight trucks, and the remaining 10% are smaller delivery vans. All our vehicles have been upgraded with modern GPS systems.

## Conclusion

The report provides a detailed view of our current fleet size and its utilization efficiency. It also gives a glimpse into our distribution network. Based on these insights, we can strategize our future logistics planning and potentially consider fleet expansion or contraction if necessary.
