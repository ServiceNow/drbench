# Q2 2025 Marketing Expenditure Analysis

## Introduction

This document provides an in-depth analysis of Lee's Market's second quarter marketing expenditures in 2025. A specific focus is placed on the cost associated with physical advertisements for our Japanese product line within the Canadian market. The purpose of this analysis is to evaluate the cost-effectiveness of our advertising strategies and make informed decisions for future marketing plans.

## Overall Q2 2025 Marketing Expenditures

In the second quarter of 2025, Lee's Market allocated significant resources to our marketing initiatives. We increased our overall marketing budget by 15% compared to the previous quarter, focusing on various promotional campaigns. These campaigns were designed to boost customer engagement and increase brand awareness.

## Expenditure on Physical Advertisements for Japanese Products

The physical advertisements for Japanese products were well received by our customers. They were placed strategically in high traffic areas of our stores. Our marketing team worked diligently to create appealing and culturally sensitive designs for these advertisements.

## Comparison of Physical and Digital Advertising Costs

In Q2 2025, we observed a shift in our advertising strategy, with a growing focus on digital platforms. Our data revealed that digital advertising for our products, on average, reached a wider audience. However, the cost-per-impression was higher for physical advertisements.

## Conclusion

The analysis of our Q2 2025 marketing expenditures reveals important insights into our advertising strategies for Japanese products. It's evident that a significant portion of our budget was allocated towards physical advertisements. As we move forward, it is imperative to assess the impact of these advertisements on sales and customer reach. This will enable us to optimize our marketing budget and explore other potentially cost-effective advertising channels.
