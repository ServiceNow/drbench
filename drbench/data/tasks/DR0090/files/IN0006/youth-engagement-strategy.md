# Elevating Online Sales Through Youth Engagement

## Introduction

As Lee's Market continues to evolve in the competitive retail landscape, we need to further capitalize on the potential of our online sales by focusing on a younger demographic. This document outlines the strategies we can employ to target consumers aged 18-35, and differentiate ourselves from our competitors. Our goal is to boost our online sales by 20% by Q1 2026, while also establishing a stronger brand presence among this age group.

## Understanding the Young Consumer Market

Our digital campaigns centered around cultural celebrations, such as Lunar New Year and Mid-Autumn Festival, have proven to be significantly impactful in 2025. We witnessed an overall sales surge of approximately 9% compared to the previous year. It's noteworthy that 40% of this growth, equating to 3.6 percentage points, is a direct result of our culturally themed initiatives. This performance indicates a strong correlation between our digital engagement metrics and actual sales, providing us with a valuable roadmap for future budget allocation and strategy planning.

## Leveraging Digital Marketing for Youth Appeal

In Q2 of 2020, we launched a new social media campaign targeting the 36-50 age group. We found that this demographic responded positively to video content, particularly tutorials and product demonstrations. As a result, our Instagram followers increased by 12%.

## Competitor Analysis in the Canadian Retail Market

Our main competitors have a solid presence in the Ontario region. In 2019, their combined market share was 60%, with an average customer retention rate of 75%. Most of their sales come from in-store purchases rather than online sales.

## Conclusion

By understanding the young consumer market, leveraging the power of digital marketing, and evaluating our competitors' strategies, we can position Lee's Market for future success in the online sales arena. It is imperative that we align our efforts and resources to meet the expectations of this target demographic. I recommend we immediately commence the proposed actions to achieve our sales goal by Q1 2026. It's an ambitious journey, but one that holds great promise for our growth and sustainability.
