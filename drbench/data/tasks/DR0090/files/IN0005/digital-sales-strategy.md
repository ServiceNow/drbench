# Digital Sales Strategy for Younger Demographics

## Introduction

As Lee's Market seeks to boost online sales and ensure its competitive position in the retail industry, it's crucial to develop a comprehensive strategy to engage younger consumers. This document outlines our plan to increase online sales by 20% by targeting consumers aged 18-35, differentiating Lee's Market from dominant players in the Canadian retail market. The strategy will be implemented over the next four years, with the goal of achieving the outlined objectives by Q1 2026.

## Competitive Landscape of Canadian Retail Market

The Canadian retail market is highly competitive with a range of players from multinational corporations to local businesses. In 2020, the grocery sector alone saw a 5% increase in competition. Retailers are increasingly focusing on omnichannel experiences to meet consumer demands.

## Innovative Digital Marketing Strategies

In the rapidly evolving retail landscape, innovative digital marketing strategies are paramount. In 2020, we saw a 30% increase in our mobile app usage due to targeted push notifications and personalized content. Additionally, our email marketing campaigns have maintained a steady open rate of 20%.

## Understanding the 18-35 Consumer Segment

In the year 2025, our culturally specific campaigns, including the Lunar New Year and Mid-Autumn Festival promotions, significantly resonated with the 18-35 age demographic. These campaigns garnered approximately 1.5 million views across all our digital platforms within the first three quarters of the year. Furthermore, these initiatives prompted roughly 12,000 shares, predominantly on platforms popular among this demographic like Instagram and TikTok. The click-through rate for these campaigns was 4%, indicating a strong response to our efforts of integrating cultural authenticity in our marketing strategies.

## Conclusion

This comprehensive plan provides a roadmap to increase online sales by targeting younger consumers and differentiating ourselves in the Canadian retail market. By understanding the 18-35 consumer segment, analyzing the competitive landscape, and implementing innovative digital marketing strategies, we can achieve our goal. The next steps involve refining the proposed strategies based on feedback and preparing for the implementation phase. The Sales and Marketing team will require cross-functional collaboration and support to make this vision a reality.
