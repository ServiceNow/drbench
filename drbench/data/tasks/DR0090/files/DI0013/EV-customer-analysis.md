# EV Customer Analysis: Richmond Store 2025

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing efforts to understand and adapt to our customers' evolving transportation habits, this report offers an analysis of customers who visited our Richmond, BC store in 2025 using electric vehicles (EVs). The data will provide insights into the environmental consciousness of our customer base and inform future decision-making related to store infrastructure and marketing strategies. This understanding is particularly vital as we strive to align our business operations with sustainable practices.

## Overview of EV Adoption Trends in Richmond, BC

Richmond, BC has a rapidly growing population of electric vehicle (EV) users. In 2020, there was a 15% increase in EV registrations compared to the previous year. This growth trend aligns with the broader Canadian push towards sustainable transportation solutions.

## Customer Transportation Preferences at Lee's Market

Our customer surveys indicate a strong preference for convenient, eco-friendly transportation options. In 2024, 45% of customers reported using energy-efficient vehicles, while another 30% preferred public transit. These trends underscore the importance of our ongoing efforts to support sustainable transportation initiatives.

## Impact of EV Usage on Store Traffic Patterns

Increased EV usage has implications for our store traffic patterns. We've observed longer dwell times from EV users due to the availability of charging stations in our parking lot. However, the overall impact on traffic congestion and parking availability needs further study.

## Conclusion

Understanding our customers' shift towards more sustainable modes of transportation, particularly EVs, provides valuable insights for strategic planning. The data suggests a growing segment of our customer base is environmentally conscious, which should be considered in our marketing and operations. Future initiatives could include the provision of EV charging stations or green marketing campaigns. As we move forward, it will be crucial to continuously monitor these trends and adapt accordingly.
