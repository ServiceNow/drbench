# Lee's Market Parking Lot Analysis Q4 2025

## Introduction

As part of our ongoing efforts to optimize customer experience and store efficiency, this report presents a detailed analysis of the parking lot area of Lee's Market store in Vancouver. The data, specific to Q4 2025, will assist in making informed decisions about parking infrastructure and potential expansions. The report also focuses on correlating parking lot size to store traffic and assessing potential environmental impacts.

## Correlation Between Parking Lot Size and Store Traffic

In 2024, a study in our Richmond location showed a 15% increase in store traffic with a 10% increase in parking lot size. It was observed that larger parking lots attracted more customers, especially during peak shopping hours. However, the correlation is not linear and tends to plateau after reaching a certain size.

## Environmental Impact of Parking Lot Expansion

The Environmental Department conducted an analysis in 2023 of the potential impacts of expanding our parking lots. The report highlighted an expected 20% increase in rainwater runoff and a potential increase in heat island effect. Mitigation measures such as permeable asphalt and tree plantings were suggested.

## Q4 2025 Parking Lot Size Analysis

A comprehensive analysis of parking lot sizes across all Lee's Market stores was conducted in Q4 2025. The study aimed to understand the utilization rates of our parking facilities, with a focus on optimizing space for customer convenience and operational efficiency. Initial results have shown a wide variation in utilization rates across different locations.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, our analysis reveals the parking lot area does have significant effects on store traffic. Any expansion plans should consider potential environmental impacts, ensuring we align with corporate sustainability goals. Based on our findings, we recommend a thorough study before any parking lot expansion to guarantee optimal store performance and customer satisfaction.
